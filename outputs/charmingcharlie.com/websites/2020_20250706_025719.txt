of payment. for example, you can redeem a gift card and pay for the remaining balance with your credit card. i would like to provide feedback on the new website, how do i contact you?we’d love to hear from you! please fill out our contact us form to share your feedback. account how do i reset my account password?reset your password here. how do i update my email address and other account details?after logging into your account online, you will be able to update your email address, mailing address and any payment information that you have saved. log-in to your account. orders when will my order arrive?please check your order confirmation email for tracking information. for orders placed prior to january 28, 2021, please contact:  customerservice@charmingcharlie.com for orders placed after january 28th, 2021, please contact:  orders@charmingcharlie.com can i modify or cancel my order?unfortunately, because we begin processing your order immediately once placed, we are unable to modify or cancel it in our system. however, you can return your purchase for up to 30 days after the ship date. i am having issues placing an order online, who should i contact?please contact customer service at orders@charmingcharlie.com or by phone at 855-655-8972. responses will be provided within 24-48 hours of receipt. can i order online and pickup in store?unfortunately, we do not offer order online and pickup in store at this time. can i get a price adjustment on my order if some of the items are now on sale? unfortunately, we do not offer price adjustments at this time. shipping what are your shipping costs?please find all of our shipping rates and details here. how do i qualify for free shipping?we offer everyday free economy shipping on all orders over $49, excluding taxes and after coupons or promotions. where do you ship?at this time we only ship throughout the continental u.s., excluding hawaii, alaska, guam and puerto rico. do you ship internationally?we do not offer international shipping at this time. when will my order ship?note that orders generally process within 2 business days, not including holidays and weekends.  you will receive a shipment confirmation email with tracking information once the order has been processed.  please allow for up to 24 hours after the shipment confirmation email has been sent to be able to track the package. do you ship to po boxes?we only provide economy shipping to po boxes. expedited orders require a physical address. do you ship to military addresses?at this time, we do not ship to apo/fpo/dpo military addresses. returns & exchanges what is your return policy for online orders?if you change your mind, we offer returns online. within 30 days, with tags attached, receive a full refund in the original form of payment. sale items are final sale. charming charlie reserves the right to deny the return of any product. please see our full return and exchange policy for more information. what is your exchange policy for online orders?within 30 days, with tags attached, simply return your item by mail and re-purchase your new item. charming charlie.com is unable to offer exchanges. if you received an item as a gift, you will need a receipt or gift receipt to process your exchange. sale items are final sale. charming charlie reserves the right to deny the return of any product. please see our full return and exchange policy for more information. how do i track if you have received my return?we recommend that you keep a copy of the return shipment tracking information for future reference. if you have further questions about your return please contact us: for orders placed prior to january 28, 2021, please contact:  customerservice@charmingcharlie.com for orders placed after january
