however, many generic pre-designed folders tailored for specific industries or situations. these come with standard art already printed, but you may also add elements of your choice such as your company name or logo. wallet style naturally, this type of folder functions similarly to a wallet, with a single pocket and horizontal orientation. it works well for holding small business-related documents such as checks and invoices. four-pocket style this folder contains four pockets in a "tiered" arrangement. this is useful when you have groups of documents that it's important to keep separated, or when you have both large and small documents that you want to display in an attractive way. top loading top loading document folders function more like an envelope than a conventional folder in that they don't "open" like a book. this type of folder is useful for more crucial or personal documents because they invoke a feeling of security and privacy. coatings document folders may have aqueous, lamination, or ultraviolet (uv) coating. imprint methods your printing options include offset pms printing (which utilized pre-mixed ink), four-color process (which uses cmyk ink to create a veritable spectrum of color during printing), foil stamping (where reflective metallic or non-metallic foil is applied to your folder) or embossing (which physically raises the stock for a
