remain in good condition, you can change their appearance by replacing the doors and cabinet hardware. let’s look at how easily you can update your kitchen’s appearance. cabinet doors, drawers, and glass consider revamping your kitchen cabinets by replacing their existing doors with antique brass grille doors or shaker doors. new cabinets can cost as much as $10,000, but you can strip the paint or varnish off your existing cabinets and replace only the doors for a less-costly update. you re-stain them or paint them your desired color. depending on the door style, doors can cost less than $50 each. bathroom and kitchen cabinet hardware sometimes, a really tight budget can create a challenge, but try updating just the cabinet locks, latches, hinges, and shelf hardware. these come in stainless steel, brass, and nickel-plated brass. ranging roughly from $5 to $10 each for hinges, this option offers an updated look without breaking the bank. shop rockler rockler has everything you
