name suggests, this type of chuck has four jaws, and these jaws can be adjusted in and out to hold a workpiece, either by contracting around a turned tenon or expanding into a recess you turn in the workpiece. most chucks also include a large screw insert for mounting blanks for shaping and turning the tenon or recess. four-jaw chucks are an investment, so you want to choose wisely. here are a
