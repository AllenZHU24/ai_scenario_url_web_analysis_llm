file that is placed on the hard drive of your computer when you visit a web site. a session cookie expires immediately when you end your session (i.e., close your browser). a persistent cookie stores information on the hard drive so when you end your session and return to the same web site at a later date the cookie information is still available. we use cookies to improve the quality of our service when you visit our web site and other web sites of interest to you. we also use cookies to remind us of who you are, tailor our products, services and advertising to suit the personal
