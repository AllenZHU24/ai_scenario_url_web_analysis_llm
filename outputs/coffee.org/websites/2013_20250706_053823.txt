items 1-15 of 29 show 15 30 60 150 per page page: 1 2 types of coffee pods coffee pods are similar to tea bags in that they are made from a filter type material filled with coffee and sealed around the edges to keep the coffee inside. there are many different types of coffee pods that correspond to the different coffee machines they are used in as well as the different types of coffee that can be purchased in coffee pods. miss ellie's coffee pods are the tastiest and smoothest pods on the market today. ellie has selected a nice collection of exquisite coffee roasts and taste, including french roast, sumatra, breakfast blend, french vanilla, hazelnut coffee, donut shop, espresso blend, colombian, and miss ellie's decaf colombian for her coffee pods. in addition to miss ellie's coffee, we have wolfgang puck pods, java one pods, city steam pods, community coffee pods, and melitta pods . melitta, one of the top names in gourmet coffee offers coffee pods filled with some of the best coffees available. (note) melitta pods only work in the melitta single serve brewer the most common coffee pod is the one used in single serve coffee machines. however, it is important to note that not all coffee pods are interchangeable. the senseo and melitta both use specialized coffee pods that are made specifically for their machine. the black and decker single serve coffee maker can use the same pods as the senseo machine.  coffee pods for use in single serve machines are available from coffee.org. in addition to coffee pods we carry tea pods, hot chocolate pods and other hot beverage pods. shop by
