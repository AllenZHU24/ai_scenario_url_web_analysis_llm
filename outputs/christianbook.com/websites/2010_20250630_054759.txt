gladly ship a replacement and reimburse you for your return postage. any item you are not satisfied with (not as expected, arrived too late, wrong size, etc.), if returned within 30 days of receipt and received in sellable condition, will be issued an exchange or refund of merchandise cost only. imprinted items may not be returned for replacement or refund, unless imprint or item is defective. computer software, videos, cds, cassettes, dvds, or other media may only be returned for refunds if unopened. if opened, they may be returned only for replacement of the same item. any return that does not comply with the above policy will be subject to a significant restocking fee, at our discretion. international customers: please contact us before returning any product. ship your return to:
