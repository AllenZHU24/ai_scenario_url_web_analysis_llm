email us (customer.service@christianbook.com) so that we can fix this for you! we will gladly ship a replacement and assist you with your return. personalized items may not be returned for replacement or refund, unless the personalization or item is defective. dvds, cds, computer software, or other media may be returned for refunds only if unopened. if opened, they may be returned only for
