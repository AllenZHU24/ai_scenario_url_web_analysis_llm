may request document measurements with your frame order. we ask for: actual paper size - the overall outside dimensions of the entire document printed area size - the dimensions from where the printed text or image begins to where it ends on the page. for example, this would be from the top of the highest lettering to the bottom of the lowest image on the document, usually a seal or signature.  we also need the measurements of the widest printed area, from left to right. shop frames & gifts our company need help? partners college & university high school & prep associations professional
