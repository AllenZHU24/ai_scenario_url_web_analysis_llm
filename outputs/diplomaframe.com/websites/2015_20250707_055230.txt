sales tax we collect sales tax for all states in the continental u.s. that have a state sales tax. states require that sales tax be collected based on where the product is being shipped to. if we ship to your home address, this is the basis for the sales tax. if you ship it as a gift to someone in another state or location, we must collect tax based on where the product will actually be delivered. sales tax is charged on merchandise, and may be charged on shipping and handling based upon individual state laws. for consumers who order items online and live in states that charge a sales tax, you are technically required to report your purchase to your state and pay the sales tax directly to the state agency. we collect and remit this sales tax as a courtesy to relieve our customers of this obligation. sales tax rates and laws are constantly changing, and we do our best to stay abreast of these changes. if you believe we've made an error in sales tax on any of your purchases, please
