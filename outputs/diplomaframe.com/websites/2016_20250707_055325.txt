quality of the materials used in your frame affect both its appearance and the longevity of your valuable document. for this reason, all of our frames feature acid-free matting and backing materials. our premium frames are showcased with the "museum quality" seal which is your assurance that the "conservation quality" matting, backing and mounting materials included with your frame are lignin-free and meet stringent library of congress standards for document preservation. all of our custom-cut mats have a pure white bevel that will not discolor over time. there is no need for you to send us your diploma. for ease of use, we design our diploma frames with a removable back so that you can easily insert your own document. we provide step-by-step instructions as well as archival-quality mounting materials. this allows you to safely insert your document while assuring ease of removal should the need arise in the future. certified picture framer on staff our framing materials are
