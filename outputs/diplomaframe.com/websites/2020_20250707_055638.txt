customer service > shipping information a note to our customers thank you for shopping our site and considering church hill classics for your custom framing needs! we’re experiencing an unprecedented surge in demand for our frames, which are all made-to-order by our skilled team of artisans. due to the national rise in covid-19 cases, connecticut has reverted back to phase ii restrictions, which is resulting in longer-than-normal lead times. as a thank-you for your support of our small business, please enjoy free shipping on us! shipping information and timing you can track the status of your order with our order lookup tool. our standard shipping method is ups ground. whenever possible, we recommend shipping to a business address. ups will not deliver to po boxes; therefore, we’ll need a street address for delivery purposes. for residential deliveries, we use the ups home delivery system, which offers the convenience of saturday deliveries. please note, orders over $750 will require a signature at delivery. each item we ship is fully insured, and our frames are shipped in specially designed smartboxes that have been tested and approved by both ups and fedex. in the unlikely event of damage in transit, we'll resolve the issue and ship a replacement promptly. please call us at (800) 477-9005 to report transit damage and request a timely pickup and product replacement. standard
