warehouse processing times is different for each item in your order. most in-stock orders are shipped within 6-10 business days (m-f) following order placement unless otherwise stated in the products shipping description page, and in your order confirmation email next to each item purchased. since each purchased item requires a specific warehouse processing time certain products may delay shipment of your entire order. estimated shipping times displayed at checkout do not include processing time. orders received after 2 pm est will begin processing the next business day. saturday and sunday orders will begin processing on the following monday, excluding federal holidays. domestic shipping (u.s.) we offer flat rate shopping only. once your order leaves our warehouse, standard domestic shipping takes 3-7 business days on average for delivery on domestic orders to the contiguous u.s. lightweight packages (under 1lb) are shipped via usps first class mail. heavier packages (over 1lb) are shipped via ups surepost. surepost packages are typically transferred from ups to usps for final mile delivery. orders shipping to hawaii, alaska (7-14 days) and to military base apo/fpo (up to 4 weeks) will take additional time. please note, a small number of items in our store ship directly from the vendor regardless of any other items in the order. items that ship directly from a supplier are noted in the shipping product descriptions, and in your confirmation email. additional shipping rates may apply to these items. international shipping (canada, u.k. & australia) barstool sports now offers storewide shipping to canada, u.k. & australia. we are pleased to offer fast & reliable flat rate shipping so you can be assured it will arrive in time. see the chart below for more information: once your order leaves our warehouse, fedex international shipping typically can range anywhere from 8-14 business days. orders are fully trackable the entire way. you may have a secondary tracking number in the shipping details to follow for final delivery. you can view this tracking number by selecting "view more tracking." please allow up to 72 hours for the tracking to initially activate on all international shipments. duties & fees customs duties and taxes are now automatically calculated and collected at time of checkout with our store. no additional duties will be required at time of delivery. although duties are not always incurred, assessment of the duties and taxes will be based on the value of the total goods purchased. these charges for customs clearance must be paid by the recipient at time of checkout; barstool sports has no control over these charges. duties & fees are non refundable. see the chart below for duty-free & tax limits in each country: for example, in canada you can order up to $150 cad before duties are charged and $40 before gst is added but if you go over that amount then duties are applied to the entire order. rest of world limited assortment of made-to-order items available for worldwide shipping. items eligible for worldwide shipping are made to order and noted on each products page. click here to view the full international collection. expedited shipping barstool sports store does not currently offer any expedited shipping options. while we aim to ship your order to you as quickly as possible, please note that we cannot accommodate any requests for expedited shipping. standard processing times prior to shipment on all orders is 6-10 business days. made to order & manufacturer direct a small number of items in our store are printed or made when you order them. made to order items are always indicated in the products shipping description, and in your confirmation email. if you place an order with regular items and made to order items, your order may be shipped to you in separate shipments at different times. items printed on demand are final sale and not eligible for return or exchange. pre-sale items occasionally we will accept pre-sale and back-orders on certain items and during special events. pre-sale items are clearly noted by the yellow pre-sale tag in the products image. if you have selected an item that is on pre-sale or back-ordered, the estimated ship date is visible in the product description on our website. if your order contains a mix of in-stock and pre-sale items, the entire order will not ship until all of the pre-sale items have arrived at our warehouse, unless otherwise stated in the product description. if you need something more immediately, please
