following order placement unless otherwise stated in the products shipping description, and in your confirmation email. estimated shipping times displayed at checkout do not include processing time. our warehouse is based in the state of ohio, united states and ships monday through friday. orders received after 2 pm est will begin processing the next business day. saturday and sunday orders will begin processing on the following monday, excluding federal holidays. domestic shipping (u.s.) we offer flat rate or free standard shipping for all orders over $75. once your order leaves our warehouse, standard domestic upsmi shipping takes 5-8 business days on average for delivery on domestic orders to the contiguous u.s. packages are always transferred from ups to usps for final mile delivery. upsmi tracking numbers are searchable on the usps tracking website to see final tracking details. usps tracking is only activated once a package is received. after receiving the package usps delivers within 1-3 business days on average. orders shipping to hawaii, alaska (7-14 days) and to military base apo/fpo (up to 4 weeks) will take additional time. please note, a small number of items in our store ship directly from the vendor regardless of any other items in the order. items that ship directly from a supplier are noted in the shipping product descriptions, and in your confirmation email. international shipping (canada) we offer flat rate or free standard shipping for all orders over $100 to canada. once your order leaves our warehouse, upsmi international shipping typically can range anywhere from 10-14 business days. once packages clear customs they are transferred to local delivery service carriers for final mile delivery. secondary tracking numbers can be found in the shipment details section within your ups tracking link under "alternate tracking number(s)." copy this number to the website of your local delivery service provider such as canada post. this number is only activated once a package clears customs. due to changing customs import policies there may be some delays with shipments which are out of our control. it is possible that the delivery of your order could be delayed an additional 5-10 business days due to long customs processing / inspections / clearance times. customs duties and taxes are not included in the price of the products or shipping with our store. although duties are not all always incurred, assessment of the duties and taxes will be based on the retail value of the total goods imported. any additional charges for customs clearance must be paid by the recipient; barstool sports has no control over these charges, nor can barstool sports predict what they may be. packages greater than 4lbs may ship with expedited service via ups standard, additional brokerage fees may apply with this ship method. those fees are the responsibility of the recipient. expedited shipping barstool sports store does not currently offer any expedited shipping options. while we aim to ship your order to you as quickly as possible, please note that we cannot accommodate any requests for expedited shipping. standard processing times prior to shipment on all orders is 3-6 business days. made to order & manufacturer direct a small number of items in our store are printed or made when you order them. made to order items are always indicated in the products shipping description, and in your confirmation email. if you place an order with regular items and made to order items, your order may be shipped to you in separate shipments at different times. items printed on demand are final sale and not eligible for return or exchange. pre-sale items occasionally we will accept pre-sale and back-orders on certain items and during special events. pre-sale items are clearly noted by the yellow pre-sale tag in the products image. if you have selected an item that is on pre-sale or back-ordered, the estimated ship date is visible in the product description on our website. if your order contains a mix of in-stock and pre-sale items, the entire order will not ship until all of the pre-sale items have arrived at our warehouse, unless otherwise stated in the product description. if you need something more immediately, please drop us a line. we've got your back worry free returns great customer service let us send
