(m-f) following order placement unless otherwise stated in the products shipping description page, and in your order confirmation email next to each item purchased. since each purchased item requires a specific warehouse processing time certain products may delay shipment of your entire order. estimated shipping times displayed at checkout do not include processing time. orders received after 2 pm est will begin processing the next business day. saturday and sunday orders will begin processing on the following monday, excluding federal holidays. domestic shipping (u.s.) we offer flat rate shopping only. once your order leaves our warehouse, standard domestic shipping takes 3-7 business days on average for delivery on domestic orders to the contiguous u.s. lightweight packages (under 1lb) are shipped via usps first class mail. heavier packages (over 1lb) are shipped via ups surepost. surepost packages are typically transferred from ups to usps for final mile delivery. orders shipping to hawaii, alaska (7-14 days) and to military base apo/fpo (up to 4 weeks) will take additional time. please note, a small number of items in our store ship directly from the vendor regardless of any other items in the order. items that ship directly from a supplier are noted in the shipping product descriptions, and in your confirmation email. additional shipping rates may apply to these items. international shipping (canada, u.k. & australia) barstool sports now offers storewide shipping to canada, u.k. & australia. we are pleased to offer fast & reliable flat rate shipping so you can be assured it will arrive in time. see the chart below for more information: once your order leaves our warehouse, fedex international shipping typically can range anywhere from 8-14 business days. orders are fully trackable the entire way. you may have a secondary tracking number in the shipping details to follow for final delivery. you can view this tracking number by selecting "view more tracking." please allow up to 72 hours for the tracking to initially activate on all international shipments. duties & fees customs duties and taxes are now automatically calculated and collected at time of checkout with our store. no additional duties will be required at time of delivery. although duties are not always incurred, assessment of the duties and taxes will be based on the value of the total goods purchased. these charges for customs clearance must be paid by the recipient at time of checkout; barstool sports has no control over these charges. duties & fees are non refundable. see the chart below for duty-free & tax limits in each country: for example, in canada you can order up to $150 cad before duties are charged and $40 before gst is added but if you go over that amount then duties are applied to the entire order. rest of world limited assortment of made-to-order items available for worldwide shipping. items eligible for worldwide shipping are made to
