completed. q: can i cancel my order? a: our first priority is to ensure that your items ship in a timely manner. once an order is placed and all of the billing and shipping information has been cancelled - your order has been canceled and you have not been charged. q: when will i receive my order? a: orders usually take 1-2 business days, monday-friday excluding holidays, to process before
