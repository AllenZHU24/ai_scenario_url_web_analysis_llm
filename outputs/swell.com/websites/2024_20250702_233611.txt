shipping does not apply to alaska, puerto rico and hawaiian orders. what is s’well’s international shipping policy? international shipping is provided through ups. to start an international order, placed on swell.com are in usd. you may incur conversion fees from your bank. rates will vary based on the individual bank and typically range from 1% to 3% of the total order. where does s’well ship
