philanthropist. kimora began her career as a fashion model at the age of 13, and was soon on an exclusive contract with the house of chanel, in addition to being on the covers and pages of international fashion magazines such as vogue, elle, and harper’s bazaar. in 1999, kimora launched the global lifestyle brand baby phat. as president and founder of phat fashions – which included baby
