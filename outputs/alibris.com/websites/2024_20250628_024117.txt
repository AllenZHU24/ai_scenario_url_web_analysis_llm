their expanding mind. shop by age: kids 0-2 years 3-5 years 6-8 years 9-12 years teens & young adult faqs what are the most popular kids books? ▾ there is no singular popular children's book, because it all depends on their age group. for example, the very hungry caterpillar is a popular kids book for kids ages 0-2. picture book classics like where the wild things are, goodnight moon, the story of ferdinand, and the snowy day captivate children aged 0-7. meanwhile, the harry potter books are most popular among those ages 9-12, young adults, and adults alike. what are some of the most popular young adult books? ▾ a few of the most popular young adult and teen books include the hunger games, harry potter, anne of green gables, and many more. we offer a wide selection of young adult books to help your teen find the best book based on their reading skills and interests. from timeless classics, including literary fiction like the catcher in the rye, to popular series like the maze runner, there's something that will engage every teen, no matter what they like to read. what is the age range for ya books? ▾ young adult novels and teen books, also known as "ya", are most appropriate for kids 12 and older because they're written for that age group's reading level. they often feature teenage protagonists going through a similar stage of life, making them more engaging to older kids. young adult literature is usually plot-driven, making it popular reading for many adults too. how to choose age-appropriate books? ▾ alibris makes it easy to choose age-appropriate kids books because we sort our children's books by age range, allowing you to find something for every reading level and age group. however, as your children get older and become better readers, it might be challenging to find a book based on their needs. to find age-appropriate kids books, always consider your child's reading level. for more advanced readers, look for books above their age. letting a child choose
