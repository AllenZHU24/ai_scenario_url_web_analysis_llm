is described on our web site. if you are not satisfied that your item is as described, return your purchase within 60 days of its original shipment date for a refund. if you return an item more than 60 days after its shipment date, the item will be discarded and you will not be eligible to receive a refund. how to submit returns returning a purchase from alibris is easy. if you need to return an item, simply follow these instructions: step 1 visit the order history in your account and click the order number link for the item you will be returning.   step 2 from the order detail page, click the return this item link. step 3 complete the return form in detail and click the submit button. step 4 print the authorization page* and return label. please rewrap the item in its original packaging, along with the authorization page*, and ship it using the mailing label provided. once we have received and processed the returned item, alibris will notify you of your refund via e-mail. you can expect a refund in the same form of payment originally used for your purchase, two to three weeks after we receive your return. *the return authorization page must be included with your return. items returned without the return authorization page will be discarded without refund. for items valued over $100, the use of delivery confirmation or other tracking is strongly recommended.
