us your mailing address as it appears on each catalog duplicate and indicate which is correct. mail to: barrie pace — dept. 02, p.o. box 4600, st. cloud, mn 56398. we'll update our information and send you a $10 gift card to say thanks for your trouble.about correspondence from barrie pacewe may want to email you a list of our end-of-season specials, latest styles, newest services or other general information. if you do not wish to receive such emails, please let us know. if you do not wish to receive our barrie pace catalog any longer, please email us, call us, or write us.
