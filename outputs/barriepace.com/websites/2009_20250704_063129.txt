authorization and verification have been obtained. typically, we ship in-stock merchandise the next business day. credit card orders are charged when the merchandise has shipped. orders shipped via standard delivery usually arrive in 3 to 8 business days (excluding holidays).we will determine the most efficient shipping carrier for your order. the carriers we may use are: usps or ups ground.we ship to hawaii and alaska via usps priority mail and ups ground. we ship to apo/fpo addresses via usps priority mail only. please allow for additional delivery time if shipping to these locations.express shippingwe offer four express shipping options:priority overnight (additional $22): orders need to be submitted by 11am et to be delivered the next business day morning.standard overnight (additional $17): orders need to be submitted by 11am et to be delivered the next business day afternoon.second day air (additional $12): orders need to be submitted by 11am et to be delivered in two business days.saturday delivery (additional $27): orders need to be submitted by 11am et on friday to qualify.orders placed with express shipping monday through friday (excluding holidays) before 11am et will ship same day, pending credit card authorization and verification. orders placed with express shipping monday through friday after 11am et, or on weekends and holidays, will ship the next business day. express shipping is not available for alaska, hawaii, apo or fpo addressesmerchandise subtotalstandardpriority overnight ($22)standard overnight ($17)second day air
