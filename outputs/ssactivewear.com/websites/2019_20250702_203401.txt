time, we’ll ship your apparel the same business day. we’ll ship orders placed after the cut-off time the next business day. same-day shipping cut-off times: enter your zip code in the shipping map search bar to find your cut-off time for same-day orders. if you need an express shipment, ask us. note: s&s activewear isn’t responsible for any delay in the delivery of the shipment of goods, or for freight: we offer free freight on orders over $200 shipped via ground with our preferred carrier within the continental united states. this excludes the all-star chair (ft002), stadium chair (sc2 seat and wsc2 seat), and oversized travel bag styles (92060aodm, 92869odm and 92356odm). free freight doesn’t apply to c.o.d. charges and is only good for orders shipped within the continental u.s.
