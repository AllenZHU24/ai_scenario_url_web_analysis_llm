shipping coverage how fast can we get your order to you? enter zip code: go shipping information for ***zip code*** ***table*** enter your zip code above to receive transit and cut-off times from all our locations. warehouse locations fort worth, tx lockport, il mcdonough, ga olathe, ks reno, nv robbinsville, nj shipping: we’re pleased to offer free shipping to all of our customers for online orders of $200 or more. if you order before the daily cut-off time, we’ll ship your apparel the same business day. we’ll ship orders placed after the cut-off time the next business day. same-day shipping cut-off times: enter your zip code in the shipping map search bar to find your cut-off time for same-day orders. if you need an express shipment, ask us. note: s&s activewear isn’t responsible for any delay in the delivery of the shipment of goods, or for any damages suffered by reason of such a delay. all shipments are f.o.b. from origin ship point. local pick up orders: all s&s will call locations are open for order pickup monday through friday, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. local time. please allow three hours for us to process your order. if you ordered after the cut-off time, it’ll be ready for pickup at 11:00 a.m. the following business day. free freight: we offer free freight on orders over $200 shipped via ground with our preferred carrier within the continental united states. this excludes the all-star chair (ft002), stadium chair (sc2 seat and wsc2 seat), face masks, and oversized travel bag styles (92060aodm). we use cookies on this site to enhance your
