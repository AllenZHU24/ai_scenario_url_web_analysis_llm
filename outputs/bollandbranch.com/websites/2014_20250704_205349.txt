policy boll & branch is pleased to offer complimentary domestic us ground shipping on all orders. us customers may elect to upgrade their order to 1 or 2-day shipping. a shipping & handling fee is applied to all international orders. orders placed for in-stock items usually ship within 24-business hours. orders placed on fridays, holidays or on weekends will be shipped the following business day. please note that 1 and 2-day shipments will not deliver on saturdays, sundays or holidays. if an item you ordered is not in-stock or is on backorder, it will be noted on your email receipt.
