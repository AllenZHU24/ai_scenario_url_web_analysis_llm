policy we make exceptional products and fully stand behind their quality. however, if you are unsatisfied with your purchase for any reason, we will gladly accept returns for any reason within 30 days of the delivery of purchase (delivery is defined as the date the shipment was delivered by the carrier based on the carriers shipment tracking information). gift cards are not eligible for returns.upon receipt of your return, your original form of payment will be refunded. if you elect to use a pre-paid boll & branch return label, the amount of your refund will be reduced by the return shipping and handling cost. you can avoid being charged return shipping and handling by using your own shipping label. however, if you choose to do so please ensure that your shipment is insured for the full amount of the item(s) you are returning.  please allow 1-3 weeks from receipt of your return for the refund to be applied to your credit card. boll & branch is not responsible for lost or undelivered returns that are not shipped using pre-paid boll & branch shipping labels.boll & branch does not accept returns after 30 days.if you have any further questions about our return policy, please do not hesitate to call or email us and we will be glad to help.to exchange your return for another item, please go through our return process for a refund. you may then purchase the
