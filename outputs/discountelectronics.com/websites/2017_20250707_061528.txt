cards pawn shop apple products printers networking speakers tablets telecom iphones news discount electronics offers interest free financing for public schools posted by rick culleton on jul 19th 2017 as the new school year approaches, discount electronics is offering an additional $750,000 in interest-free financing to public schools throughout the us. we want to offer every school district the ability to save money and stay within its it budget. the last two years discountelectronics.com has had to limit the number of purchase order sales made by public schools at the beginning of the school year due to a credit cap. we do not expect that to be a problem for the fall 2017 semester. two semesters ago we began taking custom orders for refurbished pcs. this allowed it departments of public schools to order computers and laptops with the specs and operating system they desired yet still save a significant amount of money over new equipment. the demand was greater than expected and we had not set aside enough funds to carry the purchase orders for all of the schools requesting equipment. this year we believe we have resolved the issue and have freed up enough funding to meet the demand.additionally, we have stocked up on the most popular models of dell computers, laptops and flat panel monitors. our accounting department is prepared to approve new po accounts for public schools quickly, usually the same day. for more information or to place an order contact eugene terrell at eugene@discountelectronics.com or 512-572-1791. computers for schoolspublic school purchase
