december 31 will not be processed until monday, january 4. fedex is also closed on friday, january 1, and will not be making deliveries that day. shipping rates we offer free 2-day shipping on all orders. we also offer free shipping on returns of items purchased on brooksrunning.com. shipping method all orders shipping destinations estimated delivery (processing + shipping) fedex free! u.s. street addresses only 3 business days usps free! u.s. street addresses, p.o. boxes, apo/fpo addresses 7-10 business days orders placed before 10am pacific will be processed the same business day; orders placed after 10am pacific will be processed the following business day. we do not process orders over the weekend. please note: the fedex shipping option is not available to u.s. territories, p.o. boxes, puerto rico, or apo/fpo addresses. estimated delivery times we begin processing orders quickly, so we are unable to make changes after an order is submitted. estimated delivery time is within 3 business days from the date of purchase with fedex shipping and 10 business days for usps shipping. if the item you ordered is not in stock or will be delayed, we will email you with an update on the status of your order. your credit card is only charged when your order ships. if you have any questions about your order, please contact customer service via email, phone or chat and we'll be happy to help. shipping destinations shipping is available to destinations within the u.s.
