functionality is turned off. please turn it on so that you can experience the full capabilities of this site. welcome to our run happy, green-living headquarters stone34 is an earth-friendly sight to see, a workplace that walks the walk of our commitment to running happy and living green. it’s leed platinum certified two times over: for construction and design as well as operations and maintenance. it also meets even higher sustainability standards as the first commercial office building in seattle to complete the city’s “deep green” pilot program. in 2015, during our first full year in the building, we consumed about 80 percent less energy and water than the average office building. some other awesome things about our headquarters: rainwater flows into a super-efficient heating and cooling system that uses a lot less energy than standard hvac systems. rainwater also irrigates plants around the building and flushes our toilets. daylight is a beautiful thing, and we welcome it in with expansive windows that reduce the need for artificial lights. smart sensors automatically dim or turn off highly efficient led lights when they’re not needed. we give our employees many ways to get here greenly. bike storage, timed showers, lockers and changing rooms encourage human-powered commuting. zero-emission cars can recharge in electrical vehicle-charging stations in our garage, which has priority parking spots for fuel-efficient vehicles and employees who carpool. we recycled 97% of all materials during construction, diverting more than 1,160 tons of debris from landfills. reclaimed wood throughout our hq and in our brooks trailhead retail store serve as constant reminders that we’re serious about minimizing our impact on the environment. these resource-saving measures and more place brooks’ headquarters in an elite class of the most highly sustainable buildings in the world. learn more about stone34 in our latest running responsibly report.
