years, we are proud to carry an extensive collection of tumi products. read more! their collections span a number of materials including leather, polycarbonate, and nylon bags. what makes them stands lifelong customers is the fact that they are tireless in improving design features. no bag has to go through quite as much testing as a tumi luggage piece, and it shows. features like tsa approved locking, expandable designs, foldable luggage, and detachable garment bags are just some of the latest features to hit their most recent line, the alpha 2. this just goes to prove what kaehler loves about tumi: although the brand is established, tumi never stops thinking about how they can make a bag better. even when revolutionizing ballistic nylon as a fabric choice, they didn't rest on this.
