gift guides menu gift guides men's gift guide women's gift guide stocking stuffers brief history we would love to tell you our story... but here are the basics. kaehler 1920 is a maker of fine leather goods. all products are made in chicago using leathers from our neighbors, the horween leather company. kaehler was founded in evanston, il in 1920 by locksmith walter gustav kaehler. when faced with an increasingly high demand for fixing locks on travel trunks, kaehler developed a business as a purveyor of fine luggage and leather goods. now in its 4th generation, kaehler 1920 remains a family business and is committed to locally made goods of the highest quality. we offer the best selection of the top brands in the travel world. please visit us to see our products and other great brands in
