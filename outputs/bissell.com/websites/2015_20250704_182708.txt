animals in shelters and rescues through pet adoption, spay neuter programs, microchipping and foster care. read more > partners for pets at bissell, we believe in pet adoption and are committed to helping the animal welfare groups that serve our nation’s homeless pets. through the partners for pets program, participating shelters can raise money three different ways: by earning proceeds from purchases on bissell.com, through the opportunity to win up to $40,000 a year in giveaways and sweepstakes on the bissell pet foundation's facebook page, and through their eligibility to receive one free bissell pet product annually to use in a raffle or auction. read more > petfinder.com foundation bissell continues to support the pet community through a series of grants to the petfinder.com fortunately, lostpetusa.net has the essential tools to help reunite lost and found pets with their families. the free online and mobile service offers a searchable, nationwide registry to connect owners and finders with recently reported lost & found pets, as well as many resources and tools for pet parents to use if their beloved pet ever becomes astray. to learn more, visit www.lostpetusa.net. read more > tour de force the tour de force bike ride was started in 2002 as a memorial to the fallen police officers who gave their lives in the september 11, 2001, attacks.
