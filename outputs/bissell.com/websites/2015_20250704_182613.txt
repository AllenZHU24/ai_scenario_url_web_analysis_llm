leader highlights messes to find homes for pets with new campaign sustainability the bissell commitment to sustainability is an extension of our rich history of innovation and continuous improvement. with the creation of the bissell cross-functional forevergreen team in 2006, we formalized our commitment to sustainable business practices. through this team, we’ve made sustainability an integral part of who we are and what we do. our commitment is defined by three pillars: efficient use of resources in our products and operations responsible use of chemicals, and transparent reporting of product ingredients responsible management of waste throughout a product’s lifecycle we’ve made progress in each of these areas and established goals for continuous improvement. we’re confident that our commitment to sustainability and good corporate citizenship will help us contribute to a cleaner, greener future. learn more: strategy and goals the bissell sustainability commitment begins with setting corporate sustainability goals and building a strategy for effectively pursuing those goals. read more > products product and packaging design present significant opportunities for advancing bissell sustainability efforts. read more > operations efficient use of resources in the production and transportation of our products is both good for the environment and good for our business. read more > partnerships creating a sustainable future requires a combined effort across the globe. read more > corporate social responsibility like sustainability, social responsibility is an important element of bissell global business operations. our commitment to social responsibility starts with our operations and those of our suppliers. the bissell code of conduct defines high standards of conduct for our employees. a similar code of conduct, contract provisions and a rigorous audit process ensure these same standards are deployed throughout the bissell supply chain. and, finally, bissell extends its reach within the communities in which we operate, supporting numerous non-profit organizations. read more about supply chain social responsibility read more about bissell’s
