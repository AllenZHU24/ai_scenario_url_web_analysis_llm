privacy policy. what personal information do we collect? “personal information” is any information by which you can be contacted or identified. for example, when you make a purchase, sign-up for emails or otherwise provide us with information when you interact with our website, we collect certain information from you, including your name, email address, billing address, phone number and payment information (including your credit card number, or amazon pay or paypal information). personal information may also be collected from your cookie and usage data. for example, when you visit the website, we automatically collect certain personal information including information about your web browser, ip address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. additionally, as you browse the website, we collect information about how you interact with the website such as the web pages or products that you view, or what search terms referred you to the website. we may also collect some personal information from our social media sites based on your interaction with us on social media. we collect information using the following technologies: “cookies” are used to collect data about your browsing and purchasing behavior. cookies are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. for example, the cookie data we collect may include information about what products you have purchased, what pages have been viewed and/or how our customers browse the website. for more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org. "log files" are used to better understand how the website is used, and we track actions occurring on the website and collect data including your ip address, browser type, internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps. "web beacons", "tags", and "pixels" are electronic files used to record information about how you browse our website. we may share your personal information with third parties. for example, we use shopify to power our online store--you can read more about how shopify uses your personal information here:  https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy. we also use google analytics to transmit website traffic data to google’s servers in the united states. by using google analytics’ reports, we are better able to understand the traffic to the website and how our website is used. google analytics does not associate an ip address to identify an individual user. you can read more about how google uses your personal information here:  https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy.  you can also opt-out of google analytics here:  https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. how do we use your personal information? we use the information we collect pertaining to your order generally to fulfill
