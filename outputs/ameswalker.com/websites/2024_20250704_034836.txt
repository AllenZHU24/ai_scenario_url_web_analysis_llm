hosiery and lymphedema wraps are both used in the management of lymphedema, a condition characterized by the accumulation of lymph fluid and swelling in certain parts of the body. however, they serve different purposes and are applied in different ways.   graduated compression hosiery: purpose: graduated compression hosiery is designed to provide graduated pressure, meaning it is tighter at the extremities and gradually decreases in pressure as it moves up the limb. this gentle squeeze helps to move blood and lymphatic fluid up your legs, preventing fluid from pooling and reducing swelling.
