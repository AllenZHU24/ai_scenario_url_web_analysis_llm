worn items before returning. shoes may only be returned if unworn, in new condition, with the original box and tags attached (if applicable). items marked final sale or non-returnable on our website may not be returned or exchanged. exchanges are shipped once we receive and process your returned package. if you don’t have time to wait on the exchange, then it’s best to place a new order and you shipping label is used you may ship your item or items back to us at no cost to you. return shipping labels are not provided for lounge doctor leg rests.  usps typically provides the most economical shipping for large items via usps priority mail or usps parcel select shipping. how to return: complete and enclose the return form included with your original order. please keep the invoice portion of your order form for your records. use an appropriate shipping box or shipping bag to return your item(s) to us. cover or remove any original mailing address label or carrier barcodes from package. when returning products, do not use the product packaging as your shipping carton. if you utilize the enclosed return label, you may ship your returned item(s) to us for free (usa only) . simply drop off the package at any u.s. postal service mailbox or post office or leave it for your mail carrier.  due to size and/or weight, some items do not get an enclosed return label. if you choose to ship your return via other means, please use a traceable method, and keep a record of your tracking number to ensure its safe return to us. all returns should be sent to: ames walker 300 industrial park ave. asheboro, nc 27205-7328 please allow up to 14 business days for your return or exchange to arrive and be processed. for details and instructions for customers submitting returns from outside the
