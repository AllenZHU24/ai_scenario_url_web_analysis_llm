on any of our websites!thanks, drew  posted in fun facts and furniture tips by factorybunkbeds.what is the weight limit for the discovery world furniture top bunk bed?posted on october 28, 2013 we ask people not to get hung up on weight limits and here is why.  the discovery world furniture bunk beds have been 3rd party tested to meet the astm safety standards.   that is a top bed rating of 400lbs for at least 5 minutes before failing.  what that means is the testing company sets up the bed then puts the mattress down and slowly spreads weight evenly all across the top bed area until they reach 400lbs if the bed fails before that it does not pass.that being said this is not jumping up and down weight, this is distributed weight, the beds are very strong but do not think that they will hold a 400lbs person, as there would be no way for that person to get to the lay down position without focusing a lot of weight onto 1 or 2 slats at a time.  if you have jumpy kids or larger teens / adult that will be using the bed then we recommend purchasing the metal center support slats.  the metal center support help to spread the weight out accross the slats with the intent to keep an individual slat from failing when pressure is focused on a small area rather than increaing the weight limit.i would recomend a max weight of 200lbs for the top bed or 250 with the metal center supports.  we do sell these to colleges and mens and womens shelters and have had very few calls about beds breaking from normal use.thanks, drew  posted in fun facts and furniture tips by factorybunkbeds. 1 reply 7
