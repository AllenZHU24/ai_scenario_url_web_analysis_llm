applicable cpsia, fda, fcc, and state regulations.warning for california residents: the california safe drinking water and toxic enforcement act of 1986 (proposition 65) requires consumers in california to be warned prior to exposure to any of 900+ chemicals known to the state to cause cancer, birth defects, or other reproductive harm. our product suppliers test their products to determine if warning labels are required, and that information is shared with us. the overwhelming majority of products we sell require no warnings. where warnings are required, labels are applied to the
