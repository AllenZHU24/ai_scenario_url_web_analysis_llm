placed after 5 p.m. et will ship no later than the following business day. expedited shipping rates are determined by weight and zone. next-day and 2-day orders placed by 5 p.m. et monday-friday ship this service and excludes heavy items. in addition, orders for saturday delivery must be placed by 5 p.m. et on the preceding friday. if you require saturday delivery, please place your order by
