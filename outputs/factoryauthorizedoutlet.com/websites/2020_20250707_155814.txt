delivery shipping and delivery how do i track my order? to track your order simply enter your email and order number in our order tracker page. what countries do you ship to? we ship orders worldwide. what forms of shipping do you offer? standard ground - 1 - 5 business days - (48 contiguous states only) second day air express - 2 business days - (48 contiguous states, alaska & hawaii) next day air express - 1 business day - (48 contiguous states only) what are freight shipments? freight shipments are for heavier items that are required to be shipped by an ltl freight carrier. the freight company will require a valid phone number and will usually call 24 hours in advance to set up a delivery. in some instances, freight trucks will not be able to deliver due to the size of space or terrain. what do you charge for shipping? we offer free ground shipping on all orders over $99 (48 contiguous states only) we offer $6.95 flat rate ground shipping on all orders under $99 (48 contiguous states only) next day air express is based on your order's weight and your shipping address. you can calculate your shipping easily on the first page of our checkout what is the time cutoff for express shipping? all express orders including next day & 2-day must be submitted before 3:00 pm (est) monday - friday to be shipped on the same day. delivery will be based on the next business day. orders submitted after 3:00 pm (est). will be shipped the next business day. express freight items over 150lb cutoff is 1 pm (est). what are the rules and restrictions for free shipping? spend just $99 or more and receive fast free ground shipping on your order. plus for those of you who need your order even quicker, for spending $99 or more we pass the savings on to you by offering discounted express shipping. for orders under $99, you will only pay a flat fee of $6.95. this shipping offer applies to the continental us only. all locations outside the continental us will receive discounted shipping, if you have any questions please feel free to contact us note: we do not ship to po boxes. site map about us contact us shipping and delivery businesses and professionals faqs
