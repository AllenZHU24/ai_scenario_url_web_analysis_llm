return policy is simple. we accept all returns that meet the following conditions: in order to be a valid return, you must log into your account and fill out the return authorization form for that order which can be found by clicking the return/exchange item link on your order details. be sure your return reaches bikebandit.com within the timelines outlined above. all acceptable returns that meet our return conditions below are credited for 100% of the merchandise value (including sales tax for orders shipped within ca). you are responsible for shipping costs associated with your return. you may choose to send items back using your own shipping method or for customers who use our convenient, prepaid ups return label, a flat shipping rate of $8.95 will be deducted from your refund if shipping from with the contiguous 48 us states. for returns shipping from outside the united states, please contact customer service for assistance at 1-888-339-3888. please note: shipping/handling/oversize fees are not reimbursed with returns. if we shipped you the wrong item, a broken or damaged item, we will pay the cost of your return shipping once we receive and accept the return. simply select the corresponding reason code during the online returns process detailed below and someone from our customer service department will contact you. * please follow the steps in the online return process and wait to be contacted by customer service before shipping these items back to us. in the case of a manufacturer defect, each manufacturer has its own warranty policy. most manufacturers offer an additional warranty period which covers against merchandise defects. please contact customer service and we can assist you in contacting the manufacturer. manufacturer warranties do not cover defects determined to be caused by normal wear or due to customer negligence. oem(original manufacturer packaging) please note that all items will have to include all original packaging in good condition. (free from scratches, blemishes, tears, dents, and markings - including writing of any kind.) if bikebandit.com receives your return with damaged or missing the original packaging the item will be rejected, and your return will not be processed. aftermarket parts please note that all items will have to include all original packaging and labels in good condition.
