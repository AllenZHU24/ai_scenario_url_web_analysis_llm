documentation of the benchmade limited lifetime warranty. benchmade knife company, inc. warrants that benchmade knives purchased from authorized dealers will be free from defects in materials and modified in any way, as these may cause the knife to function differently (and potentially less safely) than originally designed and intended. mail benchmade knife and service form(s) to: simply ship
