ensure the best experience, whether it be with one of our knives or while navigating our website, this sentiment is extended toward anyone that should come to our website. no matter the technology or ability, the benchmade team is dedicated to giving the widest audience possible an optimal experience on the benchmade.com website. our team is diligently working toward increasing the usability and accessibility of benchmade.com, with the utmost effort to comply to the available standards and guidelines. it is of high importance to our team that we meet the level double-a of the world wide web consortium (w3c) web content accessibility guidelines 2.1. by following these guidelines, we are able to learn how to create a more accessible website for those with disabilities. in tandem, these guidelines aid us in delivering a more accessible and user-friendly site for everyone. although we hope to meet these standards site-wide as soon as possible, there are obstacles that will make this a challenging standard to meet across all sections of our website. the benchmade team is committed to working continually to meet these standards across the entirety of benchmade.com, bringing ideal web-accessibility to all that visit our site. should you need any assistance when navigating or accessing and sections of our website, we are happy to hear from you by calling us at 1-833-557-2526 or
