for being a pioneer innovator in the production of high-end speaker systems.  bose corp. was founded in 1964 by dr. amar g. bose, a professor at the massachusetts institute of technology (mit). bose works constantly to research and develop new technology that will better the lives of their customers.  throughout the years, bose has developed unique sound solutions that have met virtually every audio challenge in every different form.  many of bose's products are designed for home audio solutions that include high end home theater systems and integrated music, acoustically customized automotive systems and component quality home stereo loudspeakers all which create a unique entertainment experience. bose speakers bose speakers are engineered to give you the best possible sound no matter what your unique situation is. bose speakers feature technology that has been researched for over 40 years and is guaranteed to give you terrific bose sound. bose outdoor speakers give the listener an unmatched "outdoor theater" experience. bose outdoor speakers are extremely durable and can function in the most extreme weather conditions. bose computer speakers feature the same technology as the bose home audio speakers assuring you of home theater quality sound coming from your computer. the bose sounddock gives you bose® quality sound from your ipod®, ipod nano or ipod video. the bose sound dock fills the room with vibrant, detailed bose sound that will improve the way you experience your ipod. bose bookshelf speakers are a great addition to any home audio system. they are both stylish and functional and can fit in nicely on a bookshelf or a table.bose jewel cube® speakers featured on the bose lifestyle® v-35 home entertainment system deliver a unique sound experience by packing theater quality sound into speakers that can fit into the palm of your hand. jewell cube® technology allows bose to incorporate 40 different innovations and technologies into a 4 1/2 inch tall speaker. the bose jewell cube speaker's design also allows it to fit nicely into your home decor. bose home theater systems bose home theater systems include both the bose lifestyle® home entertainment system and the bose cinemate® and solo systems, which are each designed to fit your specific needs. the bose cinemate® and solo tv sound systems were engineered for people who want terrific sound without a clutter of speakers. bose cinemate® home theater speaker system features a one speaker array that produces mid and high-frequency sounds customized to the size and shape of your room by the proprietary adaptiq® audio calibration system. the bose solo tv sound system delivers incredible clarity and richness of sound with just one connection to your tv and one plug to your wall.if you are ready for the ultimate home theater experience, then the bose lifestyle series is the system for you. available in five different variations (bose lifestyle 135, v25, v35, t20 and 235), the bose lifestyle® systems include legendary bose sound quality, minimalist design and unobtrusive electronics blended together to enrich the home entertainment experience in your unique living space. bose home theater systems have won countless awards and achieved universal accolades because of their quality and style. the bose lifestyle®, cinemate® and solo systems are very practical because of their sleek design that allows you to experience a high end home theater system that fits nicely into any room. bose headphones bose headphones incorporate the latest in headphone technology to give you the same clean, crisp sound that you get from their award winning speakers. during the design stages, bose took into consideration not only the quality of sound that their headphones produce, but also the level of comfort they will provide. terrific bose sound combined with incredible comfort are the two main reasons that the bose in-ear headphones are the leading
