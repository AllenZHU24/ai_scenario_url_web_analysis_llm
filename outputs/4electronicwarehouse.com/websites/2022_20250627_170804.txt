illinois corporate blog: 1stinvideomusicworld.com partner sites: discountgolfworld.com, 4discounttravel.com president and ceo: dennis boudreau our history your electronic warehouse is a consumer electronics store that is a division of 1st in video-music world, inc. the parent company, 1st in video-music world, started in 1975 as music world, a brick and mortar stereo equipment store in quincy, illinois. our founder, dennis boudreau, started music world out of a project for a college class on how to start a small business. after 5 years of selling strictly stereo equipment, music world began to sell video equipment in 1980 and the company name evolved into 1st in video-music world inc. making the transition to selling video equipment was the first of many adaptations for 1st in video-music world. in 1982, it became the concept store for the first complete a/v system for pioneer, and later incorporated video rental during the boom of the vcr. but 1st in video music world’s greatest advancements came about in the mid-90’s. in 1994, 1st in video-music world opened its new mega store and took up the name your electronic warehouse. three years later, your electronic warehouse took its business online as 4electronicwarehouse.com and over the last ten years has developed into an internet retailer top 500 company. we know what it takes over the years we have learned what it takes to be a successful internet company. not only do we manage our main website 4electronicwarehouse.com, we have an ebay store and two partner sites, discountgolfworld.com and 4discounttravel.com. we are internet authorized dealers of every brand that we carry. being internet authorized means that we purchase directly from the manufacturer, offer their warranty and have trained salesmen who are experts on all of their products. we are also the first internet authorized dealer on ebay for both monster cable and bose, two of the most well known electronics brands in the industry. your electronic warehouse is hacker safe certified. this assures you that every online purchase you make from us is completely safe and secure.. hard work pays off our hard work and dedication has not gone unnoticed. we had some great success at the 2007 consumer electronics show where we won monster cable’s most monsterous e-commerce retailer of the year award. this award goes out to the company that provides the most quality online experience to the customer. other nominees for the award included: amazon.com, crutchfield and onecall. community service we pride ourselves on our service to the customer, but we also take great pride in service to our community. our employees are encouraged to donate their time and money to better serve the community. we work very closely with our local colleges’ internship programs, giving many students a start to their careers. in fact, 50% of your electronic warehouse’s employees are graduates of quincy university and we hope to continue that tradition. we’re here for you at your electronic warehouse, we strive to give our customers the best possible online experience. we have several product specialists on staff that can help you with any questions you have about our products. if you need your products professionally installed in your home, we can do it  through our local installers or our nationwide installation network. we hope you find what you are looking for at your electronic warehouse.  if you have any trouble finding something, feel free to call us, or chat with one of our experts online. one of our
