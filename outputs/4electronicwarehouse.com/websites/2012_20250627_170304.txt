products through research® about bose the bose corporation is known best for being a pioneer innovator in the production of high-end speaker systems.  bose corp. was founded in 1964 by dr. amar g. bose, a professor at the massachusetts institute of technology (mit). bose works constantly to research and develop new technology that will better the lives of their customers.  throughout the years, bose has developed unique sound solutions that have met virtually every audio challenge in every different form.  many of bose's products are designed for home audio solutions that include high end home theater systems and integrated music, acoustically customized automotive systems and component quality home stereo loudspeakers all which create a unique entertainment experience. bose speakers bose speakers are engineered to give you the best possible sound no matter what your unique situation is. bose speakers feature technology that has been researched for over 40 years and is guaranteed to give you terrific bose sound. bose outdoor speakers give the listener an unmatched "outdoor theater" experience. bose outdoor speakers are extremely durable and can function in the most extreme weather conditions. bose computer speakers feature the same technology as the bose home audio speakers assuring you of home theater quality sound coming from your computer. the bose sounddock gives you bose® quality sound from your ipod®, ipod nano or ipod video. the bose sound dock fills the room with vibrant, detailed bose sound that will improve the way you experience your ipod. bose bookshelf speakers are a great addition to any home audio system. they are both stylish and functional and can fit in nicely on a bookshelf or a table. bose jewel cube® speakers (featured on the bose lifestyle v-30, bose lifestyle® 48 and bose lifestyle 35 systems) deliver a unique sound experience by packing theater quality sound into speakers that can fit into the palm of your hand. jewell cube technology allows bose to incorporate 40 different innovations and technologies into a 4 1/2 inch tall speaker. the bose jewell cube speaker's design also allows it to fit nicely into your home decor. bose home theater systems bose home theater systems include both the bose lifestyle® home entertainment systems and the bose 3-2-1® systems, that are each designed to fit your specific needs. the bose 3-2-1 gs and bose 3-2-1 gsx were engineered for people who want terrific sound without a clutter of speakers. the bose 3-2-1's two speaker design gives top quality sound by reflecting sound waves off of the walls in your home. if you are ready for the ultimate home theater experience, then the bose lifestyle series is the system for you. the bose lifestyle® series includes four different models (bose lifestyle® 48, bose lifestyle® 38, bose lifestyle® 35, and bose lifestyle® 28) all designed with different specifications to give a uniquely bose experience. bose home theater systems have won many awards and achieved many accolades because of their quality and style. the bose lifestyle® and bose 3-2-1 systems are both very practical because of their sleek design that allows you to experience a high end home theater system that fits nicely into any room. bose headphones bose headphones incorporate the latest in headphone technology to give you the same clean, crisp sound that you get from their award winning speakers. during the design stages, bose took into consideration not only the quality of sound that their headphones produce, but also the level of comfort they will
