and is automatically applied as a discount on your next eligible purchase. fanatics rewards members earn 5% fancash on all eligible purchases. how is fancash earned?+ fancash is earned on the purchase amount after all applicable discounts are applied and before tax is applied. fancash will be automatically credited to your account after every eligible purchase. it's our way of saying thanks for being part of our team. how do i use fancash?+ when you are ready to check out, log in with your username and password. your fancash will be deducted from your subtotal during checkout. fancash cannot be used for shipping expenses or sales taxes. remember, you can only use fancash when you're logged in! does fancash expire?+ fancash earned between 1/1/2016 and 9/14/2016 will expire 6 months after the most recent purchase; fancash earned between 9/15/2016 and 12/31/16 will expire 11:59 pm est 12/31/16. beginning 1/1/17, your fancash will expire 6 months after your most recent purchase. fancash & returned merchandise+ fancash is not applicable to previous orders or returns. if you return merchandise for which you earned fancash, that fancash will be deducted from your balance. terms &
