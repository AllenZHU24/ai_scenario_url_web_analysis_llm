buzz club rewards™ is a program that allows you to earn discounts good for 20% off a future purchase. how do i earn a 20% discount? just have the cashier scan your buzz club rewards™ card every time you shop. make 10 qualifying purchases of $20 or more (before tax is applied) within a 12-month period and earn a 20% discount on a future purchase. discounts are good for 60 days from the date you earn them. what is a qualifying purchase?
