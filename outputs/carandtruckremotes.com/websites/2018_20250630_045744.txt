your replacement car remotes, car keys and related accessories and parts. mr. mariusz sobanski, company founder and president, used to be in a the car sales business. he realized that most of his customers were purchasing cars that were simply a couple of years old. these customers were really unhappy to learn that they would usually be getting just only one key (or a couple of keys) and no remotes. they usually got even more upset to learn that it would cost them a few hundred dollars to replace missing keys or remotes. "there's got to be a better way", he thought. that's how it started. we simply started buying remotes wholesale, overstock from shops, dealers, auction houses and everyone that wanted to sell the remotes in lots and for cheap. since we get these remotes for less, we do not have to stick to dealer list price, therefore, we can sell the remotes for less.fact sheetour company remotes2003 inc, was
