guide the four most popular factors, or the “four c’s”, used to measure diamond quality are diamond cut, diamond color, diamond clarity, and carat weight. this section contains all the information you need to search through our diamond database with confidence and find the diamond that's right for you. color diamond color is one of the most important factors to consider, as it is noticeable to the naked eye. learn more cut a diamond's cut is widely considered to be the most important aspect of the four c's. learn more carat diamond weight is measured in carats, a small unit of measurement equal to 200 milligrams. learn more clarity learn about blemishes and inclusions, characteristics that can detract from the pure beauty of a diamond. learn more shape various diamond shapes all have their own distinct and unique properties that all depend upon its shape. learn more certification adiamor loose diamonds are graded by either gia or ags, the two most recognized laboratories in the world.
