payment options payment options for your convenience, adiamor accepts the following forms of payment: note about international credit cards: adiamor does not currently accept payments via international credit card. if you wish to use your international credit card, please use an alternative payment service such as paypal to complete your order. financing with affirm we have partnered with affirm to give you a simple way to make your purchase with no hidden fees. easy monthly payments: provide some basic information and get a real-time credit decision to split your purchase into monthly payments. rates from 10% to 30% apr with loans of 3, 6, or 12 month terms. flexible repayment: simply pay your monthly bill using a debit card, bank transfer at www.affirm.com/pay. paypal® after selecting paypal you will be directed to log in to your pre-existing paypal account or be directed to create a new account. after logging in you will select your preferred billing, shipping and payment options; upon completion you will be prompted to provide consent for adiamor to charge your paypal account. bankwire how do i pay by bank wire? once you have confirmed wire transfer as your payment method, we will send you an email containing instructions for initiating the bank wire transfer. please note: all payments made by bank wire will receive a 1.5% discount. this discount is offered due to the elimination of clearinghouse charges imposed by credit card companies. sales tax sales tax will be charged on all orders shipped within the state of california. customer service
