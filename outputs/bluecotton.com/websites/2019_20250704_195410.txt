our story stroll down memory lane bluecotton is actually an accident. in 1991, two college grads were hard on their luck and trying to find a job. a business venture gone bad left them with close to 1000 t-shirts and a huge credit card bill to boot. in an attempt to alleviate their cramped apartment of the surplus t-shirts and pay bills, they started selling t-shirts to fraternities and sororities at western kentucky university. one thing led to another and this happy accident eventually became the real deal!
