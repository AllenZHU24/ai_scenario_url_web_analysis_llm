compatible with thousands of games across four generations of xbox. with smart delivery games, you can buy a game once and get the best version of that game for the console you’re playing on. to play a backwards compatible game, insert the disc or search your library and select the game you want to play. does the xbox series s have backwards compatibility? xbox series s is also backwards compatible with thousands of games across four generations of xbox. with smart delivery games, you can buy a digital game once and get the best version of that game for the console you’re playing on. because xbox series s is all-digital, to play a backwards compatible game just access your digital game library through the console and select the game you want to play. which games are included as xbox smart delivery games? all xbox game studio titles include smart delivery, so you get the best version of the game no matter which console you play on. partner games that have chosen to include smart delivery will make note of the feature on their digital game page or on the box the disc comes in. how do you download games on the xbox series s? your games need to be downloaded to your device
