instructions printed on the reverse side of your order invoice titled, "how to return or exchange merchandise,” or click here to print return form. let us know if you’d like a gift card or a refund, or to exchange merchandise. we will attempt to notify you if we don’t have the items you have requested. to better ensure timely processing, please carefully package your return using original packing materials and affix the return address label/barcode that identifies your specific order (found at the bottom of the order receipt) to the outside of the box, along with your preferred shipper's label
