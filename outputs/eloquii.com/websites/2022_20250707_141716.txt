cards i can have in a single purchase? what is an eloquii egift card? eloquii egift cards are issued electronically, via email. once purchased and activated, an eloquii egift card is emailed to a recipient and holds a balance that can be used on a future eloquii.com purchase. are there shipping charges on egift cards? shipping is free when purchasing an egift card. when redeeming egift cards, shipping charges will apply to your order based on your order value; see the shipping info page for more information. can i check my egift card balance? yes! check your egift card balance here. you can also confirm your egift card balance at checkout. when do egift cards expire? egift cards do not expire. certain offers earned as part of a limited-time promotion may have a promotional balance only valid for a set time period. see the email received with your card for full details. how are egift cards sent to the recipient? egift cards are emailed to the recipient designated at checkout, within a few hours or will be sent on the date you’ve selected for delivery. the email will include redemption instructions as well as any gift note specified at checkout. what does a egift card recipient receive? the egift card email sent to the recipient will include the design you choose and the message you enter at checkout. the email will specify the amount of the egift card, an egift card code and pin, and instructions for redeeming the egift card on an eloquii.com order. how do i know that the recipient actually received the egift card i sent them? a delivery confirmation email will be sent to you when your egift card has been delivered to the recipient. can i use a promotion code, e-cash, elq reward, or other order-level discounts when purchasing an egift card? promotion codes, e-cash, elq rewards, and other order discounts cannot be applied to purchases of egift cards. will i earn elq rewards when on the purchase of a egift card? elq rewards are not earned on the purchase of a egift card. how can i redeem an eloquii egift card? you can redeem an eloquii egift card on eloquii.com by entering the egift card code and pin into the egift card fields at checkout, or on the cart page. the egift card value will be applied to the merchandise. can i use promo codes, e-cash, elq rewards, or other order discounts when i use my egift card to pay for an order at eloquii.com? yes! eligible discounts can be used on orders where an egift card is used as the payment method. can i reload my eloquii egift card, or combine balances from multiple eloquii egift cards? eloquii egift cards cannot be reloaded or combined. can i return my eloquii egift card for a refund? we can refund unused eloquii egift cards to the original method of payment, within 30 days of purchase. for a refund, please contact us. please be prepared to provide the order number or ordering email address from the original purchase of the egift card, and we’ll do our best to help! we are not able to refund egift cards that have been partially redeemed, or egift cards purchased more than 30 days ago. i made a purchase using an eloquii egift card. how will i receive my refund? when you use an eloquii egift card to make a purchase, and later return items from that purchase, we’ll load the refund amount back onto the egift card you used. we’ll email the egift card number, usage instructions, and available balance to the email address used on the order, just in case you no longer have it. if you used another method of payment in addition to the egift card, your refund is first applied to the egift card up to the amount you originally redeemed, and then any remaining refund value is refunded to the other method of payment. how can i get help with a lost egift card, or other egift card issues? for help with egift cards, please contact us. please be prepared to provide the order number, ordering email address, or recipient’s email address from the original purchase of the egift card, and we’ll do our best to help! are there any fees associated with eloquii egift cards? there are no fees to purchase or redeem an eloquii egift card. egift cards purchased from eloquii.com do not expire and are not subject to service fees. certain offers earned as part of a limited-time promotion may have a promotional balance only valid for a set time period. see the email received with your card for full details. can i use my eloquii egift card to purchase from walmart, or other walmart brands? eloquii egift cards are only valid for purchases made at eloquii.com, and can’t be used on other websites or store locations. can i redeem a walmart egift card at eloquii.com? eloquii.com accepts eloquii egift cards, as well as other methods of payment as shown at checkout. we’re not able to accept other branded egift cards. can i use affirm as a payment method when purchasing an egift card? no, affirm cannot be used on purchases that contain an egift card. is there a maximum value i can have on a single egift card? yes, there’s a $500 maximum. is there a maximum value of egift cards i can have in a single purchase? yes, you can purchase no more than $1,500 worth
