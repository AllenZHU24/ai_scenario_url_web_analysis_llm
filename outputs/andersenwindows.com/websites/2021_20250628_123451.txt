careers learn more quality diversity and inclusion our commitment to sustainability home  all windows & doors  materials using the right materials in the right places every day we have a team of engineers hard at work determining how to improve our windows and doors. every aspect of our products are scrutinized so that each product that goes into your home will provide years of faithful service. materials are one way that we've led the industry in innovation. we've concluded that it takes a carefully orchestrated use of materials to achieve superior performance, low-maintenance exteriors and long-lasting durability our customers deserve. wood it’s been at the core of our products for over 100 years. we use wood for strength and rigidity in our frames and sash, beauty on our interiors and its overall thermal performance is hard to beat. vinyl where andersen has innovated with vinyl is by using it as a protective cladding for its wood core windows. composite (fibrex® material) we've developed a material that is 2x as strong as vinyl, performs better when exposed to extreme temperatures and delivers exteriors that won’t fade, flake, blister or peel. aluminum we use aluminum to provide a virtually maintenance-free exterior that resists the elements. fiberglass fiberglass is a tough, weather-resistant material. we use it to keep the exteriors maintenance-free and combine it with beautiful wood interiors. andersen tested. homeowner approved. our materials and products are tested to meet the highest quality standards. learn more our series explore our series
