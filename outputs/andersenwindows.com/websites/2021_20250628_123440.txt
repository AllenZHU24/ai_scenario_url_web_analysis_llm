single-hung & double-hung windows double-hung windows have two operating sash that move up and down allowing for ventilation on the top, bottom or both. single-hung windows allow ventilation through a single operable lower sash. both offer easy cleaning from inside your home and remain flush with the wall, making them ideal for patios or walkways. learn about replacement options with renewal by
