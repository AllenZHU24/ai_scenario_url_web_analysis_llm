design inspiration design a window choose a window category below to get started. awning hinged at the top, the sash swings outward from the bottom. casement hinged on either side, the sash opens horizontally opposite the hinge. single-hung & double-hung venting upper and/or lower sash in a single frame that slide vertically past one another. gliding venting sash in a single frame that slide horizontally past one another. picture usually stationary, rectangular, non-opening windows that allow light into the room. bay and bow these window combinations project outward from the home, providing additional interior space and additional light. design a door choose a door category below to get started. hinged patio doors hinged patio doors are commonly known as french doors, and are available as inswing or outswing models with optional sidelights and transom windows. gliding patio doors also called sliding glass doors, gliding patio doors have at least one door panel that glides
