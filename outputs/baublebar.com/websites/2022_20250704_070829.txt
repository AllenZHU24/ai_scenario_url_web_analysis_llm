shipping options we offer free shipping on all us orders over $75 via ups surepost. please note that the shipping threshold applies to the total paid after discounts are applied. orders below $75 will be charged a fee for shipping and handling. ups ground, ups 2-day, and ups overnight shipping are available for an additional fee. orders being shipped to p.o. boxes, apos and fpos will ship via usps, and are not eligible for ups shipping methods. shipping policy we process and ship orders from our new jersey warehouse monday through friday. when your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing your tracking information. please check your spam folder if you did not receive this email. once your order is placed, we are unable to upgrade your shipping selection or change your shipping address.if you have a baublebar account, you can track your order by clicking "account" and then "my orders". please note, pre-sale and custom items will ship after their stated production timelines, which can be found on their respective product pages, at checkout, and in your order confirmation email. pre-sale and custom items are not eligible for ups ground, ups 2-day or ups overnight shipping.all orders are charged at the time of purchase, inclusive of pre-sale and custom items. address changes for address changes, please email our customer care team at helpme@baublebar.com with your updated address. our team will do our best to update your shipping address before your order ships. holiday custom orders order cutoff dates for delivery by 12/23 for all custom items can be found on their respective product pages. if you have questions about ordering custom items in time for the holidays, please reach out to helpme@baublebar.com. holiday return policy we’re pleased to offer you an extended return window for eligible items during the holiday season. unworn merchandise, unless marked as final sale or otherwise noted (see below for more details), purchased between november 1, 2022, and december 16, 2022, can be returned until january 15, 2023 for a full refund to your original form of payment. returned packages for orders placed during this period must be postmarked by january 15, 2023, at the latest. any orders placed after december 16, 2022, will follow our regular 30-day return policy.merchandise must be returned unused, with tags, and in the original packaging. gift sets must be returned in their entirety to be eligible for a refund.solid gold and diamond fine jewelry is eligible for a free return but will not arrive with a prepaid return label. please email helpme@baublebar.com for assistance returning these items. how to make a return merchandise eligible for return will arrive with a prepaid return label. when returning your merchandise, please pack unworn items in their original packaging and include your original invoice. if you cannot find your invoice, please include a printout of your order confirmation email. without this information, we will not be able to process your return.please pack your return with care. items that are damaged in transit due to inadequate packaging will not be eligible for a refund.you’re also welcome to use the shipping method of your choice instead of our prepaid return label. however, we are not responsible for lost return packages. we recommend you insure and track your package.if you choose to use your own return label, please address the return to:baublebar returns300 carol placemoonachie, nj 07074please note: solid gold and diamond fine jewelry is eligible for a free return but will not arrive with a prepaid return label. please email helpme@baublebar.com for assistance returning these items. gift returns if you were given an item from baublebar that you would like to return, please reach out to us at helpme@baublebar.com. if your gift did not include a prepaid return label: once we receive your email, we will send you a prepaid return label. package your item with the original invoice and note on the invoice that this order is a gift return. this is helpful information for our receiving team so we can correctly process the return and issue the credit. use the prepaid return label to send the item(s) back to us. the purchaser will not be notified of the return.if your gift did include a prepaid return label: once you've emailed us, package your item with the original invoice and note on the invoice that this order is a gift return. this is helpful information for our receiving team so we can correctly process the return and issue the credit. use the prepaid return label to send the item(s) back to us. the purchaser will not be notified of the return.we're unable to process exchanges, but we'll send you a credit in the form of a discount code to pick out something else. we'll be back in touch with your code once your return has been received. refunds upon receipt and processing of your return, which typically takes up to 20 business days from your date of shipment, you will receive an email from baublebar with confirmation of your refund to your original form of payment. please note that taxes will be refunded along with the items that you send back, however, shipping charges will not be refunded. credit card refunds may take up to 10 business days for your bank to complete.due to carrier delays, please allow extra days for your return to be received and processed. you will only receive an email once your return has been fully processed. you will not receive an email when your return has been received. items not eligible for returns we do not accept returns for any custom-made products and final sale merchandise. this information is also outlined on each respective product page, at checkout, and on your order confirmation email. exchanges we do not process exchanges, even if it is for the same item in a different color or size. many items sell out while the requested exchange is returned and processed. we encourage you to return the original merchandise and then visit our website to purchase the correct item in a new order. price adjustments we do not offer price adjustments. quality guarantee quality is of the utmost importance to us. if your item is less than perfect upon arrival, we want to know! send a photo to helpme@baublebar.com and our customer care team can assist. gift boxes & pouches if you order a gift box or pouch with your item, your item will not arrive wrapped. meet baublebar about us coming soon find us in stores gift cards careers
