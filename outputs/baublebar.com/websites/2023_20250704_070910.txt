shipping on all us orders at and above $75. please note that the shipping threshold applies to the total paid after discounts are applied and before taxes. orders below $75 will be charged a fee for selecting expedited shipping at checkout does not change the production time for custom items. once your item has been produced, it will be sent with the shipping method selected at checkout. please refer to the product page for production timeline.please note, orders placed after 1pm with an expedited shipping method will be shipped the following business day.we do not currently ship outside of the united states, but don't worry - we are working on adding this option soon! shipping policy we process and ship orders from our new jersey warehouse monday through friday. when your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing your tracking information. please check your spam folder if you did not receive this email. once your order is placed, we are unable to upgrade your shipping selection or change your shipping address.if you have a baublebar account, you can track your order by clicking "account" and then "my orders".custom items will ship after their stated production timelines, which can be found on their respective product pages, at checkout, and in your order confirmation email.all orders are charged at the time of purchase, inclusive of pre-sale and custom items. return policy merchandise, unless marked as final sale or otherwise noted, can be returned for a full refund to your original form of payment within 30 days of purchase.products must be returned unused, with tags, and in the original packaging. gift sets must be returned in their entirety to be eligible for a refund. original shipping charges are not refundable. how to make a return merchandise eligible for return will arrive with a prepaid return label. when returning your merchandise, please pack unworn items in their original packaging and include your original invoice. if you cannot find your invoice, please include a printout of your order confirmation email. without this information, we will not be able to process your return. please pack your return with care. items that are damaged in transit due to inadequate packaging will not be eligible for a refund.if you need any additional assistance or questions about returns, please email us at helpme@baublebar.com. gift returns if you were given an item from baublebar that you would like to return, please reach out to us at helpme@baublebar.com. custom items and clearance items are not returnable, even if they were received as a gift. if your gift did not include a prepaid return label: send us an email and we will send you a prepaid return label. package your item with the original invoice and note on the invoice that this order is a gift return. use the prepaid return label to send the item(s) back to us. the purchaser will not be notified of the return.if your gift did include a prepaid return label: package your item with the original invoice and note on the invoice that this order is a gift return. use the prepaid return label to send the item(s) back to us. the purchaser will not be notified of the return.we're unable to process exchanges; however, eligible items can be returned for a credit. please reach out to the customer care team at helpme@baublebar.com with the following information:1. item you are returning 2. order number. if you don't have the order number, please provide the purchaser's full name and/or email address. address changes please verify shipping address before completing an order. we process orders quickly, and cannot guarantee that changes can be made after an order has been placed. please email us at helpme@baublebar.com if there is an issue with the address, and we will do our best to help. items not eligible for returns we do not accept returns for any custom-made products and final sale merchandise. this information is also outlined on each respective product page, at checkout, and on your order confirmation email. price adjustments we do not offer price adjustments. gift boxes & pouches you can purchase gift boxes separately on our site, however, we do not offer gift wrapping. please note, gift boxes are not intended for custom blankets and throws. holiday 2023 return policy
