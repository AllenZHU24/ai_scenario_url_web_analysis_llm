discount code and gift with purchase offers. to obtain a price adjustment, please send us an email to [email protected] within the seven day window. include your order number in your email message. if you are within the deadline, we will refund the difference to your original form of payment. items purchased with a promotional discount are not eligible for price adjustments. items purchased with
