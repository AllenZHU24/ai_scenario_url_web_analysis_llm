faqs chat close « back to main page customer service how do i contact balsam hill? you may reach a customer loyalty specialist by phone, email, or live chat here. how do i post a review for an item? to post a review of an item purchased from balsam hill, simply click the “write a review” button located at the top right-hand corner of the product’s page. if you want to share your overall experience with balsam hill, you may write a review on trustpilot. what if the price of a product i purchased is reduced? balsam hill will occasionally make adjustments to product prices. our policy regarding price adjustments for customers is as follows: for purchases made between february 1 at 12:00am pst and december 20 at 11:59pm pst if the price of an item you purchased is reduced before december 20 at 11:59pm pst and within 14 days of your original purchase date, please contact us and we will be happy to credit you for the difference between the price you paid (net of any promotions or coupons) and the current price. for purchases made between december 21 at 12:00am pst and january 31 at 11:59pm pst if the price of an item you purchased is reduced within 14 days of your original purchase date, please contact us and we will be happy to credit you for the difference between the price you paid (net of any promotions or coupons) and the current price. please note that our price guarantee does not include clearance items or items that go on clearance. coupon adjustments are not included. coupons cannot be combined with any other offer unless explicitly stated and may not be redeemed for cash or cash equivalent. discount code must be applied online at balsamhill.com, or by phone during checkout and may not be applied to pending or previous orders. how do i subscribe to
