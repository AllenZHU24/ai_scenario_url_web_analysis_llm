policies shipping policies free shipping all orders that meet the following requirements will have the free shipping option. the order must be over $100 (does not include tax or shipping) all items in the order must qualify for free shipping. look for this on the product page underneath the price. this item qualifies for free shipping! the order must be shipped within the 48 contiguous us states. if your order qualifies for free shipping and you choose free shipping, the shipping method selected will be up to actionvillage.com’s discretion. p.o. boxes, fpo & apo addresses are excluded* processing & shipping times all orders take between 24-48 hours to process and ship from our facility. this does not include weekend days. actionvillage.com uses fedex and usps as our primary shipping carriers. for delivery times, please refer to the map and charts below. please keep in mind that the day a package is picked up is not to be considered in your shipping times. domestic shipping times
