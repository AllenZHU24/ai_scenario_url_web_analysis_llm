if you ship to a physical (street) address within the contiguous 48 states, we offer $3.99 flat rate shipping through ups. if you ship to a po box, usps shipping rates will apply. do you ship internationally? yes, we do! just change the flag at the top of our homepage to convert all prices to your preferred currency and remove items from your shopping experience that are unavailable to ship to your region. do you ship to apo boxes? yes, we do! postal service, apo, dpo, and fpo shipping rates apply (½ price shipping for military). the us postal service will deliver to the military post office, which then delivers the package to the military location. do you offer in-store pick-up? yes, we do! our flagship store is located in omaha, nebraska, and our second brick-and-mortar store is located in overland park in the kansas city, kansas area. if your zip code is near either of our stores, a ship-to-store option will show up on the check-out page. there is no shipping charge to pick up an order in one of our stores, although sales tax is calculated for those locations. how can i look at more shipping options? if you place an item in your cart and select “click here to estimate shipping costs...” you will be able to enter your zip code and see multiple shipping costs for ups and usps for standard and expedited shipping. these options will also show up under the shipping options during the check-out process. how much will it cost to ship to another country? if you change the flag at the top of our homepage to reflect the country you are shopping from—and shipping to—you will see more (and better!) shipping options through ups i-parcel. flat rate shipping is not available outside the contiguous 48 us states, but ups i-parcel offers competitive shipping rates internationally. another important note to keep in mind is that orders being shipped out of the u.s. may be subject to taxes, customs duties, and other fees upon arrival at the destination country, for which the recipient is responsible. my order hasn’t shipped because something was on backorder; what should i do? if an item is on backorder, it will be marked as such in your cart during checkout. backordered items do not split out automatically. if you wish to split the order so the rest of your order ships sooner, please call our customer care staff toll-free at 1-800-590-5987 and they can split your order for you. if items in my order ship separately, will i be charged extra? as long as you place one single order shipping to one address, you will not be charged extra shipping for a split order. when can i expect my order to ship? most of our orders ship within 24 hours! depending on the time of year, some orders ship within a couple hours! can i ship to a us address when i live abroad? yes! change the flag on our homepage to the us flag so that it will show you us shipping options. currency will be in us dollars, and conversion rates may apply (please check with your credit card company, or payment company). return what is your return policy? you can see our full return policy on our return authorization page.. what do i need to know before i start the return process? you need your order number and access to a printer. how long do i have to return an item? all returns must be made within 90 days of purchase. i need to return an opened item that was received broken. please call our customer care staff toll-free at 1-800-590-5987. when you describe the damage to them, they can assist with a replacement. please save the box and all packaging paper as a claim with ups may be required. i opened the item and played with it and didn’t like it. we want you to be 100% satisfied with your order! you can either start the return authorization process yourself online or call our customer care staff toll-free at 1-800-590-5987. opened items can be returned for a gift certificate within 90 days of purchase. some exceptions apply. i didn’t open the item. you can return the new, unopened item for a full refund to the payment method you used. some exceptions apply. can i return/exchange a gift without the purchaser knowing? yes! you will need to call our customer care staff toll-free at 1-800-590-5987 so they can look up the original order (unless grandma or great-aunt hazel sent you the receipt with the order number) and advise you how to continue. if you already have the order number (you can find it on the shipping receipt if the package was shipped directly to you), you can also complete the return authorization online. make sure you select the box next to “this item was a gift” so that you can input your own contact information—this ensures that the purchaser is never notified of your return/exchange. how should i package my return? if possible, please use the original box and packing papers we shipped to you. this helps ensure that the item is not damaged during return shipping. however, if the original package was damaged during shipping, please put the returning item into an appropriate-sized shipping box. when can i expect my return to be processed? most returns are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the return in our warehouse. will i be charged shipping for my return? as long as you fill out the return authorization and use our shipping label, shipping costs are covered. international orders, however, are excluded from the free return label process. payment what payment types do you accept? we accept visa, mastercard, discover, and american express credit cards and gift cards, debit cards, paypal, and amazon payments. i’m an amazon prime member; do i get free shipping? amazon prime member benefits do not apply to orders placed directly through fat brain toys. however, we do offer flat-rate shipping of $3.99 (see shipping faq). is your site secure? yes! you will see either a little lock in the corner next to our url, or the https that designates that our site is secure, depending on what type of browser you are using. how do i check the balance on my gift card? please call our customer care staff toll-free at 1-800-590-5987 and they can look up the balance for you. do you accept purchase orders? yes, we do! you can email purchase orders to po@fatbraintoys.com schools, government institutions, and businesses can see more about using a purchase order by clicking here. when will i be charged for my order? we charge when we ship, although you may see an “authorization” on your credit or debit card after you make the purchase. this is our system checking to make sure funds are available for your purchase. why do i see multiple charges on my card for one order? since we only charge when we physically ship an item, if you had any items that were backordered, you will see a separate charge when those items ship. why is the actual payment on my card higher than when i checked out? (international orders only) if you do not change the flag at the top of our website, you will see un-converted costs. if you do change the flag to the region you are shopping from, this will convert item prices and shipping costs to your own currency. your credit card may also charge currency conversion costs. check policies with your card company. special fat brain toys services do you offer gift wrap?
