place the items in your cart and click on secure checkout. click on the blue link on the right side of the page to estimate shipping. then enter your postal code and country. customs fees that may be assessed in the destination country are not included in our shipping costs and vary according to the inbound country regulations. note: the initial outbound shipping costs are not refundable in the case of a package returned to the sender or an unauthorized return. do you ship to apo boxes? yes, we do! postal service, apo, dpo, and fpo shipping rates apply (price shipping for military). the us postal service will deliver to the military post office, which then delivers the package to the military location. do you offer in-store pick-up? yes, we do! our flagship store is located in omaha, nebraska, and our second brick-and-mortar store is located in overland park in the kansas city, kansas area. if your zip code is near either of our stores, a ship-to-store option will show up on the check-out page. there is no shipping charge to pick up an order in one of our stores, although sales tax is calculated for those locations. how can i look at more shipping options? when you get to the shipping method section during checkout it will provide you with a list of different shipping options available to you, depending on the address you provided how much will it cost to ship to another country? we ship to international destinations using usps priority international and usps express international. these costs are based on the weight and the size of the box. some countries limit the dimensions of incoming packages so there may be items we cannot ship. please keep in mind that orders being shipped out of the u.s. may be subject to taxes, customs duties, and other fees upon arrival at the destination country, for which the recipient is responsible. note: the initial outbound shipping costs are not refundable in the case of a package returned to the sender or an unauthorized return. my order hasn’t shipped because something was on backorder; what should i do? if an item is on backorder, it will be marked as such in your cart during checkout. backordered items do not split out automatically. if you wish to split the order so the rest of your order ships sooner, please call our customer care staff toll-free at 1-800-590-5987 and they can split your order for you. if items in my order ship separately, will i be charged extra? as long as you place one single order shipping to one address, you will not be charged extra shipping for a split order. when can i expect my order to ship? most of our orders ship within 24 hours! depending on the time of year, some orders ship within a couple hours! can i ship to a us address when i live abroad? yes! currency will be in us dollars, and conversion rates may apply (please check with your credit card company, or payment company). return what is your return policy? you can see our full return policy on our return authorization page.. what do i need to know before i start the return process? you need your order number and access to a printer. how long do i have to return an item? all returns must be made within 90 days of purchase. i need to return an opened item that was received broken. please call our customer care staff toll-free at 1-800-590-5987. when you describe the damage to them, they can assist with a replacement. please save the box and all packaging paper as a claim with ups may be required. i opened the item and played with it and didn’t like it. we want you to be 100% satisfied with your order!
