________________________________________________________________________________________ how to make a return or exchange it’s a snap! first, make sure you’ve read our returns & exchanges policy above. as long as your items are eligible for return or exchange, follow these easy steps for your region: free domestic returns - now online! 1. click start a return or exchange. from here, you’ll select your order, select your items, and print your return label. 2. place your item(s) in unworn condition and with tags attached, and the order invoice in a securely sealed shipping package. (if you misplaced your invoice, simply go to my account, click on your order history, select the right order number, and print it out.) 3. affix your printed return label to your package, and make sure there are no other shipping labels on it. jot down the tracking number* on the label so you can check on the delivery status. *your tracking number can be found in your account under return history. it’s the last 20 digits of the number listed below the barcode. 4.send it on its way! you can return your package through the us postal service or fedex. for us postal service: take your package to a us post office. find locations here. we do not recommend giving your package to a us postal carrier or leaving it in a usps collection box or in your residential mailbox. this often requires a significant amount of delivery time, which could cause additional delays in processing your return. for fedex: take your package to a fedex store. find locations here. we do not recommend leaving your package in a fedex drop box. this often requires a significant amount of delivery time, which could cause additional delays in processing your return. though fedex ground drivers can accept packages, using this method may also take a significant amount of additional time. we'll email you when we process your return! please allow 5-10 business days for package transit. international returns make sure you’ve read the returns & exchanges policy above. (please note that we currently do not offer international exchanges.) as long as your items are eligible for return and fall within the 60-day international returns window, follow these easy steps: 1. include your original invoice in your package. (if you misplaced it, simply go to my account, click on your order history, select the right order number, and print it out.) 2. on the front of your invoice, circle the names of the items you are returning and fill out the column on the right: specify that you are returning under return type. enter a reason code using the options listed under return reason codes. 3. place your invoice, along with the item(s) in unworn condition and with tags attached, in a securely sealed package. address your package to: modcloth returns 2250 roswell drive pittsburgh, pa 15205 4. send your package back to us via your shipping method* of choice. *be sure to use a method that provides a tracking number so you can follow its journey! we'll email you when we process your return! free domestic (us) exchanges - now online! you can exchange any returnable item for a different size, color, or pattern (but, we cannot process exchanges for completely different items). 1. click start a return or exchange. from here, you’ll select your order, select your items, select your new size/color/pattern, and print your return label. 2. place your item(s) in unworn condition and with tags attached, and the order invoice in a securely sealed shipping package. (if you misplaced your invoice, simply go to my account, click on your order history, select the right order number, and print it out.) 3. affix your printed return label to your package, and make sure there are no other shipping labels on it. jot down the tracking number* on the label so you can check on the delivery status. *your tracking number can be found in your account under return history. it’s the last 20 digits of the number listed below the barcode. 4.send it on its way!
