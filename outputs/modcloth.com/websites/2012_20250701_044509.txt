the buyer? affiliate sign up site feedback modcloth » help » shipping information shipping information what's new? for domestic (us) orders, in addition to our regular flat rate options, we now offer the new, lower cost (or free) savvy saver shipping! now, qualifying orders over $50 can get free shipping, while those under $50 can get a flat rate of $4. check out the details below. domestic (us) shipping rates & delivery options for contiguous us: we have four domestic (contiguous us states) flat rate shipping options: free-$4 savvy saver: delivered by usps, in partnership with fedex. transit time typically 4 to 9 business days, with delivery monday-saturday. free for orders over $50, or a $4 flat rate for orders under $50. if your order qualifies, this option will automatically show up in the checkout process. please allow 1 to 3 business days for processing prior to shipping. $8 ground: via fedex. transit time typically 2 to 5 business days, with delivery tuesday-saturday. please allow 1 to 3 business days for processing prior to shipping. + more info on fedex.com $14 second day air: via fedex. delivery typically in 2 to 3 business days, monday-friday*.+ more info on fedex.com $21 overnight: via fedex. typically next business day (monday-friday) delivery by morning or afternoon, depending on destination* + more info on fedex.com *all expedited (overnight and second day air) orders must be received by 10 a.m. est to ship that day. if received after 10 a.m., such orders will ship the following business day, monday-friday (excluding holidays), as fedex does not deliver expedited orders on weekends. for po boxes in contiguous us states, we have three flat rate shipping options: free-$4 savvy saver: delivered by usps, in partnership with fedex. transit time typically 10 to 14 business days, with delivery monday-saturday. free for orders over $50, or a $4 flat rate for orders under $50. if your order qualifies, this option will automatically show up in the checkout process. please allow 1 to 3 business days for processing prior to shipping. $8 ground: via usps. please allow 1 to 3 business days for processing prior to shipping. delivery typically in 2-5 business days. $14 second day air: via usps. delivery typically in 2-3 business days. for non-contiguous us: for alaska, hawaii, puerto rico, & the us virgin islands, we have three flat rate shipping options: free-$4 savvy saver: delivered by usps, in partnership with fedex. transit time typically 10 to 14 business days, with delivery monday-saturday. free for orders over $50, or a $4 flat rate for orders under $50. if your order qualifies, this option will automatically show up in the checkout process. please allow 1 to 3 business days for processing prior to shipping. $8 ground: via usps. please allow 1 to 3 business days for processing prior to shipping. delivery typically in 7 to 10 business days. $14 second day air: via usps. delivery typically in 2-3 business days. for apo/fpo addresses, we have one flat rate shipping option: $8 priority mail: via usps. please allow 1 to 3 business days for processing prior to shipping. delivery typically in 7 to 10 business days. do you ship
