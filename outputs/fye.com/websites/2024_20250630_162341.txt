we ship to the 50 states, military addresses (aa, ae, ap) and the following us territories: guam puerto rico palau us virgin islands at this time we cannot ship to other locations not listed above. we suggest people that do not live in the above locations send their orders to a friend or family member with an address in the 50 states. delivery delays we always attempt to ship orders as quickly as possible, but delays beyond our control sometimes occur. if an item you ordered becomes backordered or is expected to be delayed we will email you with an update, please contact customer service for any questions. weather, natural disasters, and other events beyond our control may affect shipping and delivery times. if you live in an area experiencing an unexpected service delay please add at least 2 to 3 business days to the estimated delivery date of your order. shipping carriers to provide you with the widest selection of products as well as the highest level of service, your items may
