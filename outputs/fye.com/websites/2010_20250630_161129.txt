fye: : help order status it is possible to check the status of your order by logging into "your account" on your store. please note that "open" indicates that your order has gone to the supplier for processing. please allow 3 - 5 business days from your order date for the status of your order to update. to check the status on your store log into "your account" and click the "continue" button to review your order. by clicking on your order, you can review a detailed list of each item ordered and its status. please also note that orders containing more than one item may be shipped from different warehouses and, therefore, arrive at different times.
