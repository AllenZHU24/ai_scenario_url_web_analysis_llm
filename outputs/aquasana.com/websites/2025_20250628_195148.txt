performance data sheet on the manufacturer’s website, there are a few key pieces to look for. the most important is a seal or badge from a testing agency. next to the seal will be certification and testing context, explaining what nsf standards the system was tested against.the second important piece of information in a performance data sheet is the actual performance of the system tested against the test agency’s parameters. each nsf standard the system is tested against should have its own section, showing the reduction percentage against a contaminant.performance data sheets can be difficult to understand if you’re not educated on the specific standards, charts, and exactly where to find them on a manufacturer’s website. for a complete breakdown, we explain in this article how range of criteria, including material safety, structural integrity, and contaminant reduction capabilities. for a water filter to earn certification, it must undergo thorough testing to demonstrate its ability to remove specific contaminants, such as lead, chlorine, bacteria, and other harmful substances, to levels deemed safe for consumption.water filter testing and certification are an
