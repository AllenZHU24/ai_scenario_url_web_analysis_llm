protect our customers billiards.com is committed to safeguarding your privacy as you use this website to gather information and/or order our products. by sharing our privacy policy, we hope to help you understand how information can create opportunities for our customers, as well as to reassure you that we respect and honor customer requests to restrict and protect personal information. non-personally identifiable information ("cookies") we collect non-personally identifiable information through the use of a software technology called "cookies." by non-personally identifiable information, we are referring to information about: our visitors' browsers (e.g. internet explorer, google chrome, apple safari, etc.) internet service providers (e.g. comcast-xfinity, verizon, spectrum, etc.) pages visited ip address date and time of access other similar information that we track in aggregate form. this information is used for site statistics or research to improve our sites behavior. the non-personally identifiable information we track is anonymous and is never sold or distributed to any party not working on our site. personally identifiable information we consider the following, among other things, to be personally identifiable information: first and last name, email address and billing information (e.g. credit card number, expiration date). how is personally identifiable information used? personally identifiable information is primarily used to offer better customer service and to improve communications. these uses include shipping and billing purposes and to deliver product information. this information is also used to enter visitors in sweepstakes and notify sweepstakes winners, and provide visitors with newsletters. additionally, this personally identifiable information may be used to provide these visitors, via e-mail or other means, information about materials and activities that may be of interest to them, including products or services of third parties. for these reasons, we may share such information with third parties. opting out of sharing information with third parties if you do not wish to receive such information or if you prefer not to have the personally identifiable information you submit shared with third parties, please let us know by either: sending an email to optout@billiards.com with the words "opt-out request" in the subject line or sending a letter to attn: opt-out request 1200 nw naito pkwy suite 180 portland, or 97209 please include your name, address, and phone number in the correspondence. after the two to three-week time period it takes to process your request, your information will no longer be shared. changes to this policy in the future if there is some significant change to this privacy policy that affects our use of your personally identifiable information, we will notify you of those changes on this site or via email. you will then be able to opt-out of this usage if you choose. you should also check this privacy policy from time to time for changes. site security the billiards.com website is protected with ssl/tls encryption (note the padlock next to the url), so the information you provide in an online form is also protected as it is transmitted to our servers. credit card data is transmitted directly to the card processor and not stored on our computers or servers. we will give out personal information as required by law, for example, to comply with a court order or subpoena or to assist in criminal investigations. we may also give out personal information when we deem it advisable in order to protect the safety and security of our sites and visitors to our sites. we maintain our website and associated data with technical, physical, and administrative safeguards to
