bowerman, track coach at the university of oregon, had a dream to create a better running shoe.  he famously used his wife's waffle iron to build the sole of shoe and the soul of a business that would go on to change the way we train, play and compete. one of the most important shoes has been the nike foamposite and the air force 1. since that day, with the help of phil knight, then a business air force 1, that have extended their popularity off the court, into music, community and cultural events, creating a cult of personality that is unlike any sportswear brand in the game.  through a strong understanding of the changing world of athletic wear and a talented team of designers they have been able to transform into a streetwear-style brand as well, producing graphic tees and bold hoodie sweatshirts that compete with the newest, hottest brands. combining the latest technology with cutting edge design, nike has been able to stay ahead of the competition, and maintain its place
