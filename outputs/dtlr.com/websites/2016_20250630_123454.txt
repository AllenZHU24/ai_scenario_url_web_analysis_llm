product. we ship monday through friday from our warehouse in new jersey and we ask our customers to allow us 1-3 business days to process and ship their order(s). we reserve the right to hold and confirm orders should we feel your identity is at risk and being used for fraudulent activity, we do this for your protection.  we do not ship on weekends or national holidays so please do allow for a long lead time on orders placed during those times. typical shipping times range from 4  to 8  business days via usps and ups. for security purposes, we require signature confirmation on all deliveries. ups policy prevents us from removing the signature confirmation. if you cannot sign for your parcel, you will be able to pick up your parcel at a local ups branch. becuase of this requirement, we do not ship to p.o boxes. *please note - customer orders with missing items are subject to ship, and customers will be refunded within 1-2 business days of their shipment for the missing item.* expected delivery dates are for orders placed by 12 pm est monday - friday. please allow an extra day for orders placed after 12 pm est or on weekends and holidays. express shipping we now offer next day shipping on orders with expedited shipping (ups 3 day, 2nd day and overnight). please note, there may be a delay in processing your order if it gets flagged by our system as a high risk order. at the time of completing your order, you will receive an email letting you know if your order has been flagged as high risk.  if you have chosen one of the expedited shipping methods listed above and your order does not ship out by the next business day please give us a call at 646-699-7801.  *priority will not be given to orders with free/ground shipping* shipping fees shipping package and you later notice that an item is damaged or defective you have two (2) business day to report your loss to our customer service team by phone or email. we will send you a free shipping
