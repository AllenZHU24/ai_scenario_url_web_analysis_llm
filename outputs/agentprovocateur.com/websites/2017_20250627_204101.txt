email. alternatively, you can log into your account to track your order delivery progress. how your order arrives all online orders are lovingly packed and dispatched in our beautiful pink branded box, tied with black ribbon. we dispatch orders in an anonymous and discreet box with your address label. nowhere does it state agent provocateur on the outer packaging. request to cancel or amend your order when your order is placed, our warehouse begin to pack it right away to ensure it reaches you in good time. this means that it is unfortunately not possible to add to, amend or cancel an order once it has been placed. your purchases can be returned for a refund or exchange within 14 days. if you have forgotten to purchase an item, you will need to place a secondary order. insurance all agent provocateur orders require a signature on receipt. if nobody is home to sign, your order will be returned to the carrier’s local depot and instructions will be left regarding redelivery. due to their size and weight, orders containing luggage will be charged a special shipping rate (£40 / $80 / €60) which will display at checkout. russia we deliver to most international destinations, however if you wish to ship russia, please visit the relevant website. for some international destinations we can only offer express delivery. bfpo, po box and apo/fpo unfortunately, we are unable to ship to bfpo, po box and apo/fpo addresses. import duties you may need to pay duties if you are based outside of the uk and eu. when your delivery reaches the country of destination, import duties and taxes are levied and payment of these are necessary to release your order from customs. we are unable to advise on rates as they vary according to country. please consult your local post office services for more information. perfumes perfumes cannot be shipped outside of the uk due to international shipping regulation. if you are shipping outside of the uk and your order contains a perfume,
