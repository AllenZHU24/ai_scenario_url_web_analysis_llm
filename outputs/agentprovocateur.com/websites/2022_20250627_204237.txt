your order's delivery progress.how your order arrivesorders are dispatched in a discreet outer box so you can shop in confidence knowing your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. our courier network requires us to include a return name / address & description of the contents which we have kept to a minimum where possible. inside, your order is packed and dispatched in a beautiful pink agent provocateur box, tied with a black satin ribbon. note that some products will arrive in a single discreet outer box with branding inside to ensure the item(s) arrive with you in the best condition. please note that unfortunately during the sale period, your order will not be packaged with a tied black ribbon. this enables us to get your order to you as quickly as possible. request to cancel or amend your orderonce your order is placed, the warehouse begins to pack it right away to ensure it reaches you in good time. this means that it is unfortunately not possible to add to, amend or cancel an order once it has been placed. your purchases can be returned for a refund or exchange within 14 days. if you have forgotten to purchase an item, you will need to place a second order.bfpo, po box and apo/fpounfortunately, we are unable to ship to bfpo, po box and apo/fpo addresses.import duties for eu customers, import taxes will be paid by agent provocateur on your behalf. carrier is necessary to release your order from customs. this may impact the speed of delivery of your order as customs processes take place. we are unable to advise on the length of time customs may take or the rates that may be charged, as they vary according to country. please consult your local postal service for more information.customs clearanceif your order is shipping outside of the uk or
