defective after the transfer is completed, you will need to contact the manufacturer of the firearm to repair or replace it. buying firearms on the internet guidelines and regulations: federal law stipulates that firearms may only be shipped from one ffl dealer to another one. as an individual, you either have to maintain a valid ffl dealer license or have your firearm shipped to a business, agency or an individual who is a holder of a valid ffl. you must also be 21 years of age or older at the time of the transaction if you are purchasing a handgun. if you are purchasing any other firearm you must be 18 years old at the time of transaction. by submitting your order you are formally acknowledging that you are familiar with your local regulations and you are of a legal age to
