petite women’s clothing for all tastes and occasions. following are some suggestions for petite women who want to make the most of their gorgeous pint sizes. keep it simple large prints, fluffy materials (such as chiffon), ruffles, large belts, heavy jewelry and other big design elements can overwhelm a petite frame. instead, opt for clean lines in most of your clothing and then choose one main accessory to avoid piling too much on a small body. dresses can make you look taller simple prints and colorful dresses that are knee length can add visual height. consider dark-hued straight-leg pants and jeans these can help visually elongate your legs and make you look much taller. select your patterns carefully of course, no one wants to wear exclusively monochromatic clothing. patterns and prints help create different looks and can extend your wardrobe. try to keep prints small so that people first notice you – and not what you’re wearing. also consider vertical lines which can elongate your body, rather than wearing patterns with horizontal lines which can make you look much shorter. think about necklines stylists recommend a v- or u-shaped neckline for petite women rather than a crew, boat or turtleneck. again, the v or u shape helps give the appearance of a longer figure. choose accessories wisely if oversize handbags are all the rage and you love them, by all means indulge in one or two. just note that larger bags tend to swamp a smaller frame. also consider small belts since larger ones can tend to create a divide between your top half and bottom half – essentially making you look much shorter. and a simple statement piece of jewelry goes much farther in flattering your outfit than a lot of jewelry. go for the heels if you’re a fan of heels, then absolutely wear them! however, attractive flats always make an outfit look sharp no matter what your size. you can find a wide range of petite women’s clothing and accessories made in the usa at
