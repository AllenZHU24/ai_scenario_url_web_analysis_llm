(packing slip) that came with your beallsflorida.com order to the guest service desk in any bealls florida store. you’ll receive credit to the original method of payment. if a bealls gift card is used, and the purchase order is greater than the amount on the bealls gift card, the total purchase will be applied to the debit/credit card used to offset the balance. if the purchase was a gift, the total refund can be applied to a bealls gift card if desired. please note that shipping fees are not refunded. use our store locator to locate a store near you. returns are not accepted at bealls
