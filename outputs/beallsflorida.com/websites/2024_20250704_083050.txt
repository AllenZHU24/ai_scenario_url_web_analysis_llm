over time the relevant portions of the world wide web consortium's web content accessibility guidelines 2.1 level aa (wcag 2.1 aa) as its web accessibility standard, which will also bring the website properties will be tested on a periodic basis with assistive technology such as screen readers and screen magnifiers, and with users with disabilities who use these technologies. we have engaged an experienced accessibility consultant, the national center for accessible media at wgbh (ncam), to assess the website’s and other consumer-facing properties’ accessibility and to provide beall’s with specific recommendations to facilitate website accessibility and conformance with wcag 2.1 aa standards. current status beallsflorida.com has been issued a voluntary product accessibility template for
