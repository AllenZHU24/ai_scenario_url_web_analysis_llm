great for both the bedroom or living room. electric heat is safer and requires less maintenance than other fuel types such as wood or charcoal.  with a fireplace heater, you can have a beautiful faux fire without the fuss of cleaning out a fireplace or worrying about burning embers. in addition to providing supplemental heat to your home, you're also adding a "vintage" look to your living space. for the price of a space heater, you're adding beautiful decor to your home that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing. when shopping for an electric fireplace, you'll notice that there are many different styles and models to choose from.  here's an overview of 3 of the most popular types: electric stoves: these electric fireplaces are the most compact and portable.  built to resemble old-fashioned stoves, electric fireplace stoves plug into any standard wall outlet and come in a variety of designs to complement any decor. mantel electric fireplaces: these full-sized models are designed to fully simulate traditional wood burning fireplaces.  most units will include a mantel as well as an insert that fits inside the mantel.  the insert is what produces the flames and heat. corner electric fireplaces: these are ideal for areas with limited space because they fit into the corner of a room.  corner electric fireplaces also come in a range of styles and require no venting. wall mount electric fireplaces: if you're looking to add ambiance without sacrificing floor space, consider a wall mount electric fireplace.  exact as the name implies, these hang onto your wall like a painting and are great for apartments, condos and other areas short on space. media console electric fireplaces: these units combine an entertainment center with an easy-to-use electric fireplace.
