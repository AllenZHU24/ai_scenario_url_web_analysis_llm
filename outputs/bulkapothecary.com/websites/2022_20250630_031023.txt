of all our private label products. we have over 300,000 square feet across our three facilities dedicated to providing the services you need. our minimums start at 10,000 pieces per item, and we have the capacity to fill orders of more than a million units for larger customers. some of our services include: filling of liquid materials ranging from 5ml to 1-gallon sizes molten filling for heavy hydroxide blister packing and other secondary packaging services fragrance duplication formula duplication (typically for larger orders and for customers that order 25,000 pieces or more) custom essential oil blends and this is just the start. we make it easy for your business to get everything it needs to package and sell a wide variety of products with your unique branding. ready-to-label
