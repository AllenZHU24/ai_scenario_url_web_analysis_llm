pricing ratings & reviews size conversion guide faqs contact us free shipping clarks proudly offers free fedex smartpost & ground shipping with no minimum (taxes, promotions and discounts excluded). free shipping offers do not apply to shipments made outside of the contiguous united states, including without limitation those made to hawaii and alaska. fedex smartpost often requires the united states postal service (usps) to make the final delivery. holiday shipping in order to guarantee delivery before december 24th, please reference the chart below: order by date & time (fedex only) shipping monday 12/16, 8:00 am est ground friday 12/20, 3:00 pm est two day monday 12/23, 3:00 pm est next day due to longer shipping times orders being shipped usps are not guaranteed arrival before december 24th p.o. boxes for delivery to a po box, simply select fedex smartpost as your shipping method. we are unable to ship fedex ground, next day or two day orders to a po box. military apo/fpo addresses (shipping fee $20.00) orders shipped via united states postal service priority mail. please allow additional time for delivery from the apo/fpo to the recipient. once the package has been delivered to the apo/fpo we are unable track the shipment. u.s. territories (shipping fee $20.00) orders will ship to american samoa, federated states of micronesia, guam, marshall islands, northern marianna islands, palau, puerto rico and the virgin islands via united states postal service priority mail. multiple packages if you ordered more than one item, your order may be shipped in multiple packages. most packages are received within 1-2 business days of each other. we are working diligently to provide access to all of your tracking numbers within your account, however they are not available at this time. gift cards we are unable to ship gift cards to a po box at this time. please make sure to select a physical address for delivery. saturday delivery we do not offer any saturday delivery options at this time. international we are unable to support the use of international billing or shipping addresses as we do not ship to any destination outside of the united states and its territories at this time. holiday closures our fulfillment center will be closed on the dates noted in the list below and will reopen on the following business day. as a result, all fedex next day and fedex 2nd day orders placed after 12:00pm est on the business day prior to our fulfillment center closing dates will not ship until the following business day. for example, if you place an order via fedex next day on friday at 2pm and we are closed the following monday, your order will ship on tuesday and will be received on wednesday. new years’ day easter sunday good friday memorial day
