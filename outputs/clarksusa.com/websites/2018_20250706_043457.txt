continental united states shipping methods ups surepost • cost: free on orders over $50 ($6 for orders < $49.99) • estimated delivery: 5-8 business days after shipment notification ups ground • cost: $6.00 • estimated delivery: 3-6 business days after shipment notification ups two day • cost: $16.00 • estimated delivery: 2 business days for orders before 12:00pm est, m-f | 3 business days for orders after 12:00pm est, m-f ups next day • cost: $26.00 • estimated delivery: 1 business days for orders before 12:00pm est, m-f | 2 business days for orders after 12:00pm est, m-f alaska/hawaii ups two day • cost: $21.00 • estimated delivery: 2 business days for orders before 12:00pm est, m-f | 3 business days for orders after 12:00pm est, m-f free shipping clarks proudly offers free ups surepost shipping on orders of $50 or more (taxes, promotions and discounts excluded). ups surepost often requires the united states postal service (usps) to make the final delivery. p.o. boxes for delivery to a po box, simply select ups surepost as your shipping method. we are unable to ship ups ground, next day or two day orders to a po box. military apo/fpo addresses (shipping fee $20.00) orders shipped via united states postal service priority mail. please allow additional time for delivery from the apo/fpo to the recipient. once the package has been delivered to the apo/fpo we are unable track the shipment. u.s. territories (shipping fee $20.00) orders will ship to american samoa, federated states of micronesia, guam, marshall islands, northern marianna islands, palau, puerto rico and the virgin islands via united states postal service priority mail. multiple packages if you ordered more than one item, your order may be shipped in multiple packages. most packages are received within 1-2 business days of each other. we are working diligently to provide access to all of your tracking numbers within your account, however they are not available at this time. saturday delivery we do not offer any saturday delivery options at this time. international we are unable to support the use of international billing or shipping addresses as we do not ship to any destination outside of the united states and its territories at this time. holiday closures our fulfillment center will be closed on the dates noted in the list below and will reopen on the following business day. as a result, all ups next day and ups 2nd day orders placed after 12:00pm est on the business day prior to our fulfillment center closing dates will not ship until the following business day. for example, if you place an order via ups next day on friday at 2pm and we are closed the following monday, your order will ship on tuesday and will be received on wednesday. new years’ day easter sunday
