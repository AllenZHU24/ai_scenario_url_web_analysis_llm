general merchandise product returns will only be accepted on clean, unused merchandise. · replacement orders depend on inventory availability during the current season.  products that are out of stock zone/climate that is conflicting to what burpee recommends. how to return your catalog & online order: if you are dissatisfied with products purchased online or through our catalog, you may return questions) you may return your order back to us via usps or by ups; however, we recommend getting a delivery confirmation or tracking number for your shipment as we are not responsible for lost returns. returns by mail generally take 7-14 days to be processed & refunded.  once a return is processed, a refund will be processed back to the original payment method used or a refund check will be issued. items purchased at a local retailer-how to return: if you are unsatisfied with burpee seed packets or general merchandise items that were purchased from one of our partner retail store
