orders are processed within 3-5 business days and shipped via usps (standard delivery: 3-14 business days). we process orders monday – friday only. large orders may ship via ups. please note that ups cannot deliver to po boxes. all plant orders ship via ups except for sweet potatoes and onion plants (not sets). these ship via usps at the optimal planting time for your area. orders may arrive in separate shipments as they are fulfilled from multiple locations. orders cannot be cancelled or refunded once processed for shipment. some general merchandise items may take 7-14 days to process and
