shipping cost will be reimbursed or a return label will be provided (see below for return procedure for details). items must be returned with the original packaging and in original condition. any items return to us without the original packaging and/or in bad condition, will be refused and returned to sender. we do not accept back items in worn or used conditions. any indications on the product which shows signs of usage (ie: perfume smell, deodorant marks, dirtied soles) will be refused and sent back to sender. no exceptions will be made. important, please note:• due to health restrictions and sanitary reasons, we do not resell swimsuits, intimate/ intimate apparel, hosiery and lingerie items. these items will be final sale (no return, no exchanges, all sales are final).•
