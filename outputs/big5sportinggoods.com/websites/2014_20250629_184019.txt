sporting goods we offer incentive programs for teams/leagues to use as a way to save, and get more sport for your buck. more people, playing more sports, without breaking the bank is our goal. 1.) cash back league rebate program details: 5% cash back on all purchases including non-sport related purchases * the big 5 league rebate program can benefit your organization as a proven fundraising vehicle. when you participate, every purchase by your members and supporters will generate cash for your organization! how does it work? complete a simple enrollment request form. notify your members and distribute rebate flyers. the bigger the turnout, the larger the benefit receive a check from big 5 to your organization. you request a rebate earning period (no longer than 10-days, covering 2-weekends) by completing an enrollment request form. we provide you with a personalized rebate flyer (sample) that you reproduce and distribute to as many members and supporters as possible. during your rebate period, you raise funds by accumulating 5% rebates on purchases of any merchandise at big 5 sporting goods. purchases are captured and credited to your group each time a rebate flyer is presented. at the end of the program, a check will be issued for 5% of your total sales (excluding sales tax). in addition: following each sales transaction, your supporters are rewarded with a special percentage off reward coupon (sample) that may be applied to a future purchase at any of our stores. everybody wins! please note: the rebate flyer is not to be distributed to customers in front of, or in our stores. the flyer cannot be applied to quantity discount pricing for league purchases of equipment and clothing.
