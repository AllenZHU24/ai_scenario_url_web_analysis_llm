order multiple products » about us agri supply® is a family owned group of farm stores that started back in 1962 in garner, north carolina. now, 50 years later, agri supply has retail locations in north carolina, south carolina, georgia, and virginia; we release several unique catalogs per year; and we have a thriving website with a huge product selection available. the agri supply product line has grown by leaps and bounds - from what began as only farm items, to what now tops over 26,000 items and counting, featuring many categories: ag & tillage - covering all of your needs for farm machinery parts and implements, hydraulics, pto shafts and accessories, sprayers pumps and parts, tobacco transplant/harvest, tillage, and of course, tractor accessories. construction - construction and surveyor supplies from burlap to cement mix, safety flags to coolers, silt fence to drip torches... almost any of your needs, as well as both aluminum & steel roofing. cooking - find great cooking supplies such as burners, dutch ovens, griddles, grills and grill covers, cookers, seasonings and sauces, pots and pot sets, meat saws, cookbooks, an extensive selection of cookware including enamel ware and silicone ware, woks and other kitchen accessories. fasteners - we offer a wide variety of fasteners including carriage bolts, hinges, nails, locks, braces, brackets, hooks, washers, nuts and screws. home, lawn & garden - you'll find cleaning supplies, a huge selection of clothing including belts & boots, homeowner supplies, almost every lawn & garden supply you can imagine, lawnmower parts, paint and painter supplies, safety supplies and equipment, and house & yard signs. livestock & pet - for the people who care for animals, we have the supplies you need to treat your animals from buckets, baskets & scoops, to a huge selection of fencing and fencing supplies, an endless stream of livestock supplies and accessories, and almost every pet supply you or your pet could need. oils & greases - we have many different lubricants including products such as belt dressings, fuel treatments, and of course... grease - as well as the accessories you need to use them all. plumbing & electrical - the plumber and electricians friend, we have pipes & supplies such as drain fittings, metal, plastic and polyethylene pipe, copper tubing, washing machine hose, and the plumbing supplies you need to go with them and the other plumbing jobs you have to tackle... and don't forget our electrical supplies such as circuit breakers, generators, heat lamps, outlet boxes, plugs, pulleys, testers, wiring and much more. shop & tool - if it belongs in your shop, you can find it with us - we've got: air compressors & fittings, pressure washers, shop supplies like bottle jacks, creepers, hand trucks, jack stands, vises, tape, glue and more - and we have a large selection of hand & power tools, and welding supplies. sporting goods - if you are in need of boating & marine supplies such as bilge pumps, fishing chairs, coolers, fire extinguishers, gigs and rod holders, or sporting goods such as atv covers, flashlights, game seasonings, camping supplies, outdoor furniture, deer feeders, insect repellents, or more... come see us. tarps & covers - we carry grommets, s-hooks, straps, bungee cords, tarps, quick shades and shelters. take a look at our tarps & covers section. trailers & trailer parts - we have a huge supply of parts for trailers. from brake controllers to fenders, hub assemblies to reflectors, switches & flashers to trailer springs, and wheels to actual trailers and trailer kits, we’ve got you covered. to repair/upgrade your trailer, visit our trailers & trailer parts section today. trucking, towing & automotive - we stock everything from automotive & batteries, anti-freeze, brake fluid, funnels, tubes and wd-40, to rope, cable & chain, cable cutters, pulleys & rings, spring snaps and clevis grab hooks. and don't forget our extensive selection of truck accessories covering your truck inside and out, front to back, and everywhere in between with all the covers, protectors, boxes, racks, mats, guards, steps and shields you could ever need. our product line has expanded to cover your needs, and will continue to do so - no matter whether you are on the farm needing that part for your tractor, in the shop looking for that perfect power tool, in the home preparing a scrumptious dinner, or in the garden trying to teach those weeds a lesson! whether it is for your farm, shop or home, agri supply has you covered! in fact, we have... something for everyone! since 1962, customers have always been number one, and we have kept the same mission to this day: agri supply's goal is to provide quality merchandise at the most reasonable price, to
