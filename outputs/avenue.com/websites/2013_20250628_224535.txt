a new order on our website and return the wrong item using the instructions on the back of the packing slip. or call us toll-free at (888) 843-2836. we will issue a credit to the original credit card for the wrong item and charge your card for the correct item when it has shipped. what do i do if an item is missing from my order? if an item is missing from your package, but listed on the packing
