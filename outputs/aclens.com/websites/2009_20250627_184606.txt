accessories contact lens cases glasses lens & eye care products reading glasses sunglasses swimming goggles how to place an order: step 1: find your contact lenses there are five ways in which you can select your lenses: enter your lens type into the search box on the top left of your screen select your lens type from the drop down box in the left margin select the manufacturer of your lenses from the drop down box in the left margin and then choose your lens type from the proceeding page select a lens category from the drop down box in the left margin and then choose your lens type from the proceeding page. click the “order contacts” button at the top of the page and then choose your lens type from the proceeding page step 2: place your order once you have found your lenses, follow these directions to complete your order. enter your prescription information including sphere power, base curve, diameter, etc. and enter the quantity of lenses desired. click on the “add to cart” button double check the information in your shopping cart to ensure that it is correct click the “checkout” button - you will be taken to our online order form, which is secured by verisign. choose the shipping method that you would like for your lenses from the drop down box provided enter your name, address, telephone number, etc. on the appropriate lines. check the "billing address is the same" box if your credit card billing address is the same as the information you entered for your shipping address. when finished entering all the required fields (those in bold lettering), click the “continue” button enter your billing information if it is different than your shipping information. enter your payment information (credit card number, expiration, etc.) in the spaces provided. also, enter any special comments or instructions in the box provided. we accept visa, mastercard, american express, and discover. we also accept checks and money orders, but we cannot ship your order until after we have receive your check and it has cleared your bank. click the “complete order” button, and print a copy of your receipt, if desired. you can return to our web site to track your order by following a link that you will receive in your email or by following the "track order" link on the left sidebar. you can also fax your order to ac lens by using the printable fax form. if you are having any unexplained eye discomfort, watering, vision change or redness, remove your lenses immediately and consult your eye care professional before wearing your lenses again. all information,
