outside of the united states orders are shipped either by international express mail or by global priority mail. global priority mail - £9.95 international express - £16.95 additional international shipping information: international express is generally significantly faster. delivery times by both methods vary widely between countries and often depend to a large extent on local import/customs processing. with some exceptions your lenses can generally be expected to arrive within 1-4 weeks. global priority mail is slower than global express with delivery times that generally fall between 2 and 4 weeks, depending on the destination country and the time required for the package to clear customs in the destination country. duties, sales or value added taxes please note that some governments (especially in europe and asia) collect sales or value added taxes (vat) on contact lenses and other optical items.  such duties and/or taxes are not included in the purchase price of your order. it is the your responsibility to pay any such taxes as required by local statute at the point of delivery. package loss and fraud ac lens monitors package loss and credit card fraud rates for different countries and regions on an ongoing basis. on occasion, we find it necessary to require additional information prior to filling an order and/or decline to fill an order. the company reserves the right to decline to fill any order to any country or region at its sole discretion. *shipping and handling charges include actual shipping charges, as well as order processing, labor and materials
