comments or instructions in the box provided. when finished, click the "continue" button enter the name of the patient and the doctor for each set of lenses in your cart. when finished, click the mastercard, american express, and discover. we also accept checks and money orders, but we cannot ship your order until after we have receive your check and it has cleared your bank. click the
