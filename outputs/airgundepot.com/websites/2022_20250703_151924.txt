home // customer service // about usabout usairgun depot opened in 2002, when founders scott thomas and todd holmberg opened the airgun depot website, selling a selection of quality pellet and bb guns. our goal was to provide airgunners a great resource to view and purchase a much broader selection of high-end airguns that were not available in "brick and mortar" sportsman's stores. by providing exceptional customer service along with an expanding line of products, airgun depot has grown to become one of the largest volume dealers for airguns, airsoft, paintball, blank guns, crossbows, and shooting accessories in the us. we are headquartered in a 10,000 square foot facility in draper, utah, with an alternate warehouse located in ohio for orders shipping to the east coast. as a small company, we are able to provide the type of personalized service, selection, and low prices you deserve. airgundepot.com is constantly updated, utilizing technological advancements to make
