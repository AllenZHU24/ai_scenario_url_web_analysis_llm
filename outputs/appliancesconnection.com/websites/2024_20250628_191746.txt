as refrigerators, freezers, dishwashers, ranges, cooktops, wall ovens, range hoods, and over-the-range microwaves. looking for appliance find a refrigerator that meets your daily culinary needs. fridges are available in the top freezer, bottom freezer, side-by-side, drawer, all refrigerator, and french door configurations. they can be as slim as 23" in width and stretch up to 48" in width. built-in refrigerators can be integrated into the cabinetry. some built-in fridges are panel ready to streamline with cabinetry. for more flexible placement, freestanding refrigerators can be installed almost anywhere for easy accessibility. most deliver an impeccable cooling system without harming the environment, as they are energy rated. create delicious meals with an assortment of cooktops and rangetops. they are available in gas, electric, and induction fuel types. sizes vary from a compact 24" up to 48" in width. gas cooktops and gas rangetops get the heat started with an open flame using natural gas and liquid propane. most feature continuous cast-iron grates to easily slide heavy pots and pans from one burner to the next. the smooth top surface of electric cooktops and induction cooktops will be effortless to clean. these flameless cooktops also provide a safe cooking surface with hot surface indicators that illuminate when zones are too hot to touch. from succulent roasts to decadent desserts, wall ovens are equipped to cook almost anything you desire. single-wall ovens and double-wall ovens are available in widths ranging from 24" up to 36" to match other built-in appliances. some models go beyond operating as an oven only. single wall ovens can function as speed ovens to quickly prepare dishes, along with steam oven models that enhance foods with moisture. select double wall ovens are a microwave/oven combo, allowing you to melt butter in the microwave to brush onto pastries before baking them in the oven. ranges combine a cooktop and an oven in one appliance. ranges are available in gas, electric, and dual fuel types. gas ranges and dual fuel ranges can feature up to 8 burners, with an assortment of sealed and open burners. electric ranges and induction ranges can include up to 6 elements which will make it easy to prepare a multi-course meal. many ovens feature a convection heating system so foods evenly cook. you won't even have to worry about abrupt smoke from culinary creations. the air can easily be cleared with corresponding range hoods and over-the-range microwaves. after preparing a festive dinner, aftermath clean-ups will be the least of your worry. simply toss dirty dishes into the dishwasher, select your cleaning cycle, and let it work its magic. dishwashers are available in a smaller 18" width and a standard 24" width. built-in dishwashers can simply be installed undercounter for easy access. dishwasher drawers slide out and slide in as an alternative design to the drop-down door of standard dishwashers. energy star-rated dishwashers not only ensure cookware and plates come out sparkling clean but also help save the planet by leaving a small carbon footprint. many appliance packages are available in a classic stainless steel and more luxurious black stainless steel finishes for a premium kitchen look. wi-fi-connected appliances can be connected to a smart home and be remotely
