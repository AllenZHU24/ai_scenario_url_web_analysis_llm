sale beading components shop beadaholique's wide array of jewelry-making findings, including bracelet, earring and ring findings, jump rings, clasps, head pins and eye pins, crimps, bails, buttons and more. components and findings are the nuts and bolts of any jewelry piece, the building blocks from which handmade designs are created. in this category you'll find foundational pieces for many types of projects. ring findings come in a variety of sizes, bracelet bases are available in bangle, charm, pandora and cuff styles, and earring options include bezels, hooks, chandelier pieces and hoops. show more in addition, this category is where you'll find some of the most universal jewelry staples, such as jump rings, clasps and crimp beads. these items not only hold your jewelry pieces together, but they can also add unexpected style elements to the overall look. make sure to peruse the different finishes, shapes and styles of these ubiquitous components—especially our curated selection of magnetic, multi-strand, toggle and box clasps, some of which are decorated with filigree, pearls and crystals. other elements in this section include head pins and eye pins, which allow you to dangle beads or charms from larger components, and rivets and eyelets, which help you connect metal and leather pieces. pendant bails, cord ends and chain extenders round out this group of
