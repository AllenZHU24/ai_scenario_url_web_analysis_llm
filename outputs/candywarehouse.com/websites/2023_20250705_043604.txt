have certain items in stock. shipping cost shipping charges are calculated using fedex and usps shipping tables and are based on package weight and size, delivery distance, and shipping method. shipping costs approximately double for every upgrade (e.g., fedex overnight will cost roughly twice that of fedex 2 day). unfortunately, weight plays a significant factor in determining shipping costs and bulk candy has a high shipping-to-product-cost ratio. for example: if you purchase a diamond for $1,000, shipping is likely going to be approximately $20, (2% of the diamond price). on the other hand, if you purchase $1,000 worth of candy, your shipping cost will likely be $200 (20% of the cost of the candy). if you order $15 of candy, the shipping cost may be as high as $15 (100% of the cost of the candy). products such as jewelry, cds, electronics, clothing, and flowers are light; therefore, their shipping costs will not represent a large percentage of their total order cost.
