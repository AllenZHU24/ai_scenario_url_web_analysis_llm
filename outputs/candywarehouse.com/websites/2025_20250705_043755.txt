remote bases. if you are shipping this way, we recommend placing your order by phone at 310-343-4099. freight collect: if you or your company has its own fedex account, you may choose to have fedex candywarehouse does not know how much your freight account will charge you to ship our products so please inquire with your freight carrier directly for such information. if you would like to ship via fedex or another carrier, you may create your own shipping labels and have the order picked up from our warehouse. please keep in mind the following: no matter which shipping method is selected, we require between 2-4 business days to process your order (if you select fedex overnight, you may not receive your product the day after you place the order). an order must be received by 12:00 p.m. right to cancel or modify orders depending on availability. because we carry over 6,000 products and keep a limited inventory (so as to keep our inventory as fresh as possible), we may at times not have certain items in stock. **our shipping carrier is chosen dynamically between fedex and ups, depending on which offers the best transit time and lowest cost for your order.** shipping cost shipping charges are calculated using fedex, ups, and usps shipping tables and are based on package weight and size, delivery distance, and shipping method. shipping costs approximately double for every upgrade (e.g., overnight shipping will cost roughly twice that of¬†2 day shipping). unfortunately, weight plays a significant factor in determining shipping costs and bulk candy has a high shipping-to-product-cost ratio. for example: if you purchase a diamond for $1,000, shipping is likely going to be approximately $20, (2% of the diamond price). on the other hand, if you purchase $1,000 worth of candy, your shipping cost will likely be $200 (20% of the cost of the candy). if you order $15 of candy, the shipping cost may be as high as $15 (100% of the cost of the candy). products such as jewelry, cds, electronics, clothing, and flowers are light; therefore, their shipping costs will not represent a large percentage of their total order cost. sweets, for better or for worse, outweigh the competition! shipping promotions please note that any candywarehouse shipping promotions/discounts are not applicable to items listed as 'ships fresh directly from factory' and such promos are specific to candywarehouse items only. those products which 'ship fresh directly from factory' will incur their own delivery fees which are set by the respective manufacturers. address
