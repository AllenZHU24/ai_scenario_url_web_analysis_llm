our roots™. our saplings stay within the appalachian region of the u.s., where our materials are sourced, and where our furniture is then built and sold. 220,450 trees planted and counting 5,000the total number of trees planted in 2016, the year we began our pledge 215,450the total number of trees planted between 2017 and 2025 32,000trees planted in 2023, our biggest annual number to date. 20,000the amount of trees we’re committing to replanting in 2025 our mission is to make high-quality goods that are built to last - but we’re committed to caring for the environment in the process. here’s how we’re doing exactly that through returning to our roots™. we plant purposefully this is all about giving back what we take away, so for every bench made style we sell, we plant two trees in its place. we plant responsibly we use a variety of saplings to create a healthy and diverse environment that feels as natural as possible. we plant together our team members participate in the planting each year as a way for us to work together towards ﻿a greater good. environmental initiative no. 2 material world we’re mindful of the materials we use, and we’re committed to recycling as many of them as we possibly can. that includes everything from packing products to wood dust to leftover fabric scraps. here’s just a snapshot of the impact we’ve made thus far. 1,770 tons of
