you. as a custom furniture manufacturer, we work diligently to ensure that every purchase brings a positive experience. it’s also important for you to know what post-purchase options are available to you regarding returns and cancellations, especially because much of our product is customized by you. online returns online returns are accepted for bed linens, cast + forge hardware, and finish samples get started with online returns products custom productsbassett does not accept cancellations or changes on any custom-ordered product after 24 hours of the order being placed. we also cannot accept returns on any custom-ordered/ “made to order” product. all custom items will be identified as such on the sales order.custom rugs can only be canceled within 24 hours of the order being placed, and are otherwise not eligible for return.non-custom productsfor any non-custom items, bassett will accept cancellation prior to delivery with a 15% restocking fee. this fee is 15% of the total sale price of the merchandise, excluding tax and delivery.non-custom rugs can be returned with a 15% restocking fee within 30 days of receipt of merchandise.stocked non-furniture itemsall stocked, non-custom accessories can be returned within 30 days with proof of purchase. the product must be in new and unused condition.some states have laws that differ from the above policies, please see your local bassett store/retailer for details.clearance and flash sale items are final sale and cannot be canceled or returned. quantities are limited and no price adjustments will be made on prior sales.benchmade finish samplesundamaged finish samples may be returned for a full refund within 90 days of purchase. finishes must be returned in the original packaging. once returned, you'll receive a full refund via the original purchase method.non-benchmade finish samplesthese finish samples are non-returnable and non-refundable. mattress return policy we want to make sure you are 100% mattress for a full refund. if you decide that the jasmine mattress is not right for you and don’t want to try another jasmine comfort option, notify us within the trial period that you want to arrange a return. once we receive the return of the original mattress, we’ll issue a full refund. learn more about our mattress sleep trial and warranty. return authorization some accessory categories
