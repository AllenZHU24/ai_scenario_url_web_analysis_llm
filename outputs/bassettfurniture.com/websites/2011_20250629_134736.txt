alliance enhancing furniture's environmental culture program efec (enhancing furniture’s environmental culture) is a voluntary environmental management system (ems) for furniture manufacturers. efec was created with the purpose of maintaining and furthering the industry’s strong environmental performance. efec is a set of management tools and principles to integrate environmental concerns into daily business practices. efec provides a systematic approach to review and improve operations for better environmental performance and improved profitability. benefits are obtained by setting up procedures to get the work done and reviewing these procedures to ensure they are working properly. company informationstore locator careersinvestorsquality without compromise upholstery warranty wood
