energy consumption. through the education and involvement of our associates, business partners, and communities, we will continue to see new and innovative ways to sustain and enrich the environment. we view this endeavor as good business practice, and our responsibility to future generations. our progress so far... warehouse bassett, va. partnership between bassett employees and zenith transportation to create and implement a new waste management program within the facility. reduced waste disposal costs by 49% in first 6 months of recycling. eliminated the need for trash dumpster and waste yard box as a result of recycling program. table plant martinsville, va. built two loading docks for easy transfer of recyclables from the manufacturing floor to trailers. partnership with warehouse in bassett to implement a new foam recycling program. recycled 217 tons of cardboard, 4,400 lbs of paper, and 22,700 lbs of plastics to date. lighting was replaced in half of the factory with energy efficient bulbs. main office bassett, va. developed methods to encourage employee suggestions and participation. recycled 150 tons of office related materials to date. analyzing data and identifying ways to reduce daily energy consumption. upholstery plant newton, n.c. reduced kilowatt hours used in 2012 by 4.5% from 2009 through employee education on energy awareness. also, office windows were replaced with new energy efficient windows. revised our furniture pack for all furniture manufactured in newton, nc. packaging materials now can be recycled instead of sent to area landfills. reduced landfill tons by 20% since 2009. recycled over 1.3 million pounds. all lighting in both plants and office will be replaced with energy efficient t8 bulbs within the first quarter of 2012. this will reduce kilowatts used and carbon dioxide emissions. this will increase the lighting which will improve quality and morale. efec (enhancing furniture’s environmental culture) is a voluntary environmental management system (ems) for furniture manufacturers. efec was created with the purpose of maintaining and furthering the industry’s strong environmental performance. efec is a set of management tools and principles to integrate environmental concerns into daily business practices. efec provides a systematic approach to review and improve operations for better environmental performance and improved profitability. benefits are obtained by setting up procedures to get the work done and reviewing these procedures to ensure they are working properly. now open! coming soon be
