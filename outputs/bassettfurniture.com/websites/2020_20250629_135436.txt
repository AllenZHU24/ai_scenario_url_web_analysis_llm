perfect space. book an appointment visit a local store set your patio to relax mode with outdoor chaise lounges from bassett make your backyard feel as luxurious as a resort-style spa by adding a set of patio chaise lounges from bassett furniture. an outdoor chaise lounge is designed to sit low and provide support for the entire length of your body, allowing you to stretch out and relax in the most comfortable positions. backyard lounge chairs are perfect for catching rays out by the pool or sprawling out beneath a pergola out on your beautiful patio. is there a better way to spend a summer afternoon than leaning back with a great book and a refreshing beverage? find a great selection of outdoor chaise lounges in bassett furniture's extraordinary outdoor furniture collection. patio chaise lounges are made for comfort if you're looking to create a peaceful oasis to escape the stresses and noisy intrusions of everyday life, you should definitely include lounging patio chairs in your design planning. every aspect of these chairs caters to a relaxed, hassle-free atmosphere. a multi-positional reclining back allows you to freely adjust your incline angle, easily going from sitting upright to laying flat, or any preferred position in between. the chair's cushions provide a soft, pleasant layer of support for your neck, torso, and legs. many patio lounge chairs also come with armrests, which come in handy when you're leafing through magazines, texting your friends on your phone, or just sitting back taking in the view. there's no doubt about it, lounging patio chairs are made for leisure time. choose your preferred lawn lounge chair frame material it's easy to match your lounges to your existing backyard decor. start by choosing the frame material that will compliment your outdoor furniture. at bassett, we rely on high-performance materials that look great, last a long time and deliver the comfort you expect. rest assured, no matter which frame material you choose, you'll end up with lawn lounge chairs of the highest quality. wicker like the look of wicker? if you have a deck full of wicker furniture, there's no reason not to add a few wicker chaise lounges to your collection. bassett's wicker furniture is made from high-performance wicker resin, which stands up reliably to all the outdoor elements, like moisture, heat and direct sunlight. wood wood is probably the best furniture material at regulating temperature, which could be a good thing if your patio chairs are parked in direct sunlight for much of the day. wood never gets too hot during the summer heat, and it never gets too cold during the cooler seasons. at bassett, our wood chaise lounges are made from teak, one of the most elegant and durable hardwood options. metal our metal chaise lounges are made from high strength aluminum. thanks to a thin protective top layer, aluminum is resistant to rust and moisture. in addition to offering great durability, aluminum assures that your patio chairs can remain outside all year round. customize your lounge deck chairs now that you have your material picked out, get busy customizing your backyard lounge chairs to reflect your
