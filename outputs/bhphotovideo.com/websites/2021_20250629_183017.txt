team with all the relevant details via chat, email, or by calling 800.606.6969/212.444.6615 during business hours. if we are able to match the price, we will email you a link to make your purchase. this link will be valid through the end of the business day. note: if approved, one price match per identical item per customer is allowed. price adjustment on prior purchase if the price of an item
