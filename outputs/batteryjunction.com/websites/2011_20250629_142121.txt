chargerschoosing the right charger is important. the three basic types of chargers are trickle, timer, and smart chargers. trickle chargers give batteries a constant low charge, they're compact and inexpensive. timer based chargers are the next step up, as they are faster; however, they will turn off after set amount of time whether or not batteries are fully charged. smart chargers are the best type: they detect when cells are fully charged and will automatically switch to trickle mode. universal chargers can charge aa, aaa, c, d and 9v batteries; universal chargers can be smart or timer
