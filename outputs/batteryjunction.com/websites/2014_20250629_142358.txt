we happily ship to po boxes, apo, and fpo addresses. when shipping to po box, you must select usps first class, priority, or express mail service. when shipping to an apo or fpo address, you must processing.our domestic shipping services are: super saver: the best option for many orders. order will ship ups ground or usps first class or priority, at our discretion. must be deliverable to a best option for orders shipping to apo/fpo, po boxes, ak, hi, pr and guam. usps express mail: usps express mail is available for all us and protectorate addresses. in-transit time is 1-2 days. orders must be placed before 11:00am eastern time (est) to ship same business day. ups ground: 1-5 days in transit. superb track-ability and reliability, fixed in-transit times. ups 3 day select: guaranteed
