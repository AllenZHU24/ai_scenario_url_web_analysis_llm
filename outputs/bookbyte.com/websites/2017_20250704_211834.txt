a textbook’s condition can affect how much it’s worth. good books with minor signs of wear and tear: such as staining, discoloration, writing, highlighting, markings, tears, and other general damage from use. all pages included and all text legible. you will receive the whole quoted amount. acceptable books with excessive writing, highlighting, and other markings; cover damage; torn pages; odor; or severe staining. all pages included and all text legible. you will receive half the quoted amount. poor books with missing pages or illegible text. we do not accept books in this condition. they will be recycled if sent. alternate editions bookbyte buys alternate edition textbooks, but you should make sure to enter the correct isbn for the alternate edition you’re sending us. alternate editions have the same title but different isbns and therefore different buyback values (which can be a shock if you’re expecting hundreds, but you get offered less). these include all types of international editions (global edition, new pearson international, asian pacific edition, etc.). these editions require all supplemental materials (cds, access codes, etc.) to be included. be sure to enter the correct isbn to receive an accurate quote! where do i find the isbn?
