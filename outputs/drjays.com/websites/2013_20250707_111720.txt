shipping, please consider delivery to an alternate location in the continental u.s. standard shipping — low cost-effective delivery for packages under 70 lbs. typically 2 to 7 business days within the contiguous u.s. for delivery. standard shipping is available to all u.s. addresses, including p.o. boxes. this carrier picks up your drjays.com package from our fulfillment facility and passes the package on to the u.s. post office (usps) for final mile delivery. * for us customers - fragrances are not eligible for express delivery. please allow 2-7 business days for delivery to the us and 8-12 higher than our standard rates. orders meeting these provisions, will be held, and you will be contacted for approval on additional shipping charges before releasing your order for shipment.
