log in and click "order history" to check the status of an order. what does carbonfree shipping mean? every product we deliver is shipped carbon-free from our door to yours. by using carbon-free shipping we're able to offset unavoidable emissions, support renewable energy, and achieve our goal of a minimal impact in shipping. how are bambeco products green? bambeco's products meet strict guidelines for sustainability in every way. materials used in the manufacturing of products are all sustainably obtained and organically grown, recycled, recyclable, reclaimed, repurposed, natural, and non-toxic. our manufacturing processes use clean, renewable energy, recycle materials, and utilize water reclamation whenever possible. we are constantly working with our suppliers to further reduce our carbon footprint and find innovative ways to make new products more sustainably. finally, all of the workers who make our products are paid a fair wage and provided with a safe, healthy working environment. what do bambeco's acronyms mean? on many of our products you will see acronyms related to certifications from 3rd party agencies. they include: gots - global organic textile standard. this is a certification managed by the international working group on global organic textile standards and it provides credible, meaningful assurances to customers that our textiles are made using organic fibers and that our productions processes are environmentally responsible, from harvest to labeling. skal - this is a certification for organic products from the netherlands. oeko-tex - this is an international testing and certification system for textiles that focuses on limiting the use of toxic chemicals and dyes in the textile industry. the certification body tests for more than 100 harmful substances and is continually adding to their list. all of our oeko-tex certified products use non-toxic processes and dyes that protect you and the environment. does bambeco comply with the federal trade commission's green guides? of course! we will never overstate the claims about our products and whenever possible we will provide background information and 3rd party certifications that verify our sustainability claims. will bambeco sell my information to other companies? your privacy is important to us so we want to make sure you're informed. on occasion, we may share mailing information with carefully selected companies. to opt out of having your information shared please contact our brand advocates at 1.866.535.4144. is my credit card information secure? bambeco uses encryption technology provided by verisign's secure sockets layer to transmit your information. to verify that your connection is safe, look for the closed lock icon in your browser window. does my bambeco gift card expire? no! the value of your bambeco gift card never expires. can i exchange my bambeco gift card for cash? we cannot exchange gift cards for cash. do i have to pay sales tax? only orders shipped to addresses within the state of maryland pay sales tax. can i exchange a gift? yes, items in their original condition can be exchanged. please contact our brand advocates for more
