protect the security and privacy of your personal information. when you enter your personal information on our websites, we use verisign secure sockets layer (ssl) to protect your information as it is transmitted to us. you can always confirm you are accessing our secure server before you submit personal financial information by looking at the lower left-hand corner of your browser to see an unbroken key or a closed lock (depending upon your browser), which indicates an active ssl. to double-check for security, look at the url or location line of your browser. if you have accessed a secure server, the first characters of the address in that line should change from "http" to "https." if you are having difficulty accessing the secure server or if you're not comfortable shopping online at any of our websites, please feel free to place your order with us by telephone at the number listed below in the section entitled, "how do i contact you?" a customer service representative will be happy to assist you. << back to top what is phishing?
