bambeco believes that everyone can make a difference, and that incremental change leads to real impact. our name bambeco celebrates bamboo, the planet’s most renewable and versatile resource, and ecology, the essential relationship between people and the natural environment. our commitment at bambeco, we make it easy for you to live your values without sacrificing quality and price. as you browse our web store, you’ll discover a wealth of stylish and modern products carefully selected to advance a more sustainable and just world. our innovative products are made from materials that are all-natural, biodegradable, nontoxic, organic, recycled, recyclable, repurposed or renewable. our distribution process is designed to be as energy efficient as possible as we move toward our goal of becoming carbon neutral. our shipping materials are made of recycled and biodegradable materials whenever possible. our shippers are committed to reducing their own carbon footprints. we place a high value on human dignity, and strive to ensure that those involved in the creation of our products are paid a fair wage and provided with a safe, healthy work environment. bambeco works with partners, suppliers and artisans who share these values. how we give back bambeco employees are encouraged to participate in eco-friendly service projects in their communities. in addition, bambeco annually donates a portion of its profits to organizations devoted to improving the environment. in 2009, bambeco is contributing 1% of net sales to wwf to support its global conservation mission. to learn more about wwf, please visit www.worldwildlife.org. together, for a greener tomorrow confucius said that a journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. in the same way, bambeco believes that incremental change leads to a real impact. we’re excited by the growing momentum behind the sustainability movement and the opportunity to help people make enlightened choices in line with their values. we're always looking for ways to make your experience better, and to improve the things that we do as a business and a responsible corporate citizen. we invite you to visit our "in the press"
