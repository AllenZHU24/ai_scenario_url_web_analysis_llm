collections etc. offers an unconditional satisfaction guarantee for all of our merchandise. if for any reason you are not 100% satisfied, simply return the item for exchange, refund or full credit within 100 days of the date of your purchase. shipping and handling charges are not refundable. returns are easy! here's how: to expedite your return, please complete return form section a on the back of your packing slip. if you would like to exchange the item you're returning for a different item, also complete return form section b. enclose the packing slip return form, packaging material, and the item(s) within the box being returned. detach the "return label" on the front side of your packing slip. use clear tape or glue to affix the return label on your package. all returns should be sent to: collections etc. returns processing 4800 proviso drive melrose park, il 60163-1301 customer is responsible for the original and return shipping cost. cod returns will not be accepted. use your most convenient carrier to send the return to us. collections etc. is not responsible for packages until they are received at our facility. if returning multiple cartons, please return the items with the matching packing slip in each carton. if you do not request an exchange, a refund will be processed if you paid by check or money order. if payment was by credit card, we will credit your account. please allow 3-4 weeks from receipt at collections etc. to process your return and receive your refund. not following the instructions above may delay the process. go to top of page go to top
