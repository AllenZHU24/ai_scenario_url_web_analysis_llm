suggestion is to consider the aesthetic, theme or location of your wedding. having that starting point is sure to spark some ideas. for example, maybe you are having your wedding on the beach. in that whether you want the favors to be practical or more on the so-called fun side. if you are going for practical, then you might consider favors that can easily be used over and over again by your wedding guests. such as a seashell bookmark in a personalized folder, personalized candle tins, or personalized luggage tags. if you are opting for something a little more 'fun' then consider personalized wedding favors such as personalized plantable seed cards or personalized playing cards. the possibilities are truly endless. another popular idea for personalized wedding favors is edible wedding favors. this can be anything from custom cookies to candy, brownie pops or chocolate covered oreos. we even have personalized bags of champagne gummy bears and custom fortune cookies. you that can be used at the wedding and also brought home or personalized bottle openers that do the same. just remind guests that when the wedding is over, that they should keep whatever it is they were using. wedding favors can also be personalized with packaging. depending on the favor you choose, you may want to put it in a personalized bag or a personalized jar. or maybe even tie on a personalized tag. we also strive to add pretty personalization to wedding favors by having our exclusive designs printed on them. you will see several phrases, quotes, and pictures throughout our collections, and have the ability to customize with colors, names, and dates. some of our favorite customizable wedding collections include 'we do', 'happily ever after', and 'best day ever.' whatever
