contact you, for the continued collection, use or disclosure of your information, at anytime, by contacting us.section 3 - disclosurewe may disclose your personal information if we are required by law help ensure the secure handling of credit card information by our store and its service providers.for more insight, you may also want to read shopify’s terms of service (https://www.shopify.com/legal/terms) or privacy statement (https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy).section 5 - third-party servicesin general, the third-party providers used by us will only collect, use and disclose your information to the extent necessary to allow them to perform the services they provide to us.however, certain third-party service providers, such as payment gateways and other recommend that you read their privacy policies so you can understand the manner in which your personal information will be handled by these providers.in particular, remember that certain providers may provider, then your information may become subject to the laws of the jurisdiction(s) in which that service provider or its facilities are located.as an example, if you are located in canada and your
