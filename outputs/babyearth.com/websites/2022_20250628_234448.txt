in the lower 48 states* - details close about us the babyearth story a company is born in 2003, a company, baby-wise was born. it was a risky venture for a small, entrepreneurial family to start, but baby-wise filled a much-needed void in the industry by offering a mix of both tried-and-true classics and innovative breakthroughs. for the first year, baby-wise’s inventory, was stored in a two-car garage. a year later, the inventory was moved into a large warehouse that should have lasted them two or three years, but the company outgrew that location in less than a year and moved into a larger warehouse. again, this one should have lasted another two to three years. a mere ten months later, a wall had to be knocked down to make more room. welcome babyearthhalf a million products later, today baby-wise has matured into a thriving company. and just like baby, the company is celebrating new milestones. in 2008, the company rebranded itself as babyearth to reflect a growing interest in green living. while only a portion of their products are eco-friendly, babyearth boasts one of the largest selections of eco-friendly baby products in the market today. to further illustrate their commitment to corporate social responsibility, babyearth has implemented a variety of eco-friendly business initiatives. home about us advertisement babyearth.com earns a commission
