to any address in the united states, including alaska and hawaii, puerto rico, u.s virgin islands, and all apo/fpo addresses. your estimated delivery date is dependent on the shipping method you choose and your shipping destination. for faster delivery, you may upgrade your shipping method for orders shipped within the contiguous united states. expedited, overnight, and shoprunner 2-day shipping are not available to alaska, hawaii, puerto rico, u.s. virgin islands, and apo/fpo addresses. standard ground shipping will typically deliver in-stock items to you within 4-7 business days. expedited shipping will typically deliver in-stock items to you within 2-3 business days. overnight shipping will typically deliver in-stock items to you within 1-2 business days. shoprunner 2-day shipping will typically deliver in-stock items to you within 2 business days. note that customers must login to their shoprunner account before completing their purchase to qualify for shoprunner 2-day free shipping. if you choose to upgrade your shipping method, your order must be received and clear credit authorization by 1:00 p.m. est or your order may not be processed until the following business day. business days are monday-friday, excluding federal holidays within the united states. shipping charges your shipping charge will be calculated during checkout based on your total order value and your preferred shipping method for each ship-to-address. please see the chart below for shipping rates. rates include labor and material costs, and may exceed the actual cost we pay our
