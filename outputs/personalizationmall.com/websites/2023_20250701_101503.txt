or just adding your own creative style to your home, at the heart of it all is you - your name, your family, your story. as the industry leader in personalization, we love our customers. they are not only represented on everything we make, but they make everything we represent – creativity, individuality, and joy. our relationship with our customers is truly special, and always at the core of everything we do. founded in chicago, il in 1998, and now a part of the 1-800-flowers.com, inc. family, we take pride in offering an unbeatable personalization experience for all of our customers. our
