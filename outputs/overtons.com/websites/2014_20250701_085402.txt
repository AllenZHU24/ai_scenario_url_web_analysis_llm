overton’s advice & tips news home > customer service > shipping & handling shipping and handling (48 states) the flat-rate shipping and handling charges listed above are for shipments to the contiguous united states only. all delivery times are quoted in business days not including weekends and holidays. certain items may require additional shipping charges, and some items can only be shipped by truck service. hazardous merchandise cannot be shipped by air express services.a hazardous shipping charge may be applied for specified items. overton’s reserves the right to limit quantities and/or charge actual shipping charges on bulk orders. the following shipping methods are available for most orders shipping to the 48 states. free standard ground shipping most orders ship within 24 hours and will arrive in 5 to 7 business days via fedex smartpost with final delivery by the u.s. postal service. expedited ground shipping most orders ship within 24 hours and will arrive in 2 to 5 business days, from time of shipment, via fedex or priority mail. please see chart for estimated delivery times to your area. items that ship by truck service, or are shipped directly from the manufacturer, may not qualify for expedited ground shipping. 2nd day and next day air express shipping in-stock phone orders placed before 3:00pm e.t. and web orders placed before 2:00pm e.t. will ship the same day and arrive the 2nd or next business day. the flat-rate 2nd day and next day shipping charges listed are for most 1-20 lb. orders. orders over 20 lbs. will be charged actual shipping charges that will be calculated during checkout. a street address is required for air express shipping. we do not refund 2nd day and next day charges unless overton’s has made an error. certain items may not qualify for air express shipping. offer does not include saturday delivery. saturday delivery is available to certain areas. please call customer service (1-800-334-6541) for saturday delivery rates and availability. alaska, hawaii, puerto rico, virgin islands, apo/fpo, and all u.s. territories please allow 6-10 business days for shipments to these areas. these areas require a special handling fee of $10.00 plus actual shipping charges based on the weight and dimensions of the package(s). charges will be calculated during checkout. any applicable duties, taxes, and customs charges are the responsibility of the customer. international and canada shipping we ship our products to over 150 countries around the world. international shipping charges are dependent upon weights and
