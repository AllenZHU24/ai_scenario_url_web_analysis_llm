history overton’s began selling waterskis in the mid 1970’s. over the years, we've built our reputation by offering quality products at the best prices found anywhere. today you can look to overton’s to provide the highest quality products for all your boating needs. over the years, our company has grown from its humble beginnings in the back of a grocery store in greenville, nc, to the "world’s largest watersports dealer". widely recognized and respected in the industry, overton’s catalog and website provide consumers with the widest selection of boating accessories and watersports equipment out there. our acquisition by gander mountain in december of 2007 assured continued growth, allowing us to expand our offerings and have our products available in gander’s 115+ retail stores throughout the united states. we sincerely appreciate the opportunity to serve you by meeting your boating and watersports needs. whether it’s a product we’re selling or assistance we’re providing,
