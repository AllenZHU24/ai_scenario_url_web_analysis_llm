crown & caliber scholarship we believe in making a lasting positive impact and want to invest in others who do the same. what it is? we are looking for a high school or university student who embodies at least three of our core values below. we want to know the impact you have had on your community and others around you. the best submission will be awarded a scholarship of $2,500 to help them achieve their academic goals. our scholarship program is centered around our values we believe in living for the line and not the dot. to us, what we do in any particular moment isn’t as important as the legacy we leave. our values guide the way and help us make the most of our time here. we seize opportunities to grow and change the world around us through hard work and innovative solutions we cultivate relationships that elevate others above ourselves through our thoughts, words, and actions we magnify moments to build others up and change the course of lives, even if just marginally we pursue excellence by delivering the most value possible and never becoming stagnant we promote stewardship by giving of our talents and resources to leave a lasting impact on this earth how to apply submit the following to scholarships@crownandcaliber.com: an essay talking about how you embody the values and the impact it has had on others and you documented volunteer hours 2-3 references a
