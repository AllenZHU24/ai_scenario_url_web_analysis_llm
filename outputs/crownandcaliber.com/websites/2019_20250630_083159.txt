shipping free, insured shipping crown & caliber insures and pays for every package coming to and from our atlanta facilities. whether you’re buying or selling, you’re in good hands. to protect your purchase, someone over the age of 18 must be available to sign for the watch package upon delivery. meet our partners view shipping faqs how to ship your watch in order for your watch to be fully insured during transit, please follow the instructions below. print out your shipping labels (there will be two). grab two new, sturdy boxes (one will go inside the other). pack your watch, watch box, and accessories. tape the “inner box” label to the box. pack the inner box into the larger outer box. tape the pre-addressed label to the outer box. schedule a ups pick-up or drop your package off at an authorized ups shipping facility. do not drop off at a drop box. to schedule a ups pick-up, call 1 (800) 742-5877. still have questions about how to ship your watch? email us international orders
