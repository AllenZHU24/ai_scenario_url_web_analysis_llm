give back in our office, we focus on creating a lasting positive impact. sure, our daily mission is to take the risk and fear out of buying or selling watches. but if we stopped there, we’d be falling seriously short of our values. partnering with map international allows us to do a bit of good with each and every watch that we sell. map international is a global health and relief organization providing life-changing medicines and health supplies to people in need worldwide. map serves all people, regardless of religion, gender, race, nationality, or ethnic background. in times of disaster, map international also provides medicines and health supplies to people affected by crisis. as part of our charitable partnership with map international, crown & caliber supports map’s medicine for the children program. this program is about bringing health and hope to children in resource poor countries through the delivery of life-saving antibiotics that are otherwise too expensive or unavailable. how have we helped so far? 500 disaster relief kits made 100 + hrs of community service 26,324 rounds of antibiotics antibiotics. the sale of each watch allows map international to purchase a potentially life-saving, 5-day regimen of antibiotics. disaster relief kits. when natural disasters happen, oftentimes people no longer have access to basics. map international sends disaster relief kits to give people toiletries and ease the hardship. packing disaster relief kits gives us the chance to help during difficult times. be the first to know subscribe to the crown &
