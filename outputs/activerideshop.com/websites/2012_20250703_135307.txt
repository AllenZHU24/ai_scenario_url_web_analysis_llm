you can view your order status at any time by logging in to your account here. your order status is updated in real time. how do i track my order? once your order has shipped you will receive an email with your tracking number. you will be able to track your package within 24 hours of the time it ships from that email. how do i know if something is in stock? our website features real time inventory. if you see the "add to cart" button, it means the item is in stock. can i make a return? yes. all returns must be made within 30 days of receipt. fill out the return slip attached to your invoice and mail your return to the address specified. for additional questions about returns click here. do you do backorders?
