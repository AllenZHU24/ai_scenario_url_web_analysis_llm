firsthand themselves, the coughlin family established the nonprofit jegs foundation in 2003. the goal of the jegs foundation is to help raise cancer awareness and to ensure 100% of all donations made go towards funding cancer research. through the jegs foundation, donations are made monthly to the arthur g. james cancer hospital and richard j. solove research institute in columbus, ohio. decades later, the jegs foundation continues to run strong, having contributed millions of dollars to finding a cure. support breast cancer research breast cancer awareness month is commemorated every october to reinforce the importance of early detection and preparedness against the disease. while many are aware of breast cancer, not everyone has a proactive approach for early detection. to support and raise awareness for breast cancer awareness month, jegs changes their logo from the traditional yellow color to pink. additionally, the annual jegs employee health fair proudly hosts a mobile mammogram unit. for resources and guidance throughout the journey of cancer - from prevention to recovery, consider these organizations: national breast cancer foundation american cancer society
