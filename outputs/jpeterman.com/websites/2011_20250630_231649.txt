tape measure using the instructions below. (or have a friend do it. serve sangria, as a bribe.) jot down your measurements and compare them with the body measurement sizing charts found below and on each product page. hat. measure circumference of your head where hat would naturally rest (normally above the brow). waist. measure around your natural waist or, if you prefer, a little lower, where you may actually wear your pants. neck. measure neck. (add a little for adam's apple.) seat/hip. standing, with feet together, measure around the fullest part of the hips. don't cheat. chest/bust. measure around fullest part, but with tape under your arms. you'll need a friend to do this for you. (more sangria.) inseam. use a well-fitting similarly styled pair of pants. lay them flat, with seam on seam. measure from crotch to bottom of hem. arm/sleeve length. arm must be bent to do this correctly. then, measure from center of back of neck, over the shoulder, down the arm, past the elbow (bent) all the way to wrist. (friend needed for this too.) "for men and women." what does that mean? men's sizes, in fact. on women, these items will be attractively oversized; sleeve length will be long. it helps us to know why you werent happy. carefully pack your return merchandise and secure it with strong tape. you may use our convenient, prepaid merchandise return label and $7.95 will be deducted from your refund when we process it. or you can use the carrier of your choice to ship your return to us at your own expense. if you do not use our merchandise return label, we encourage you to insure the package. it usually takes about two weeks for your return/exchange item(s) to be received and processed by our customer care team. we cheerfully accept all returns of unworn, undamaged merchandise purchased at full or sale prices in our catalogs or on our website for a refund of the merchandise price you paid within 60 days of the original purchase. we do not accept any returns for items that we sell in the last chance dept. of our website (because these are very limited quantities of items). however, we will exchange these items for a different size of that item if it is available. to exchange merchandise:   please follow the same packaging process explained above for a return. please make sure you fill out the back of your packing list to explain the type of exchange you would like to make. we offer two options for exchanges: to exchange same item for different size or color (or damaged or incorrect merchandise): shipping is on us. you will not be charged the shipping cost to return these items to us and you will not be charged the shipping cost to get that exchanged item back to you. to exchange item for different item: shipping is on you. if you use our convenient, prepaid merchandise return label, $7.95 will be deducted from your refund when we process it. in addition, you will be charged shipping cost again when we ship you that different item. if additional payment or credit is required upon exchanging for different item, we will process it on your provided credit card (or original form of payment, if different).
