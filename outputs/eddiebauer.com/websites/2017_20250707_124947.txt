"let all who enjoy the outdoors and its natural environment join to conserve, preserve, and build from what remains." – our founder, eddie bauer eddie bauer is rds-certified we are one of the first brands to commit to using down from rds-compliant sources. rds is an independent standard to ensure humane treatment of the waterfowl providing down and feathers for apparel and home products. the welfare of the birds is respected at all times; live plucking and force feeding are strictly prohibited. each stage in the supply chain is audited by a third-party certification body, and only products with 100% certified down and feathers carry the rds logo. in order for eddie bauer to be rds-certified, our corporate offices and distribution center are randomly audited by the certification body to ensure that our systems, processes, training, staff, management, and records are and continue to be in full compliance with the standard. learn more about rds shop women shop men responsible wool standard (rws) in 2016, eddie bauer also signed onto the responsible wool standard (rws). created by the same organization that developed rds, rws is a set of standards that apply to the conditions and care of animals raised for wool fiber, including merino and cashmere. the rws supply chain will take 3+ years to be developed. eddie bauer is committed to using rws fiber by 2020. learn more about rws customer service 1-800-426-8020 help & support contact us order status shipping returns gift cards store locator request a catalog catalog quick order wish list size charts company info about us careers affiliate program supply chain disclosure site map eddie bauer credit card learn more & apply pay my bill adventure in your inbox get the latest news and offers. enter email address go live your adventure. get rewarded. already a member?
