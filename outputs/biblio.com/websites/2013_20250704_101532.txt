that have a limited supply. there's no one single determinant for a book's scarcity. some books are individual, unique works, like fore edge painting or original manuscripts. others are considered scarce because the number of interested collectors exceeds the number of copies available on the market. these categories represent a tiny portion of the millions of books on biblio. these collections
