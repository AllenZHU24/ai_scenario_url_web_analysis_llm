accessibility our commitment to accessibility we are committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. consistent with this goal, we have started taking steps to enhance our site and improve its usability by users who require assistive technology. we are currently working to conform the accessibility of our website to the web content accessibility guidelines (wcag) and the accessible rich internet applications (aria) specification. we will continue to enhance our site to deliver accessible experiences to all users. if you experience difficulties in accessing our website content, wish to share feedback, or require assistance from us, please call us at 800-813-9432 between the hours of 10 am - 4 pm est monday through friday. you
