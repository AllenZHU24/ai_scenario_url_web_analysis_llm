libraries biblio for libraries for over 20 years, biblio has consistently held two core values: a fierce independence from corporate monopolies and a commitment to supporting literacy. we sincerely appreciate the vital role that libraries play in preserving and safeguarding the most significant cultural and literary artifacts—books. and now, we want to build on that commitment by offering libraries a free bibliophile club membership. this means libraries will receive an automatic 10% discount on every purchase they make from the world’s largest independent marketplace of used, rare, and out-of-print books. the bibliophiles club is an annual membership that saves you 10% on every purchase every day, with savings of up to $20 per purchase. as a library, you benefit from this special membership for free. there are no strings and no obligations –it's simply our way of supporting your valuable work. join now this is how it works if you have received from us a membership number and a claim code: activate your bibliophiles club membership by clicking hereenter your membership number and claim code.when prompted, log in to your existing biblio account or create a new one. that's it –your membership is now active. each time you return to make a purchase, simply log in to your biblio account. your unique membership code will be automatically applied in your cart, saving you 10% on your purchase. if you are a library and want to benefit from the program, please contact us at libraries@biblio.com. biblio is a staunch supporter of literacy, and as a socially responsible business, we want to see libraries thrive. in fact, we've used our profits to construct 16 community libraries in south america. we’ve supported reading and literacy programs in southeast asia and australia. and now we are furthering our support by providing libraries with a 10% discount on over 80 million books. we deeply appreciate the work that you do to promote, curate and preserve our collective heritage and hope this new program will strengthen your collections by helping your funding stretch just a little bit farther. need more help? our support team will be happy to help!
