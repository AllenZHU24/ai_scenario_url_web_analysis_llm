;) now, almost two decades and three buildings later – sherri, scott and the entire big dot team run this remarkable party supply business called big dot of happiness. why we love what we do we don’t just create party supplies; we create goose bump moments…memories that will last a lifetime! our designer party collections make it easier for everyone to be a party-throwing hero.  life is one big celebration – let’s have some quality fun! what makes our products unique? we are where quality and fun meet! from concept and design, big dot of happiness products are 100% unique and original with the majority crafted right in our wisconsin facility. we pride ourselves on the quality of our party supplies and specialize in making your party a fun event to remember for years to come. dot along with us... more than our hairstyles have changed over the years from the very first employee to the most big dotters ever, and even a few years in between when we were a smaller team focused on reinventing ourselves, one thing always stays the same - big dot is here to make your party even more extraordinary! it all started with a woman, a card table and a dream. journey through our history below. who knows what will be next, but it sure has been a thrilling ride so far! 1999 ... a dream. sherri started crafting baby shower gifts as a hobby, filling the random moments she wasn’t busy with her kids. 2000 new store: first website launched. 2001 first employee is welcomed. 2002 moving day - a workspace big enough for everyone! 2003 featured: woman's world. 2004 business is growing. her husband, scott decided he wanted to be part of the team. 2005 big dot of happiness becomes our official brand. 2006 we need a bigger building - moving again! 2007 featured: discovery channel documentary on baby showers. 2008 did someone say "moving day?"the new 20,000 square foot building is big, blue and perfectly doterrific! 2009 product launch: large format printers 2010 inspiring others. sherri was the keynote speaker at the women's business conference. 2010 menomonie chamber of commerce business of the month 2011 we've shipped 500,000 orders!
