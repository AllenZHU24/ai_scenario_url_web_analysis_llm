dot of happiness, llc our commitment to privacy we at big dot of happiness are fanatics about everything, including your privacy. to better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our pages, you can submit information about other people. for example, if you order a gift online and want it sent directly to the recipient, you will need to submit the recipient's address. in this circumstance, the types of personal information collected are: name, address, phone number, etc. the way we use information we use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order to order status. once your order has been placed you will receive a confirmation email to let you know your order is being processed. you will also receive a confirmation email once your order has been marketplaces we sell on. any guests that use these marketplaces are subject to these marketplaces’ privacy policies. what are cookies? cookies are small text files that are stored by browsers (for example, safari or firefox) on your computer or mobile phone. they allow websites to store things like user preferences and store contents of your shopping cart. you can think of cookies as providing a ‘memory’ for the website, so that it can recognize you when you come back and respond appropriately. cookies can also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the
