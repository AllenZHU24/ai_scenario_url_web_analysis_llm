yellow over time? our white gold rings do not turn yellow over time because they are made of our exclusive true whitetm gold, which never fades in color. typically, 18kt white gold is actually 75% pure yellow gold, which must be rhodium plated. rhodium plating is standard for all white gold jewelry and gives the item a pure white brilliance and luster. in time, however, the plating tends to wear thin, showing the natural color of the white gold beneath. vigorous cleaning of the ring (such as with a toothbrush) can accelerate the process. true whitetm gold was specifically designed in
