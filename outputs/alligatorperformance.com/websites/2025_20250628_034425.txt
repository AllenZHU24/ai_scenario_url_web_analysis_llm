give us a call! we're more than happy to get your stuff on its way in a hurry, whether it's by air, 2-day, or 1-day shipping. we can't, however, offer expedited shipping for free. you must call us at 208-719-7400 if you need expedited shipping on your order. turnaround time most orders containing items listed as “in stock” will ship the same day if placed on a weekday before 12pm pst / 3pm est. orders requiring additional verification (security concerns, incorrect information, etc.) may need additional processing time. items listed as “on order” or “out of stock” typically ship by the estimated ship date listed on the product page. these estimated dates are not guaranteed and are subject to change periodically. our customer service representatives monitor backorders daily to ensure your order ships as soon as possible. transit times the delivery time of your package primarily depends on the shipping carrier and the service level selected. expedited shipping is guaranteed on 'in stock' products only. shipping cutoff times for expedited and regular shipments are 11am pst / 2pm est. drop ships and backordered items will ship out as soon as they become available. when selecting the shipping method for your package, please keep in mind that most carriers do not work on weekends or major holidays. when carriers quote shipping days, they refer to business days. business days are defined as monday through friday, except for holidays observed by the selected parcel carrier.  for specific delivery estimates, you can usually find detailed information on the carrier’s website, or you can also contact one of our customer service representatives for specific details. as a general rule, ups transit times are as follows: ups next day air - delivered within 1 business day ups 2nd day air - delivered within 2 business days ups 3-day service - delivered within 3 business days ups ground - delivered within 3-5 business days *if you provide an apartment address as your ship to destination, this is at your own risk. alligator performance will not be liable for loss or theft. out-of-stock orders if an order is placed for a product that is labeled “on order”, “call for availability, or “out of stock”, we will keep your order in our system and ship the item as soon as it becomes available. alligator performance reserves the right to split your order into multiple shipments without notification in an attempt to try and get your products to you sooner. orders are normally only split automatically if both orders qualify for free shipping. if you wish to split your order voluntarily due to an out of stock item, please notify our sales department prior to placing your order and be aware that additional shipping charges may apply if both shipments do not qualify for free shipping. billing / shipping addresses if you wish to have your item(s) shipped to an address other than your billing address, please be aware that your order will require additional verification and may be subject to processing delays. we recommend allowing extra time for order fulfillment when shipping to an alternate address. if you have any questions about your order verification, please feel free to contact our sales department. alligator performance does not ship to a po box or apo/fpo address. shipping insurance alligator performance offers shipping insurance for all orders. insurance costs an additional 3% over the price of your order. insurance is easily selected at checkout. insurance only protects you for lost, stolen or damaged items. to begin your claim in case of a lost or stolen item, please email us at customerservice@alligatorperformance.com. please include your original alligator order number in the subject line. customer service team must be notified within 5 days of delivery date. please allow up to 5 business days for claim consideration. if your claim is approved, please allow up to 1-3 business days for your replacement part to be re-shipped. no refunds will be issued for approved claims, customer will receive replacement parts. shipping insurance does not cover delayed shipping. damaged / lost shipment if your shipment becomes damaged or lost in transit, please notify our customer service department immediately at customer service. please include photos of the packaging as well as pictures of the damaged part(s).   do not discard any boxes or packaging materials that were included with the shipment. in order to fully process a damage claim, all original products and packaging materials must be provided. alligator performance is not liable for damage claims that are denied due to customer negligence. if you provide an apartment, multi-unit housing complex or townhome address as your ship to destination, this is at your own risk. alligator performance will not be liable for any package(s) not received. all damaged or missing parts must be reported to alligator performance customer service within 5 business days of delivery. tracking numbers tracking numbers will typically be emailed once an order has been processed and shipped. however, in some events an item is not “in stock”, alligator performance may choose to have it shipped directly from the manufacturer so that it can be delivered to you as quickly as possible. in this event, a tracking number will not be provided but if one is needed, contact customer service and we would be more than happy to obtain one on request. conditions of use by visiting or purchasing from alligator performance, you agree to abide by our terms and
