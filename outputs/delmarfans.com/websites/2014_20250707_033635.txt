- from close-to-ceiling to pendant there are different degrees of lighting. the first type of home lighting is called general lighting. this form of illumination is used on a daily basis and serves as the primary source of home lighting. the second source of home lighting is called task lighting. task lighting fixtures focus the illumination on a particular spot or area. generally, these light fixtures are installed in a kitchen or home office to help users focus on the task at hand. the third type of home lighting is called accent lighting. accent lighting provides subtle illumination and helps create mood lighting. lighting fixtures are available in a range of shapes, sizes, and finishes for indoor and outdoor use. outdoor lighting highlights a home’s unique architecture while discouraging would-be intruders from trespassing. here are some common lighting fixtures: chandelier lighting – formerly used in the 15th century as a symbol of luxury, these light fixtures create a sense of elegance and sophistication. close-to-ceiling lighting – ideal for homes with low-profile ceilings, these lights complement any existing light fixtures. track lighting – this type of home lighting is comprised of a continuous track lighting flexible monorail that contains several electrical conductors wherein light fixtures are attached. pendant lights – a versatile source of home lighting, hang these pendant lighting fixtures over a dining table or a kitchen bar. recessed lighting – this form of home lighting is installed directly onto the ceiling for a clean, streamlined look. wall lighting – these lights provide a warm glow to the walls and surrounding areas. if your home has high or vaulted ceilings, wall lights are useful. lamps – floor and table lamps are a convenient way to light up a room. lamps are portable as they can be moved from room to room to where they are most needed. ceiling fans with lights – this multi-functional fixture combines necessity of lighting and comfort of ceiling fan as well as it simplifies the amount of items in a room. it is great for rooms that are in need of more lighting and comfort. once you have selected the right
