trade areas, a delivery area surcharge may be applied to those items requiring non-standard delivery methods. this includes large items such as furniture. you will be contacted if these fees apply to your order. expected delivery times stocked items are usually delivered the next business day within local trade areas when the order is placed before 5:00 locally. for deliveries outside the local trade area, additional time will be calculated and noted. orders placed after 5:00 pm local time will also require additional delivery time. products shipped via third party providers, such as furniture and large or heavy items require longer delivery lead times. off-shore (alaska, hawaii, puerto rico and the u.s. virgin islands) yes, we deliver to off-shore addresses in alaska, hawaii and and made aware of the charges before your order is shipped. these charges are based on the delivery area and the size and weight of the item(s) being delivered. expected delivery times stocked items are delivered within five days. products shipped via third party providers have limited availability for off-shore delivery. furniture items will require extended delivery time to the islands of
