that do not meet these conditions. products shipped from the manufacturer may have a 15% restock fee. processing your return will take from 2-4 weeks. refunds are for the cost of the products only. return to a northern tool store: products purchased online, by phone or catalog may be returned at your local northern tool store. return by shipment: for deliveries that arrive by parcel use the original box or use a sturdy substitute to ship products. write your return authorization number you received on the return label located on the front of the packing slip. exceptions: products shipped from the manufacturer cannot be returned to northern. gas and diesel-powered products cannot be returned to northern once they have been gassed or oiled. if they have been gassed or oiled they need to
