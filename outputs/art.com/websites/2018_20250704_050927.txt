what's affirm? affirm brings an easy financing process and offers real-time decisions for online purchase. with affirm, you can buy and receive your purchase now, and pay for it in fixed monthly installments over time. what's the catch? no catch! just an easy way to make art more affordable. affirm will text and e-mail you payment reminders so you never need to worry. you can even select whatever payment plan fits your budget best! no deferred interest. no hidden fees. just art — at the price you want to pay. how does it work? select affirm payment at checkout and see if you qualify! you’ll be asked a few simple questions , and you’ll receive a text confirmation with whether you qualify. will this affect my credit? affirm does perform a credit check, but checking your eligibility will not affect your credit score. what kind of payment plans are available? it’s a choose your own adventure! sales under $50 may allow you to defer payment entirely for 30 days, while larger sales vary depending on your credit. affirm offers 3, 6, and 12 month payment plans*, so you can choose whatever amount you’d like to pay. *as an example, a $500 art.com purchase might cost $44 over 12 months at 10% apr. affirm payment subject to credit check and approval. down payment may be required. for purchases under $50, limited payment options are available. affirm loans are made by cross
