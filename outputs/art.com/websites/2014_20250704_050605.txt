the most-recognized figures in 20th century art, pablo picasso (1881-1973) was a spanish painter, sculptor, printmaker, ceramicist and stage designer. his early success, through the blue period (1901-1904) and rose period (1904-1906) led to the establishment of cubism (1909-1912) – one of his major contributions to the art world. picasso's personal life was as controversial as his work – he
