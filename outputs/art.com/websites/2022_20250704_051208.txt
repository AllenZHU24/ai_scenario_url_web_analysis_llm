you don’t need to be a designer to pick out the perfect wall decorations, from contemporary to coastal. consider how you use your living room: is it formal, or is it where everyone hangs out? how do you want your family or guests to feel when they are in the lounge: inspired, relaxed, playful? do you have parties in that space, or host all-night movie marathons? whatever you use your sitting room
