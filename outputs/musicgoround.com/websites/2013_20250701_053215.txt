you are purchasing product from one store. because our stores are independent franchise locations and the product you are purchasing is coming direct from those stores, we need to split your payments made for purchases to each location you are ordering from. paypal does not allow payments to be “split” between accounts.the paypal option will appear in the payment screen of the shopping cart when you are ordering product/s from one location. if you are ordering from multiple locations, the paypal option will not appear.if you want to use paypal for payment to multiple stores, you can simply create different purchases from the different locations. there will be no additional costs to you as the shipping charges for each product are based upon the location of the store to your billing
