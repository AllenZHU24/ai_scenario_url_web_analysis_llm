costs are listed on each product display page.  these costs are, at times, an estimate based on all of our packages being properly packed and insured.  at times, the shipping price may fluctuate based are interested in to discuss an exact shipping quote to the billing address of your credit card. to help prevent credit card fraud for our customers and our stores, music go round® stores will only approve a transaction when the shipping address provided is an exact match to the billing address of the purchaser's credit card.   please ensure that the address you enter into the shopping cart is
