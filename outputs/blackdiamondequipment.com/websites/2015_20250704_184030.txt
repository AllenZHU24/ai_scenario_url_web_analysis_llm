weekend, will process and ship on the following monday. we make every effort to ship fedex 2-day air and fedex overnight requested orders the same business day we receive them. however, orders must be received by 12 p.m. mountain time to ensure this service. we do not offer saturday delivery on fedex 2-day or overnight shipments. we do not ship on weekends or holidays. please plan accordingly. note that holidays do not count as business days and we are closed for a fair amount of them. you will be notified of any backorders or stock outs. we use and define our ship methods as follows: economy:
