shipping policies shipping policies your browser's javascript functionality is turned off. please turn it on so that you can experience the full capabilities of this site. shipping details we ship to the united states and canada only! orders are generally shipped the next business day after we receive your order. orders received on fridays, after 12 p.m. mountain time, or over the weekend, will process and ship on the following monday. we make every effort to ship fedex 2-day air and fedex overnight requested orders the same business day we receive them. however, orders must be received by 12 p.m. mountain time to ensure this service. we do not offer saturday delivery on fedex 2-day or overnight shipments. we do not ship on weekends or holidays. please plan accordingly. note that holidays do not count as business days and we are closed for a fair amount of them. you will be notified of any backorders or stock outs. we use and define our ship methods as follows: economy: fedex smartpost, fedex home delivery or usps first class mail / 3-10 business day delivery fedex ground: fedex home delivery or fedex ground / 2-6 business day delivery fedex 2nd day air: delivers 3rd business day—orders must be received by 12pm mountain time for same day shipping fedex next day air: delivers next business day—orders must be received by 12pm mountain time for same day shipping
