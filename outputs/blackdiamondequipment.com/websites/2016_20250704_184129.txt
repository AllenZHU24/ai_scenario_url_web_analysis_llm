collection is stripped of excess for technical alpine objectives where every ounce counts. engineered to withstand the abuse of rugged mountain terrain and built using the most advanced materials available, the helio collection represents the core of what we do at black diamond. navigating crevassed glaciers, climbing over snow, ice and rock, and descending steep, no-fall terrain requires the full set of mountain travel skills, and as such, the helio collection encompasses the spectrum of essential equipment—from apparel and skis to packs and harnesses. it’s gear you can trust. gear that holds up but won’t hold you back. the helio collection exists to help you go lighter. faster. stronger. helio skis the helio collection is all about equipment that maximizes performance and minimizes weight, and nothing represents this idea better than our helio series skis. featuring four different sizes to cover the gamut of conditions and objectives, helio skis are handmade in austria with a pre-preg carbon fiber layup that offers incredible torsional stiffness and balanced flex in a lightweight package. the result is a collection of skis that won’t weigh you down on the skin track or the climb, and provides incredibly reliable and precise performance on steep, technical descents. the helio collection represents the core of what we do at black diamond. helio shell the distillation of
