shipping box or envelope. step 3 write your ra number clearly on the outside of the shipping package (not on the product packaging). feel free to also include your ra number on a slip of paper inside the shipment. step 4 use a return ship method best suited to you. hold onto the tracking number assigned to your return package; it is helpful if your shipment or refund is delayed. we cannot be responsible for your refund until your package has been received at our distribution center. step 5 once we receive your package, your return will be processed in 7-10 business days (excludes weekends
