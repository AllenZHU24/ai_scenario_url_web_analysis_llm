orders over $75 you must use the coupon freeship. the free shipping coupon cannot be used at the same time as another coupon. can i use a coupon on an auto delivery subscription? coupons are not eligible to be used on auto delivery orders due to the fact that these orders already get 5% off and free shipping. do you offer discounts for veterans or senior citizens? unfortunately, we do not
