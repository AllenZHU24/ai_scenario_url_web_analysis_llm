are not completely satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you have sixty (60) days from the date of purchase to return the product(s) as follows: 30/60-day return policy for all returns you are responsible for the delivery and return shipping costs (does not include defective or erroneously shipped items). certain items are subject to a 25% restock fee if returned within 30 days and a 15% - 40% restock fee if returned within 31-60 days. after 60 days we are unable to accept your product(s). exclusions (items that can’t be returned) special orders, sterile items and products that are made unsalable once opened (e.g., food, creams, diapers) are not returnable. how do i return something? read all information below call customer service at 888-746-2107 or 973-746-2582, extension #1 obtain a return merchandise authorization (“rma”) number and the return shipping address before returning any product(s), you must first obtain an rma number; no returns will be accepted without an rma number. please have the following information handy: customer name, order number, and reason for the return. the rma number should be clearly written on the return address label only. do not write on the shipping box. packages received without an rma number will be refused and sent back to you. all products must be returned in resalable condition, with their original packaging, documentation, warranty cards, manuals and accessories. if you paid for the purchase with a credit or debit card, your account will be credited back the amount of the purchase less any shipping charges within fifteen (15) business days from the time parentgiving receives the returned product(s). shipment of returns you may either arrange for shipment of the product(s) on your own, or in some cases for an additional fee, parentgiving will arrange for the product(s) to be picked up from your location. for all returns, other than defective product(s) or erroneously shipped items, you are responsible for the delivery and return shipping costs. we recommend that you fully insure your return shipment in the event that it is lost or damaged, and that you use a carrier that can provide you with proof of delivery, since parentgiving will not be responsible for any losses or damages during return shipping. defective and erroneously shipped items if you need to return a product that is defective or that was shipped in error, we apologize for your inconvenience. please contact us within sixty (60) days of the purchase for an rma number and we will reimburse you for both the original and return shipping costs. at your request, parentgiving will arrange for the defective product(s) to be picked up from your location for return - at our cost. shortages any product shortage must be reported within 48 hours from receipt of the order at your location. you must count your item(s) upon arrival and document any shortages on your delivery receipt. last updated: february 26, 2010 about us
