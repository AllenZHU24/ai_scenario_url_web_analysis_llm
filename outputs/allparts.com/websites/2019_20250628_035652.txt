wrenches wood tools misc tools 2021 catalog dealer resources welcome to your discounted purchasing account on allparts.com.  we've put together some information and resources that we think will benefit your company, as they are frequently requested and sometimes overlooked.  please visit our contact page and let us know if you require anything else. placing orders order by email we gladly accept orders by email. please send them to allparts@allparts.com so our software can sort them. you may type your order in the body of an email or attach your own po. you are also welcome to use this order form  which we have made for you. in any case, please submit legible orders and minimize extra pages. please reserve allparts@allparts.com for general communication. pricing additional discount tiers as an allparts dealer you can earn additional discounts on high-value orders; these discounts stack with dealer pricing. we offer 5% off orders $500 - $999 and another 5% off (10% off total) orders $1000 and greater. these discounts are calculated and applied automatically when ordering through the allparts.com webstore. bulk products, necks, and bodies do not count toward these discount tiers, nor do these discounts apply to these products. data files and photos be aware that the use of these resources or acting on these suggestions is entirely optional, and uploading digital files to your database is done at your own risk. the following files have been generated to help you update your pos system, vendor database, or website. product data download data file here. product images please view this shared google drive folder.  in most cases we use one product image to represent all variations of that product.  for example, our bb-3350 omega bass bridge is available in 4 colors, but we display only the nickel image on the product's page.  if you want images for the other colors, then you would need to take and create those images on your own. catalog and prices the pdf version of our catalog is available to download here. this is also where you can get the free print catalog. other services register your retail store in our store finder we want to send retail end users who need in-person help directly to your retail store. we've built a dealer store locator tool to help them find their way. this is not tied to our customer database - it is a separate product, so you need to add your business to it on your own. you can do this by visiting the store registration page while logged into your dealer account. follow us find us on facebook find us on twitter find
