about us about allparts music what you hear, comes from here we are allparts® music corporation. we have provided guitar and bass parts to manufacturers, wholesalers, musicians, luthiers, retail stores, and guitar repair shops all over the world since 1982. with over 7,000 products on-hand in our warehouse in houston, texas, all orders are processed in-house by our staff and never drop shipped. not only does this make allparts the world's largest distributor of guitar, bass, and amplifier parts, but it keeps product availability high and shipping costs low. we have official allparts distributors on six continents to meet the world's need for quality guitar parts to make quality music. wholesale and dealer accountswe primarily sell to manufacturers, retail stores, repair shops, resellers, luthiers, and other persons and businesses in the guitar parts industries. allparts dealers have access to the entire allparts inventory, receive generous discounts, and are entitled to numerous perks and benefits. to become an allparts dealer, wholesaler, or reseller, please see our dealer information section and follow the instructions. brandsin addition to our own high quality guitar parts, we distribute abm, bartolini, bigsby, cts, crl-centralab, danelectro, dice knobs, dunlop, electro-socket, fender®, gotoh, graph tech, grover, guiran, hipshot, lace pickups, leo quan, moses, oak grigsby, pigtail, razor, schaller, sovtek, sperzel, switchcraft, gibson®, tonepros® and wilkinson. we are america's exclusive distributor for abm-mueller hardware and razor pickups, and we are the largest gotoh distributor in the world. use the left navigation menu to shop by brand. retail orderswe fill retail orders to the end user. depending on the country, there is a flat-rate shipping fee for all retail orders - regardless of order size. credit card transactions are not charged until the order is shipped, but may be authorized when the order is placed. typical lead time on order shipment is 1 - 2 business days. the allparts office and warehouse are not open to the public. licensing and trademarksmany allparts guitar necks are licensed by fender® and are marked
