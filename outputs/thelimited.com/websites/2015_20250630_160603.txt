guidelinesterms & conditionspaypalpaypal reserve your items online and they'll be waiting for you in store. easy and convenient, here's how it works. click find in store on the page of the item you'd like to reserve. after choosing your size, you'll see a list of stores. next to the location that's convenient for you, click reserve in store to reserve the item there until close of business the following day. at the store, try on your items before you purchase. or if you love them, simply purchase and go! excludes the limited outlet locations. frequently asked questions are all online items available in stores? many items at thelimited.com are also available in our stores. online exclusives, final sale items, select colors for some styles, and items in petite, tall, and extended sizes are available only online. how long will my items be held? once a confirmation email has been sent, we will hold your reservation at your selected store until close of business the following day. where in the store do i pick up my items? after you receive your confirmation email, simply stop by the specified store. approach any associate and let them know you have a reservation waiting. when do i pay for my items? if you are happy with your selections, simply make your purchase at the store. what price will i pay for my items? you'll pay the lowest in-store price at the time of your purchase, which may vary from the online price. online promotional codes will not apply. available in these select stores currently available at polaris fashion place, tuttle crossing, easton town
