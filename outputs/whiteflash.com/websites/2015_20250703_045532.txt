come in all sizes, styles and price points. depending on the size and quality of diamonds used to make them they can be casual gifts or much, much more. regardless of the price point, diamond earrings are one of the most universally appreciated of all jewelry items. and now that they have crossed the gender gap, there is nothing more popular than sparkling diamond studs! being known for diamonds of the finest cut quality, whiteflash offers diamond earrings with the best light performance available. commercial earrings usually consist of diamonds of inferior quality as customers aren’t as particular about the 4 c’s in selecting fashion jewelry as they tend to be when shopping for engagement rings. but beauty in diamond earrings is really all about the sparkle, and as such excellent cutting is critical. diamond earring styles diamond earring styles vary from classic diamond studs to diamond hoop earrings and elaborate dangle styles. and there are many types of earring settings including prong, bezel and pavé. diamond earrings are available in white and yellow gold as well as platinum. most styles made for pierced ears are secured by posts with push-on “friction” backs or by threaded posts with screw-on backs. the screw-on backs are more secure but require more time to put on and take off. in some cases they may be a little less comfortable as the threads make the posts a bit thicker and can sometimes irritate the ear. it is often possible to exchange one for the other, but threaded posts do cost more. diamond studs diamond studs are definitely the most popular choice because of their versatility – equally appropriate for the workplace as for the most upscale evening event. they come in a full range of sizes and can conform to almost any budget. diamond studs are available in basket settings with prong or with bezels. our most popular setting is the “martini earring” featuring 3 prongs and a profile that sets more flush to the ear than conventional baskets. and with minimal “hardware” more of the diamond is allowed to shine. speaking of versatility, diamond earring jackets are a fabulous addition to any pair of studs. they feature a circular pattern of diamonds with a space in the center for the stud. they beautifully enhance any type of diamond or colored gemstone stud transforming the look from casual to formal with complete ease. many whiteflash customers prefer to create their own special diamond earrings by choosing certified diamonds and the earring settings separately. in this way the earrings created can feature ideal hearts and arrows diamonds from our expert selection category, or from our internationally acclaimed a cut above® super ideal diamond brand- the “best of the best”- available exclusively at whiteflash. when buying both a whiteflash diamonds and settings, labor to set the diamonds is complimentary and is done by our own master craftsmen. no matter the size, style or price point you choose, a pair of sparkling diamond
