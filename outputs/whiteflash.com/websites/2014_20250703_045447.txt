gold and platinum.  many handsome gents wedding rings today feature diamonds or other gemstones in the designs. some couples enjoy having rings that match one another stylistically, while other couples allow one another the design freedom to find the style that matches their individual taste and lifestyle.  being totally comfortable with your choice is obviously very important for that
