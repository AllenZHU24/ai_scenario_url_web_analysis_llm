current conversion rates. shipping rates displayed above are an estimate. the available shipping methods and rates for your cart will be displayed during checkout. delivery fees: oversized items and items with additional delivery fees are not included in the order subtotal when calculating the cart shipping rate. delivery fees are not included in free shipping promotions. what delivery options are available? standard shipping: deliveries are made in 3 to 5 business days after the day it ships out. rural and remote areas may incur longer delivery times. holidays and unexpected weather may impact delivery times as well. economy shipping: for free shipping promotions only deliveries are made between 5 to 7 business after the day it ships out. rural and remote areas may incur longer delivery times. holidays and unexpected weather may impact delivery times as well. need it faster? 3 day express - arrives 3 business days after the day it ships out. 2 day express - arrives 2 business days after the day it ships out. next day - arrives on the next business day after the day it ships out. orders shipping outside of the contiguous us may take additional time. please note: deliveries can occur as late as 8pm local time for the delivery location. where can we ship to? we can ship most* products to the following areas: contiguous united states alaska & hawaii u.s. territories canada *certain items have shipping restrictions that may limit where they can be shipped. please check the product page for any listed limitations. what if my order contains personalized items? orders for personalized items may require 7 to 14 business days of processing time depending on quantity and type of item. processing time is the period from when the order is placed to when it leaves our warehouse. expedited shipping options are available in checkout. expedited shipping will not speed up your personalized item's processing time. personalized items are not returnable or exchangeable. please review the product's shipping information while shopping or during checkout to obtain estimated delivery dates for these items. what are dropship or third-party items? dropship or third-party products are items that ship directly from a third-party fulfillment center. this merchandise will arrive separately from other items on your order and depending on your location may take additional time to be delivered. please review the product's shipping information while shopping or during checkout to obtain estimated delivery dates for these items. do you ship to po boxes or military addresses (apo/fpo)? yes, we do! please review the information below for any restrictions that may apply to your order. orders shipping to a po box cannot ship via any express method. hazardous items (such as helium tanks) cannot be shipped to a po box or military address. apo/fpo/dpo delivery times for these addresses are dependent upon location. orders to po boxes will incur an additional service charge and take an additional 2-3 business days to arrive, please supply a physical address for faster delivery and to avoid the surcharge. please check the product page for your items to ensure your item can be shipped to your po box or military address. how do i determine when i will receive my order? each product detail page contains information about an item's estimated delivery. once your order is submitted you will receive emails with tracking information for each package in your order. shipping rates and policies are subject to change without notice. contact
