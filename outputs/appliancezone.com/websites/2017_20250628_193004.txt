spend (excluding any shipping fees, taxes & discounts). you must be logged into your account at the time of purchase to be awarded points for placing an order. how can i use my points that i've earned? as you accumulate points, you can use them to redeem appliance zone rewards. you can monitor and use your points to redeem rewards by clicking on the appliance zone rewards tab on at the bottom of your browser. terms and conditions points cannot be awarded for purchases made prior to setting up an appliancezone.com account. you must have an account set up and be signed in at the time of purchase in order to accumulate and redeem points. for every $1 spent, 10 points are awarded to your account. points are not awarded for shipping charges. points do not expire and can be redeemed at anytime, as long as the account remains active. points can only be awarded and redeemed for purchases made on appliancezone.com. international orders are exempt points received for purchases are awarded to your account once the order ships. once your order ships the appropriate amount of points will be applied to your points total and can be redeemed for future orders.  if an order is canceled or refunded, the points that were awarded for that order will be deducted from your rewards points total. points and rewards are to be accumulated, redeemed and used exclusively by the account holder. redeemed points cannot be used against orders requiring special shipping authorization, such as international shipments. appliance zone reserves the right to revoke any points and
