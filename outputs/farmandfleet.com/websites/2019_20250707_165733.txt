price, our price match only applies to the price of items we sell, and not to the price of the installation, repair, delivery or assembly of an item when comparing a competitor’s on line price to our in store price, any applicable shipping charges will be included in the total price we match our competitor’s price must be valid at the time of the price match request the item we are price matching must be in stock in our store where the customer requests the price match. no rainchecks will be given. our price match is for in-store purchases only and does not apply to online orders. our price match does not apply to mail-in offers or instant rebate offers. our price match does not apply to offers related to the bundling of items. our price match does not apply to preowned, used or refurbished items our price match window is limited to 30 days from date of purchase our price match does not apply to competitor pricing based on subscription saving programs or offers our price match does not apply to pricing only available to select groups of customers including loyalty offers, and government special pricing offers our price match does not apply to prices offered in error by our competitors our price match does not apply to any financing offers, free items, items that are advertised as limited quantity, open-box items or credit card our price match does not apply to items sold by or shipped from online marketplace sellers or other third-parties online (for example, ebay) our price match does not apply to competitor pricing on clearance items, special orders,
