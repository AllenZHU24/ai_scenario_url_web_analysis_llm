account & ordering faqs product faqs material & care faqs about laser engraving terms & statements privacy policy dogids return and exchange policy at dogids, we work hard to only offer products that are built to last and want you to have the best experience possible with the products you receive from us. contact us using the form below if you are not 100% happy with your purchase and we will work to make it right. some of our personalized products cannot be exchanged or returned evenly because of the way it has been personalized. depending on the product, there may be a resizing or remake fee to get a different style or size. therefore, we like to set up exchanges and returns on a case by case basis so that we can give you the best option for your situation. *please contact us before sending the product back, as not all of the products come from the same location. exchanges: need a different size or color? we can work with you to set up an exchange, resizing or in some cases, remake. if exchanging due to size, item must be in new, re-sellable condition. returns: returned items must also be in re-sellable condition for a full refund. customers are responsible for the cost of shipping the product back to us unless the return is due to our error. once your item is returned, we will issue a refund for the cost of that item. the refund will be issued onto the payment method used for the order as soon as we process the return. we will also email you a return confirmation email for your records. please note, our credit card processor will only allow us to submit refunds for purchases made within 60 days of the refund. if you are returning an item purchased outside of that timeframe, we may have to issue you a refund check. dogids return or exchange request to request a return or exchange, please fill out the contact form with your name, email address, order number (if you have it) and specific details about why you need to exchange or return your item. we
