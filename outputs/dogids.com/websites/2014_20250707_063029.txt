service team if you are ever unhappy with one of our products and we'll work with you to make it right! returns: need to return an item? contact our customer service department and we'll get you the return authorization information you need to send the product back. customers are responsible for the cost of shipping the product back to us. once we receive it, your credit card will be credited the original purchase price, plus sales tax if applicable. we will also email you a credit confirmation email for your records. we do not charge restocking fees for returned items. exchanges: need a different size or color? contact us and we'll send you the exchange instructions. if exchanging due to size, collar must be in new, re-sellable condition. we do not charge restocking fees for product at dogids, we use a high power laser to engrave our tags which actually vaporizes and removes the metal so it is actually engraving instead of marking or laser bonding. this allows us to engrave deeply into the metal with pristine detail and a nice contrasting engrave color...excellent looks with a true engrave that will never wear off of your dog's tag. laser engraved dogids tags, collars, and leashes are guaranteed for the life of your pet. if the tag becomes unreadable, contact us to get a replacement. if your order arrives with incorrect engraving and we are at fault, we will also
