and operated business with humble beginnings. starting out with just a handful of violins being sold online, we now ship out thousands of musical instruments worldwide, providing personalized service and support to customers from our austin office. if you're looking for an alternative to big box retailers that still provides you with the best prices possible, you've come to the right place! from guitars and amplifiers to orchestral and wind instruments, we carry an extensive selection of music instruments from trusted name brands like fender, takamine, washburn, and yamaha. in addition, we have gear for all your professional audio needs, including microphones, pas, mixers, computer interfaces, and portable recorders from industry-leading brands like casio, korg, m-audio, and shure. commitment to customer service our sales and customer service teams are here to provide you with guidance and support from a musician's perspective. whether you're buying your first guitar or putting together a professional recording studio, our goal is to exceed your expectations of an online shopping experience. we offer a variety of shipping options to ensure that your order is delivered as quickly as possible, and if you're in the austin area you can also pick up your order locally. if for any reason you are not 100% satisfied with your purchase, we have a generous return policy that is the best you'll find anywhere. safe, secure online shopping shop with confidence! your online order is transmitted over a secure internet connection and we never share your information with
