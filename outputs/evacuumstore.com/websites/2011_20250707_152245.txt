and thousands of different models to choose.  every home owner and business has a different surface to clean.  allergies, asthma, pets, flooring, price, and weight can be deciding factors to help you select the correct vacuum cleaner for your needs.  going to a large department store and asking for assistance with the vacuum cleaners can usually waste your time. the store clerks typically have very little knowledge in regards to vacuum cleaners and may not question your specific needs. finding the right vacuum cleaner can have a few variables.  you need to determine if you will be using the vacuum cleaner for household or commercial use. you will then need to determine what type of furnishings you need to clean such as carpets, area rugs, hardwood, tile, stone, furniture, or a combination of all or some of the furnishings.  based on what you will be cleaning, you may or may not need a vacuum cleaners with attachments.  some vacuum cleaner have full sets of attachments while others have none. then you will need to determine the style of vacuum cleaner that you are looking to use, such as a canister or tank vacuum cleaner, a upright vacuum cleaner, hand held vacuum cleaner, wet dry vacuum cleaner, central vacuum cleaner.  within the styles there are several types of vacuum cleaners, such as hepa, light weight, heavy duty, pet friendly, cordless vacuum cleaners.
