simply posting a list of vacuums and related parts online for web surfers to browse without a guide, the creators of evacuumstore.com realized early on that there existed a gap between the ask or how to ask them. so, instead of creating a website that would serve as no more than an online shelf of vacuum parts, evacuumstore.com was founded to serve the customer, provide them with the information they sought, and help them obtain the parts they needed for their vacuum or find the perfect new vacuum for their home or office. by offering live support and direction in finding the correct parts and accessories from our very own customer service and technical teams, in addition to maintaining low prices on brand new products, online shoppers can now use evacuumstore.com as the
