shipments to apo/fpo addresses take 4-6 weeks. please note that this is the time it takes your order to reach the centralized apo/fpo address, and we cannot guarantee delivery times to specific military installations. however, we ship via usps parcel post, and most orders arrive in a timely manner. shipments to u.s. territories take 3-6 weeks. we ship to the following u.s. territories: puerto rico, u.s. virgin islands, guam, marshall islands, palau, america samoa, federated states of micronesia and northern mariana islands. while we cannot guarantee delivery times to u.s. territories, we ship via usps parcel post, and most orders arrive in a timely manner. 2nd day air for orders shipping within the contiguous u.s., the 2nd day air charge is 25% of the total merchandise cost, with a $14.95 minimum. for orders shipping to alaska and hawaii, the 2nd day air charge is 30% of the total merchandise cost, with an $18.95 minimum. orders arrive in 2-3 business days. 2nd day air is not available for p.o. boxes and apo/fpo addresses. next day & 2nd day orders placed by noon pt will be shipped the same business day. gift cards will be sent separately from the rest of your order at a cost of $4.99 per shipment (whether you order 1 or 50 gift cards). if 2nd day air is not available on one or more items in your order, the entire order will arrive by standard shipping. these items are noted with a shipping restricted icon on the product detail page. next day air next day air charges are 30% of the total merchandise cost for all orders, with a $24.95 minimum. orders arrive in 1-2 business days. next day air is not available for alaska, hawaii, p.o. boxes and apo/fpo addresses. next day & 2nd day orders placed by noon pt will be shipped the same business day. gift the entire order will arrive by standard shipping. these items are noted with a shipping restricted icon on the product detail page. priority mail priority mail charges are 25% of the total merchandise cost for all orders, with a $14.95 minimum in the contiguous u.s. and $16.95 minimum in alaska and hawaii. priority mail is offered for orders shipping to alaska, hawaii, and p.o. boxes and apo/fpo addresses within the contiguous u.s. your order will arrive in 3-5 business days.
