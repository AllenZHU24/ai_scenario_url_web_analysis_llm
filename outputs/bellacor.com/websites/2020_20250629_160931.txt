fixtures for your space while we’re proud to offer a vast assortment of lighting fixtures, we also realize that with endless options comes endless decisions. before settling on a single design, it’s important to look at some different factors. first, you’ll want to consider the size of the space you’re working with to best determine what type of light it requires. smaller spaces are best served
