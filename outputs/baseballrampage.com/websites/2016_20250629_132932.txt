catcher's gear sets, large bags, and smaller training aids. x-large items are very heavy or over-sized bags, gear sets, buckets of balls, and training aids. an order's size class is based on the largest class of product in the order, and the quantity of the products. the largest item in an order will determine it's class. if your order contains 3 or more medium items, it is considered large. if your order contains 2 or more large items, it is considered x-large. each item's shipping class is listed on the product display page above the description and under the specifications section. free & discounted regular shipping baseball rampage offers free regular ground shipping on orders over $99.00. this is up to an $8.95 value. small or medium size class orders shipped to the continental us will ship free. larger class orders and orders shipped to non-continental us addresses will receive an $8.95 discount on their shipping charge. canada shipping all canada orders are shipped via usps priority mail. estimated delivery dates will range from 14 to 18 days depending on your province. due to manufacturer restrictions we cannot ship all brands international. products who's manufacturer do not permit shipping to canada will have a notice on the display product page under the specifications section. apo & fpo shipping all apo/fpo and puerto rico orders are shipped via usps priority mail. there may be slightly longer delivery times based on location and processing through customs. when checking out, please choose standard shipping (see table above for rates). shipping notes team or bulk shipments will be reviewed by management and additional shipping fees may apply. overnight, 2nd day, and express guaranteed orders for in-stock items received by 3:00pm est monday - friday are shipped the same day. orders received after 3:00pm est, or saturday through sunday ship on the next business day. baseball rampage uses federal express or the united states post office depending on region and selected shipping method. however, if you wish to leave us notes regarding use of one particular carrier, you may do so in the "special instructions" field at checkout. fedex is unable to delivery to po boxes. so standard shipping through usps is the only shipping method availiable when shipping to po boxes. we will advise you before processing your order in the unlikely event of delays due to manufacturer back-orders, etc. shipping and handling charges include actual shipping charges, as well as order processing, labor and materials charges associated with
