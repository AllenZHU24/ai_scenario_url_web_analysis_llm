undergarments). you can also use string, and then line it up against a yardstick. if your measurements fall in between sizes, order the larger size (unless you prefer a snug fit vs. a generous fit). 1 - bust: while wearing your usual undergarments, measure under your arm around the fullest part of your bust. 2 - waist: measure around the narrowest point of your natural waist (usually at your navel). keep one finger between the tape and your body. 3 - hip: stand with your heels together and measure around the fullest part of your hips. 4 - inseam: using a pair of pants that fit you well,
