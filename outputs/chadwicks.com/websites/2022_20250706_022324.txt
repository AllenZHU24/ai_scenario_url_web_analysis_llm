accessibility statement at chadwicks, we are committed to making our website and our services accessible to all of our guests, including those with disabilities. we follow universal design principles in an effort to ensure that the site is flexible and adaptable to different users’ needs or preferences and is accessible through a variety of different technologies, including mobile devices or assistive technologies. we regularly test and monitor our website in an effort to maintain accessibility. web content accessibility guidelines our efforts are guided by the web content accessibility guidelines, version 2.1 aa (wcag 2.1 aa). we will continue to enhance our digital properties to deliver an accessible experience. please note that we may be limited in our ability to ensure accessibility of components or content on the website provided by third parties. provide feedback please contact us if you have questions, suggestions, or concerns regarding accessibility (specify the web page/url if your inquiry concerns particular pages on the site). also, in the unlikely event that you are unable to obtain information or complete a transaction on the website due to an accessibility issue, let us know and we will do our best to get you what you need by some other means. timeless classics. exceptional quality. women's clothing in misses, petite, plus, tall sizes.
