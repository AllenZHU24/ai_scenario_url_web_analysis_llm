beltoutlet.com was founded in naperville, il in 1997 as a small family business. our company’s founder decided to partner his knowledge of retail buying, manufacturing and leather goods/accessories with his entrepreneurial spirit to create a new company. the company began selling product in several high volume flea markets in the midwest before selling through ebay. sales on ebay launched our website that has grown from storing product in the family basement to expansion that includes a multi-unit warehouse facility that now houses almost all operations.with a commitment to exceptional customer service and affordable, quality goods, beltoutlet.com went online with its own web store in 1999. almost 20 years later and with millions of customers served, the same commitment continues to define beltoutlet.com. whether shipping overseas, providing belts to major motion pictures, or helping you find that perfect gift, beltoutlet.com still offers exceptional service to our customers,
