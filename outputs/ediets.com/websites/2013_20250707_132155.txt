is an online who’s who of the hottest celebrity “slimmers” and world-renowned weight loss experts – along with many of the products and diet plans they’ve successfully used to take off the pounds, improve their health, and get in shape. the new ediets platform is compiled, designed and written in a tabloid style that’s as entertainingly informative as it is accessible. when a member subscribes to a healthy weight plan, he or she generates a personalized profile. nutrition insights™ technology analyzes food items in a database, including recipes, and a meal plan is created. meal plans are determined based by three dietary guidelines – clinical, goal and preference. clinical guidelines are sourced directly from major health organizations or institutes for specific health conditions or considerations. goals include losing weight or eating sustainably. preferences include gluten-free, vegan, etc. our partner foodcare provides the nutrition insights™ technology, and works with a range of partners to provide reliable personalized dietary guidelines from healthcare organizations and registered dietitians. the new ediets platform is available on the web as well as on tablets and smartphones, with mobile optimized functionality. we have been offering subscriptions to our online weight-loss program since 1998, when we launched our first diet plan. ediets is a brand of as seen on tv, inc. (otcqb: astv), is the leading multichannel distributor of as seen on tv products and services and serves up fun for engaged consumers worldwide.  celebrities and industry experts often
