our design services team is there to help you create stationery supplies that speak to you.frequently asked questionswhat can i do with personal stationery? custom personal stationery can be for you or a great gift for a loved one. it can include notepads, letterheads, thank you cards, notebooks and more. stationery can be used for everyday correspondence or special messages, like birthday wishes or condolences, but can also be used for personal tasks like journaling or to-do lists. other related stationery items like return address labels and personal envelopes can elevate correspondence while showcasing your unique style.how do i customize my personal stationery? vistaprint’s design tools make creating custom personal stationery for you or a loved one super easy. first, you’ll choose a stationery type. you can select from our stationery categories, like personal journals, notebooks and more, or start fresh with your own design. second, upload your design or choose a design template. you’ll also be able to choose your paper stock and quantity. if you have any questions or just need some design help, visit our help center. finally, make any adjustments to your design and preview to make sure it will be printed just how you envisioned it. now you can add additional stationery pieces to match, or head straight to checkout.how should i select my personal stationery?for impression. for personal journaling or daily note taking, you may prefer custom post-it notes®, notebooks and a pen or pencil. for letter writing to friends and family, thank you cards and colored
