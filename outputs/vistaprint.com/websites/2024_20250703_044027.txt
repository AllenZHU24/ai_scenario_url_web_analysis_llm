questionshow do i design custom christmas cards? start by selecting one of our templates in the gallery above or upload your own design (you can find downloadable templates for each size in the product options section below). if you decide to browse our gallery, you can upload your photos or a business logo to preview them on all our different templates. next, we’ll take you into the design studio. here you can personalize your card by adding images, names, greetings and more. if you need help at any point, our design team is available to assist you at every step of the way. oh, and don’t forget envelopes! after you complete your design, you’ll be presented with a range of envelope styles, each perfectly sized for your card.what are your most popular christmas card sizes?our most popular size card is 4.6" x 7.2", which comes in a flat or folded style. most customers using one or more photos choose a flat style. but if you don't want to use a photo or want room for messages, folded cards are great, too. our folded cards arrive flat with a score for easy folding and are available in rectangular or square sizes. (note: square-shaped cards require additional postage when mailed through the united states postal service. check your local post office for pricing.)when should i send my christmas cards? according to our etiquette guide, the ideal time to mail cards is 2-3 weeks in advance of the holidays. with that in mind, we recommend placing an order before the end of november. that gives you ample time for mailing once we’ve printed your cards and shipped them to your address. and if you do run short on time, we offer express and rush delivery.how do i ensure i am sending the best quality photo christmas cards?to ensure the best print quality, we recommend using a bright photo that has not been cropped. we also suggest you use the largest file size possible to maintain detail. between your gorgeous images and our design options, you can look forward to a beautifully crafted christmas card that not only shares your holiday greetings, but also leaves a memorable impression on every recipient.why should i choose vistaprint for my christmas cards? with over 30 years of print expertise, we’ve printed millions of stunning christmas and holiday cards (literally!). when it comes to
