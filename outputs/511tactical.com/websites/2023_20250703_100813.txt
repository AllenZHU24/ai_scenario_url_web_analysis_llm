return policy returns & exchanges what is your return & exchange policy? you may return your eligible merchandise to 5.11 for a refund or exchange within 90 days of receipt. all closeout items are final sale and not eligible for return. shipping and handling fees will not be refunded. refunds will be applied to the original payment method used at the time of purchase. purchases made online at 511tactical.com may also be returned at a 5.11-owned retail location so long as you provide proof of purchase and have the original payment method (e.g., the same credit card you used to purchase the product). if original payment method was paypal or affirm, refund will be issued as store credit in the form of a gift card. please allow five to ten business days from receipt of your returned item(s) for your refund to post. what items are eligible for returns and exchanges? please be sure to follow washing and handling instructions carefully, as 5.11 does not offer returns or exchanges due to the mis-washing of products defective merchandise will be exchanged at the current value unless accompanied by receipt. to make an exchange online simply return your item(s) using reason code “exchange” and add a note indicating the exchange item you desire. an alternative would be to complete an exchange at one of our 5.11 owned stores. defective 3rd party product must go through manufacturer. returned items will be inspected and non-defective merchandise must be returned in original, unworn condition. underwear and face masks are considered final sale and therefore cannot be returned. to protect the authenticity of our patches, all sales are final on patches. we accept returns for refund or exchange for patches that are damaged or defective only. other items marked “final sale” and/or promotional items (e.g., free gifts-with-purchase, buy-one-get-one-free items, etc.) may not be returned. altered/embellished items are not eligible for return. gift cards may not be returned or refunded, unless required by law. 5.11 does not accept returns or exchanges from purchases made outside of the united states. how do i make an online return? 1.) click here to initiate a return. 2.) follow the steps on your return instructions. how do i check the status of my return? click here to check the status of your return. business to government (b2g) returns if you placed an
