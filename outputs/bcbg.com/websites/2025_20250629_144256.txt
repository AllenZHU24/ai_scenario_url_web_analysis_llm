celebrate today’s dynamic woman by creating elegant collections that infuse our romantic heritage with a modern twist. founded in los angeles in 1989, bcbgmaxazria was named for the popular french expression “bon chic, bon genre” — which conveys the idea that a good attitude is the basis of good style. 1989 – 1994 max azria launches bcbgmaxazria and opens first headquarters in downtown los angeles.the first freestanding bcbgmaxazria store opens in brentwood, ca. bcbgmaxazria store opens in san francisco, ca. 1995 max azria is awarded california designer of the year. bcbgmaxazria footwear collection launches. spring campaign featuring charlize theron. 1996 bcbgmaxazria debuts its first women’s collection during new york fashion week. 1997 – 1998 bcbg.com establishes its online presence.bcbgmaxazria launches sunwear and swimwear collections. 1999 bcbgmaxazria launches handbags and small leather goods. 2000 – 2002 bcbgmaxazria launches eveningwear collection. 2003 bcbgmaxazria bridesmaid dress collection debuts.the brand launches its first shop‑in‑shop. 2004 – 2007 bcbgmaxazria launches signature handbags, watches, outerwear, and optical.bcbg.com officially becomes an e‑commerce destination. 2008 – 2009 bcbgmaxazria celebrates its 20th anniversary: "be chic by giving." 2010 bcbgmaxazria launches a full bridal collection with accompanying accessories and footwear. 2011 bcbgmaxazriagroup is an official sponsor of the 2011 billboard music awards and hosts the after-party. the brand launches its fragrance, bcbgmaxazria. 2012 – 2013 michelle obama wears bcbgmaxazria during campaign speech at memorial hall. bcbgmaxazria launches its 2nd fragrance, bon chic and 3rd fragrance, bon genre. bcbgmaxazria launches outerwear, hats and scarves. 2014 – 2016 bcbgmaxazria and the whitney museum of american art hold a vipopening night party for the 2014whitney biennial in new york city.bcbgmaxazria celebrates its 25thanniversary. 2017 – 2018 introduction of 10 new product categories. bcbgmaxazria and flaunt throw a hollywood party to honor olivia culpo. 2019 – 2022 bcbgmaxazria opens 3 flagship stores in mexico. introduction of 6 new product categories. bcbgmaxazria features adriana lima and rosie huntington-whiteley as the faces of the brand. 2023 bcbgmaxazria launches limited-edition collaboration with celebrity stylist maeve reilly.
