diamonds tiffany diamonds are brighter and more vibrant because we craft them that way. the combination of tiffany’s exacting standards and unparalleled craftsmanship produces the most beautiful diamonds in the world. only the most expertly crafted diamonds earn the right to be placed in the legendary tiffany blue box®. the 4cs every diamond, like a human fingerprint, has certain distinguishing characteristics. the 4cs—color, clarity, cut and carat weight—are the globally accepted standards for assessing the quality of a diamond. cut cut is determined by how a diamond’s facets interact with light. learn more color color refers to the natural tint inherent in white diamonds. learn more clarity clarity is a measure of the purity and rarity of the stone. learn more carat carat denotes the weight of a diamond. learn more engagement ring styles and settings modern or vintage-inspired setting? diamond or simple platinum band? round, princess or cushion cut? our tiffany diamond experts will help you narrow down your options from diamond shape to ring setting. book an appointment responsibly sourced we believe fine craftsmanship starts with a respect for our natural world, responsibly sourcing high-quality raw materials and continues through to the creation of the final piece of jewelry. from the moment they are unearthed, tiffany diamonds selected for our engagement rings never leave the hands of highly skilled artisans, who work with passion and precision to unleash intense vibrancy and light. once a rough diamond is deemed worthy of the tiffany name, it is delivered to our diamond cutting experts and our master craftspeople. tiffany is proud to employ more than 1,500 expert cutters and polishers in our own state-of-the-art workshops to ensure our sourcing practices honor the places and people that provide us with such astonishing beauty. we go above and beyond the kimberley process—with our own chain of custody controls and by advocating for stronger global standards—to ensure that human rights and the environment are protected. explore our diamond source initiative home love & engagement guide to diamonds: 4cs diamond education sign out
