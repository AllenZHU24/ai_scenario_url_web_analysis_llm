us tequipment.net is one of three divisions of interworld highway, llc. iwh is a well established, authorized manufacturer / distributor of major brands of test equipment, interactive whiteboards, overhead projectors and educational technology products. our mission our primary business objective is to increase business by providing excellent service to our clients around the globe. in addition, we aim to provide competitive pricing in all our products through price match guarantees and special offers. company history founded in 1969 as shortess-rawson associates inc., our primary business was technology education and test equipment sales. as shortess-rawson, we became a leading retailer in the test and measurement industry, proudly supplying companies like jvc, sony and panasonic with test equipment. in an attempt to provide our customers with better products and more options, we added additional interactive whiteboard lines like polyvision, softboards, microtouch, numonics and more. due to our relationship within the educational market place shortess-rawson was selected as a smartboard distributor in 1997. today our family of companies has experienced significant growth over the past few years and currently has 46 employees, a well stocked inventory area and well over 35 million dollars annually in sales. our strong internet presence is complemented by outside sales support. we pride ourselves with our product knowledge, strong phone support and speedy customer service. we offer delivery services through a variety of nationwide carriers, and transporters. in addition, we offer hand-delivery to have your purchases delivered to your door in a timely fashion. we exhibit and participate at major regional and national trade shows such as: njsba, ahr, globalcon, nfmt, iste, neca, pete & c and nyscate, to name a few. we understand the importance of connecting our customers with the friendly faces that represent our company.
