price. 800-860-3577 call chat email need a price match? call, live chat or email us for assistance. all you need is the link to the competing retailer's product. or visit us in store store hours & directions please note: competitor's price is subject to matching (including any shipping and handling charges). competitor must be an authorized dealer (as listed by the manufacturer). customer must present a link to the lower-priced product for a price match. rewards and points programs do not apply. product must be in-stock at the competing retailer and be new merchandise. no other offers or discounts may apply to price match. one time price match applies within 30 days of purchase. price matching does not apply to parts, in-store only*, limited quantities or specials. manufacturer instant rebates or package bundles are not eligible for price matching, as these promotions are available to new purchases only within the specified promotional time frame. these special offers will not be honored outside of their promotional periods. *"in-store only" refers to products that are only available to purchase in store and are not available for purchase on abt.com. these categories include but are not limited to: select custom av products, select home security systems, garage doors and more. to inquire further about in-store only items, please call 800-860-3577. footer content
