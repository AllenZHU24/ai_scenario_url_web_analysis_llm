technical support award winning service why shop abt you're more than just a customer…you're a member of our family. abt electronics has been around since 1936 and is a family owned business with a 493,400 square foot facility sitting on 68 acres in glenview, il a suburb of chicago. abt is a winner of the better business award for market place ethics, and member of several business associations. as one of the largest independent retailers of consumer electronics and major appliances, abt's goal is complete customer satisfaction, from start to finish. remembering bob abtphoto galleries authorized online dealer being an authorized online retailer allows abt.com to provide you with a higher level of protection for your goods and assures you the best possible service available online. award winning customer service abt.com customers have toll-free access to highly trained, award winning customer service and technical advisors for solutions for a variety of questions. contact abt customer support with any questions you have during your shopping experience. call us toll free at 888-228-5800. in addition, you can live chat, e-mail directly to our sales and customer service staff, tweet us, write on our facebook page, or leave a message on our blog. go ahead, contact us, we are here to help you get your questions answered correctly, promptly. learning center abt wants customers to learn everything there is to know about the products they love. whether you're looking to take advantage of new features, or expand your knowledge to become an informed consumer, abt's learning center has guides designed to inform you through every step of the purchase. the abt youtube channel presents the newest products, reviews, video buying guides, and a series of how-to videos, helping you every step of the way. tired of looking through that pile of old product manuals? you can find full manuals on their respective product pages throughout abt.com. free shipping abt currently offers free shipping on thousands of items on our website. just look for free shipping logo on the product page to see if what you want qualifies for free shipping. check out our shipping offers and policies for more information. low price guarantee abt electronics always strives to give you the lowest possible price. if you find a lower price on any product from a legitimate retailer, or if your item has dropped within 30 days of original purchase, please call us at 888-228-5800. our low price guarantee means our customers get the best price on their favorite products. flexible returns & exchanges if you're not completely satisfied with your merchandise consult our our return & exchange policy. within 30 days or the purchase, we will arrange your choice of a refund or exchange. customers are responsible for covering the return shipping cost. secure shopping guarantee abt electronics has gone to great lengths to make sure your shopping experience is both secure and safe. our privacy policy and security policy are designed with your personal safety in mind. abt electronics does not share any of your personal information provided online without your permission. free technical support abt electronics also has an authorized service center onsite for each of the brands we carry. each manufacturer has certified our repair technicians to fix their products if a problem arises. when you purchase from us, your merchandise can be repaired directly at our store or at any other authorized service center in the country. green program as a family-run, family-oriented business, abt electronics is committed to making the environment cleaner and safer for the benefit of our customers, our employees, our children, and generations to come. in 2008 abt opened its 10,000 square-foot recycling center and kicked off a series of green initiatives. the recycling center takes in over 240,000 lbs of styrofoam, 10 million pounds of appliances and 3 million pounds of electronics each year. in 2010, abt electronics efforts earned it cepro's greenest a/v retailer in the country award. as recently as 2016, abt completed construction on our rooftop solar array, which provides 850-megawatts hours (mwh) of power per year. that’s enough electricity to run 80 homes for a year! abt's green program is focused on improving the recycling practice of consumer electronics, improving the environmental impact of our building, incentivizing employees to make green-conscience choices, and developing a sustainable fleet of delivery vehicles. our goal...complete
