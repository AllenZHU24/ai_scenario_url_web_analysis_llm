include stairs). this service does not include unpacking, plugging in, installation or debris removal. white glove shipping this is a special service for large and overweight items . it includes normal delivery plus un-crating, setting your goods in place, and removing the packing material. this service does not include installation of any type or delivery up stairs. it normally takes between
