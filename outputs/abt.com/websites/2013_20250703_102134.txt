some reason your paperwork gets lost in the mail, you'll need this information to have your rebate reprocessed. it's also a good idea to have this information in front of you if you need to call the customer care phone number listed on your rebate form. check your postmark date! most rebates require you to postmark your submission within 30 days of the date of purchase. if you miss this deadline,
