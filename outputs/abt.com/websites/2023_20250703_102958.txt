extended return period through january 15th, 2024. satisfaction guaranteed or your money back. every product we sell can be returned for a full refund, some category exclusions apply. extended return products must be sealed and unopened. abt is committed to making you happy before, during and after every purchase. if you are not 100% satisfied with your merchandise, you can request a return or set up an exchange within 30 days* (exceptions below). all returned or exchanged items should be in the same condition they were received and include all original packaging. items that are worn or damaged may incur a restocking fee or may be rejected from being returned or exchanged based on condition. how to return your item: step 1 log in or go to your account to access the order and select the items you want to return. items must be in their original packaging and condition. step 2 print your return shipping label and attach it to your original abt box or any shipping box. remove old shipping labels or cover the original tracking numbers entirely. step 3 drop off your box at any authorized ups shipping center. we recommend that you return items via ups insured. find a ups shipping center near you. start a return once our returns department has received and inspected your return, your refund or exchange will be issued within two business days. abt has a 30 day return policy on most
