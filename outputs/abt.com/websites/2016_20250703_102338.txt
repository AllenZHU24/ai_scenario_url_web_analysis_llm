returns & exchanges abt electronics is committed to making you happy before, during and after every purchase. if you are not 100% satisfied with your merchandise, we'll gladly exchange it or give you a refund for the full purchase price paid, whichever you prefer. if you live in the chicago area, you can return or exchange your merchandise directly to our store. customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the product arrives damaged or defective. returns as easy as 1-2-3 step 1 log in or go to your account to access the order and select the items you want to return. items must be in there original packaging and condition. start a return step 2 print your return shipping label and attach it to your original abt box or any cardboard shipping box. remove old shipping labels or mark out the original tracking numbers. step 3 drop your box off at any authorized ups shipping center. we recommend that you return items via ups insured. find a ups shipping center near you. start a return once our returns department has received and inspected your return, your refund or exchange will be issued within two business days. if you have an overweight/oversized item that was shipped to you via a freight/ltl carrier, abt will arrange for the carrier to pick the product up directly from your home. the return shipping price will be sent to you before it is picked up. if
