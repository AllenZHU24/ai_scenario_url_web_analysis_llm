guarantee price match guarantee policy we are very proud of our pricing and service. we are happy to offer a price match guarantee to make sure that when shopping at blue bath you will always get the best possible price available online at that time. if you find a lower price than ours on the internet for an identical item currently in stock at a recognized online retail store, you may be eligible for a price match or a discount up to the time a payment is made for your order. in order to submit a price match request please send us an email to info@bluebath.com with the web address of the lower price product. we will consider your claim and reply with either a price match or an explanation if rejected. blue bath will make every effort to match or beat competitor’s pricing as long as all of the following are met: conditions the competitor must be an authorized dealer of the product. auctions or individuals are not accepted. the competitor must have an identical product “in-stock” when the price match is requested. the lower price must be published on the competitors website. the competitor’s price cannot be conditioned with membership fees, coupons, product combos or rebate offers. the product on the competitor’s website must have a published true list price and be priced accurately. calculating the competitor's total price must include shipping and handling charges and tax. exclusions products by companies with internet minimum asking price.
