shop now remove hair from carpets and upholstery – without tangles conventional turbine tools have brush bars which hair wraps around. the dyson tangle-free turbine tool doesn’t have a brush bar. instead, it has counter-rotating heads with brushes to remove hair and dirt from carpets and upholstery. the flexible heads bend to maintain contact across uneven surfaces, and more hair is released straight into the bin than with any other mini turbine tool. it’s the only turbine tool that doesn’t get tangled. shop now mess-free dog grooming the dyson groom tool removes loose hair directly from your dog before it’s shed around the home. it then self-cleans – sucking hair from the bristles straight into your vacuum. no mess. shop now tools for hard to reach places dyson engineers have developed tools that make it easy to clean dirt from awkward places. these tools include the multi-angle brush that twists to clean on top of hard to reach surfaces, and the flexi crevice tool, which bends and flexes to clean around corners and in tight gaps. shop now tools for removing dust and allergens dyson tools are engineered for removing dust and allergens from around the home. these tools include the mattress tool, which has lint pickers on its front and back edges that gather, ball and then release fibers into the airflow, where they are sucked away. shop now the science behind removing allergens from the home the dyson microbiology lab was set up in 2001, and contains the kind of equipment more commonly associated with hospital laboratories. dyson scientists use the lab to research ways of eliminating allergens to make the home cleaner and safer for allergy sufferers. to do this they cultivate and study household allergens. they even have a dust mite farm – the allergenic mite feces are used for cyclone and performance testing. removing stains and grime zorb™ and dyzolv™ have been specifically formulated to remove stains and grime from floor surfaces. zorb™ carpet maintenance powder can be used to keep new carpets looking new without the drawbacks of wet cleaning. dyzolv™ spot cleaner contains four active agents to remove spots and spills from carpets and rugs. shop now tools for all surfaces there are dyson tools for cleaning different types of surface all over the home – up top, down below and in-between. the soft dusting brush has soft bristles to gently clean surfaces and furnishings such as lampshades, blinds and computer keyboards. the stiff bristle brush dislodges ground-in dirt from carpets. and the articulating hard floor tool has soft brushes to gently remove dirt from wood and other delicate floor surfaces. shop now dyson wet wipes for use with the dyson hard™ cordless vacuum cleaner. wood nourishing wet wipes. shop now hard floor
