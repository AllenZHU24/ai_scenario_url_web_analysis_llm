about us - who is amsan? about us promoting a cleaner & healthier working environment amsan® is america's leading supplier of janitorial, cleaning, and facility maintenance supply products and is committed to bringing new standards of quality and value to customers. we offer products, programs, and solutions that enhance customer productivity, efficiency, and cost control. with multiple distribution centers across north america, amsan provides the highest quality service with the largest expert field team in the industry. amsan has over 300 field consultants available to you every day, providing solutions to your facility through the amsan proforma® program that includes our amsan® smart assessmentsm and amsan university®, as well as brand name and renown® products, environmentally preferred, green seal certified and energy star certified product choices. we're out to make a difference in the cleanliness and quality of your environment while supporting your operational efficiency and bottom line. these are tall orders requiring knowledge, expertise, resources, and focus. we are the leading solutions-based provider in the industry. we offer high quality products and resources that help our customers better manage total costs. our services and cost-saving programs are designed to help you enhance efficiency, improve quality, and increase productivity.
