mission the public affairs office is responsible for informing and educating the public about the mission, policies and activities of the army & air force exchange service. the office communicates to the public and exchange stakeholders through internal (department of defense) and external news outlets. the office responds to media queries, publishes news releases, issues public statements, arranges interviews and advises the exchange senior leadership and staff on all aspects of media relations and external affairs communication issues. the public affairs office ensures accurate information is provided to the public and release of information complies with applicable laws, regulations and policies. sign up to receive exchange news releases. to follow the exchange public
