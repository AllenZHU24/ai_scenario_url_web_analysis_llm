make sure our emails are not being blocked by either your internet service provider (isp) or your email program. this may be caused by a security setting or "junk email" and "spam" filters. in order to receive our emails, you may have to revise these settings or contact your isp to allow mail from "support@artbeads.com" to be accepted in the future. if you have checked your junk email folders and do not have an order confirmation email, please call us at 866-715-2323. i have received a shipping confirmation email and would like to know where my order is. did you get a tracking number?
