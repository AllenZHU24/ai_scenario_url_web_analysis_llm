total annual contribution limits remain the same. what types of iras are eligible to transfer or rollover? all iras (traditional ira, roth ira, sep ira, simple ira, solo) are eligible for direct rollover. ask your ira custodian for details or any limitations. can i open a new silver or gold ira with a contribution? yes, you can open a new ira with a contribution and your annual contribution metal etf? etfs, or exchange traded funds, are most popular with traders and investors who need to quickly move in and out of markets. physical metals are favored by those who buy and hold. if you own
