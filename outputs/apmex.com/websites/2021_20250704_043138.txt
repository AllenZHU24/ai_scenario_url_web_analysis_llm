ira-eligible products for products to qualify for a precious metals ira, they must meet minimum fineness requirements as set by the irs. gold must be at least .995 fine (the american gold eagle is the only exception) while silver must be at least .999 fine. all platinum and palladium must be at least .9995 fine. popular ira-approved products include favorites like american gold buffalo coins, american silver eagle coins, australian platinum koala coins, and canadian palladium maple leaf coins, in addition to trusted bullion bars and rounds. shop ira products precious metals ira frequently
