shipping options does limoges jewelry ship internationally? do you offer rush delivery? how much does shipping cost? we charge a flat shipping rate based on the total amount of the merchandise you are ordering. shipping and handling is calculated after any coupons or discounts have been applied. please see the chart below for details: merchandise total shipping & handling orders less than $75 $5.99 over $75 free! will there be sales tax on my order? a sales tax of 9.50% will be applied to all orders shipping to illinois. a sales tax of 7.0% will be applied to all orders shipping to tennessee. how can i track my orders? click here to to check on the status of your order. how long will it take for my order to arrive? for non-personalized and selected engraved items, please allow 48 hours for shipment and up to 1 week for standard delivery. personalized jewelry takes a bit longer, as every item is made to order. please allow 2-3 weeks for shipment of your personalized order. items from the express ship collection will be shipped via ups next day air for arrival up to 4 business days from order date and usps priority or ups 2nd day air for arrival up to 5 business days from order date. remember, all of our personalized jewelry is manufactured to your exact specifications and a careful, thorough process takes a little extra time when done right. how will my order be shipped? generally, items ship via the united states postal service (usps) via first class mail. personalized items are shipped separately so there may be a slight gap between deliveries of items on multiple item orders. how will i know when my order ships? when your order ships, you will receive a shipment notification email. this email will provide you with the date your order was shipped. how long will it take for my order to arrive once it has shipped? the majority of our jewelry is shipped through the united states postal service. orders shipped via usps may take 4 to 12 business days to arrive. when you select the express ship option, items will be shipped via ups next day air for arrival up to 4 business days from order date or usps priority or ups 2nd day air for arrival up to 5 business days from order date. personalized items are shipped separately, so please be aware that not all items from one order will necessarily arrive on the same day. does limoges jewelry ship internationally? we ship only within the united states and canada at this time. do you offer rush delivery? yes! for a one time charge all express shipping items in your order will be shipped as noted below. usps priority shipping: $9.99 our least expensive express shipping option. all express ship items in your order will ship via the us postal service and should arrive within 6 business days  (usps does not guarantee delivery date). this option is trackable via the order status page. ups blue 2nd day air : $17.99 all express shipping items in your order are guaranteed to be received up to 5 business days from order date. this option is trackable via the order status page. ups red next day air : $26.99 all express shipping items in your order are guaranteed to be received up to 4 business days from order date. this option is trackable via the order status page. please note that only items with the express shipping truck icon can utilize the express shipping services. please select usps priority to express ship your order to a p.o. box.
