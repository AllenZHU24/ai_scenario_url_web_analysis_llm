accessories. melissa sweet wedding dresses - modern romantic brides inspired by the well-made things of the past are sure to fall under the spell of our melissa sweet collection. the graceful gowns dresses - when you dream of your big day, do you envision luxurious, elegant details that your guests will remember forever? if so, our oleg cassini collection is made just for you. these timeless dresses - if you love fashion and drama, you'll want every detail of your wedding to be sophisticated and glamorous, starting with your gorgeous gown. the galina signature collection features rich,
