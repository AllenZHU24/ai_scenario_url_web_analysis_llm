1950, david's bridal is now an international wedding dress destination with more than 330 stores throughout the united states, canada, mexico, and the united kingdom. along the way, we've stayed true to our guiding principle that each bride deserves the wedding of her dreams. meet the db community > we know a bit about making dreams come true: in fact, one in three brides in the united states purchases her dress from david's bridal. we wouldn't be where we are today without the generations of loyal brides who have come to us for beautiful, high–quality wedding dresses—including exclusive designer styles from vera wang, oleg cassini, zac posen, and melissa sweet—in a full range of sizes at prices that make getting married easier to afford. more about our quality > creating head–to–toe bridal looks is just the start, however. we're also experts at outfitting every member of the wedding party, flower girls, moms, and special guests with the same level of care and know–how offered to our brides. our breathtaking selection of dresses in an array of styles and colors, including party, occasion, and prom dresses, coupled with personalized style advice, expert alterations, and finishing touches like lingerie, shoes, and accessories, make us the one–stop bridal shop that millions of brides and their families have trusted for over 65 years. plan your appointment > frequently
