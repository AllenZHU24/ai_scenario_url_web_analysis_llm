photos of products are a representation of the item you will receive and may or may not be the actual item you will receive. please use the photos as an indicator of the general quality of the item you will receive and review the product page for any additional details regarding the condition of the item. all prices are subject to change due to market changes. we reserve the right to refuse an order if the quoted price of an item is incorrect do to typographical error, technical or system error. we reserve the right to accept or reject any order. once an order is placed through providentmetals.com, you have entered into a legally binding agreement. if you chose to cancel your order for any reason, you will become subject to providentmetals.com’s market loss policy. which will include a fee as well as the price difference between your confirmed price and providentmetals.com’s current ask price. the following payment options are available bank wire- any order amount credit card- any order amount (subject to 2.6% processing fee) paypal- any order amount (subject to 2.6% processing fee) personal check- orders $1-$2,000 cashier’s checks- orders $1- $2,000 money orders- orders $1- $2,000 if you do not submit payment in full within 48 hours for bank wire payments or within 5 business days for personal checks, cashier’s checks or money orders we may at our discretion, cancel your order and implement the conditions of our market loss policy. all cashier’s checks, bank money orders and personal checks are held 10 business days or until funds clear. due to recent changes in privacy laws, many banks cannot provide verification of funds which may lead to longer personal check processing times. if this is unacceptable to you please take advantage of electronic or bank wire payment options. most banks offer bank wire processing through any branch location. see your local bank for further details on wiring procedures. all orders paid via credit card, paypal, postal money order or bank wire are processed immediately. we offer a refund on non-bullion returned items up to 10 days from the day you received items. items are not returnable if they have been removed from original or shipped packaging. all returns on credit card orders will be charged a 4% re-stocking fee. shipping charges are not refundable. bullion items are not returnable, but may be refunded only at our current “bid” price. shipping policies orders are shipped only after receipt of good funds. this is instantly for orders paid by bank wire, credit card, paypal or postal money order. orders paid by personal check, cashier’s check or bank money order are held until funds clear (typically 10-12 business days). a majority of orders are shipped via usps and are fully insured. all packages require a signature to be received. all packages shipped via usps will be shipped first class, priority or express mail. all orders totaling $2,000 or more will be shipped usps priority registered mail. depending on the shipping service used, a package tracking number may be provided. all packages are shipped securely in inconspicuous packaging and give no indication of contents. boxes are addressed from “provident”. some oversized packages may be shipped federal express or ups. at this time we do not ship internationally. this is a service we aim to offer our customers shortly. we offer flat rate shipping based upon each order’s subtotal. shipping rates are as follows: order sub-total shipping rate $0-$99.99 $4.95 $100-$499.99 $13.95 $500-$999.99 $18.95 $1,000-$24,999.99 $22.95 $25,000-$100,000 $34.95 please note- shipping rates do not include the 2.6% processing fee that is added to orders paid via credit card or paypal. market loss policy when purchasing from, or selling to, provident, once your order is confirmed, the price is locked in for you and the transaction may not be canceled, but may be offset at our current “ask” or “bid” price. any deficit between the price at which provident sold or purchased the product(s) to or from you and the applicable offsetting ask or bid price is your responsibility. that amount plus a $35 administrative fee will be charged to you. any market gain on such offsetting transaction(s) shall remain the sole property of provident. privacy policy provident respects your privacy. we will never willfully disclose individually identifiable information about our customers to any third party. privacy philosophy provident respects your privacy. provident does not sell, rent, or loan any identifiable information regarding its customers to any third party. any information you give us is held in the utmost care and security, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented. use of information gathered provident will not collect any personal information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. examples of such information are: name, postal address, email address, phone number, and credit card information. this is needed in order to complete your orders. provident may share non-personal aggregate or summary information regarding its customers with partners or other third parties, but provident does not sell or share any information at the individual customer level. subject to applicable laws and regulations this
