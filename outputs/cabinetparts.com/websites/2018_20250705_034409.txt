cabinetparts.com provides free hinge matching no other component is more important to the look and function of your cabinetry than having the proper hinge. that’s why we offer the largest variety of european, traditional & special application hinges from all the major manufacturers. helping you select the correct replacement hinge is our specialty! at cabinetparts.com, we know the correct method to identify and match your hinge application. plus, we have the largest inventory of "hard to find" hardware available to get your cabinet doors back on and hanging in the correct position. get started now by simply emailing us the following information to: matching@cabinetparts.com tell us the brand name of your hinge. provide all identification numbers shown (imprinted) on the hinge and base plate. take pictures of the hinge to be replaced and attach to the email. photo should include the top, side and bottom as shown below. providing the measurement of your hinge overlay is critical to the proper fit. watch this how-to video to ensure accuracy. provide us with your full contact information, (name, address, phone, email) and the quantity of replacement hinges needed. be sure to replace all the hinges on a given door at the same time to ensure the best overall function. please note: manufacturers have changed hinge designs over the years and therefore the screw hole placement can not be guaranteed.if you need any additional assistance, you can call us at 877-445-3583. if you prefer "snail mail", you can send us your replacement hinge and we will match it for you by hand.
