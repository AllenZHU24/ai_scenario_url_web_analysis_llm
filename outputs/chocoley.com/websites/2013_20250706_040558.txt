(example: a package shipped on monday that takes 2 business days would arrive on wednesday, the second business day after the ship date.) ** for fedex ground home delivery, a business day does not include sundays, mondays or holidays. (example: a package shipped on friday that takes 2 business days to arrive would arrive on tuesday, as you do not count the day shipped as a business day, saturdays do count, but mondays do not count as a business day.) we now also offer usps priority mail as a shipping option orders that ship usps priority mail will ship within one business day. usps does not guarantee delivery times, and packages shipped via usps are not insured. do not select this method of shipping if you must have your package by a specific date. apo/fpo military shipments must ship via usps. puerto rico shipments must ship via usps. the map below is a general representation of transit times with fedex shipping for a specific zip/postal code to zip/postal code transit time, please use the get transit time
