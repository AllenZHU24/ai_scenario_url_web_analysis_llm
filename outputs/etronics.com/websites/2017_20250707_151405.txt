personalize the information you see on this site. even more so, the ip address is used to help identify you, your wish list and you're past purchases. the ip address is not at all linked to any personal identifiable information. we keep this information for our internal use; in order to personalize your use of the site. for example, in the future, we may recommend products or features you may like based on what you have liked in the past.our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. we collect visitor's contact information (like their email also personalize your shopping experience by using your purchases to shape our recommendations for electronics, music, games and other merchandise that might be of interest to you. if a user elects to use our "email a friend" program for informing a friend about our site or products, we ask them for the friend's name and email address. etronics will automatically send the friend a one-time email inviting them to visit the site. etronics stores this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email. this email address is removed form our database once the email has been sent. when you sign up for our newsletter, we need only an e-mail address--which is used to send the information requested. when subscribing to the newsletter mailing list, you will not receive separate emails from other marketers as a result. you might receive offers from our company including those made through the newsletter itself, which, of course, you have elected to receive. customers requesting a newsletter may receive emails notifying them of our special promotions and deals. if you choose to be removed from the newsletter please click on the unsubscribe link from the actual newsletter. our site uses cookies to store information. cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. we use cookies to remember your name and your customer id number. this allows you to leave our site, come back later, and still view contents in your wish list and/or purchases made in the past. these cookies allow us to better understand, in aggregate, the preferences of our users and thereby create new services that better attend to those preferences. most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that. even without a cookie, you can still use most of the features in our store, including placing items in your shopping cart and purchasing them. special relationships/third
