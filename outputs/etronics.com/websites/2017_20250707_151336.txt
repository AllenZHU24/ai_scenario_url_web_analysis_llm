buyer is responsible to pay for the return shipping. etronics is not financially responsible for the cost of return shipping back to our warehouse. please contact our customer service department via this link to receive an rma number to affix to the outside of the package to be returned. all original parts, pieces, and packaging is required to be sent back when returning an item. the returns
