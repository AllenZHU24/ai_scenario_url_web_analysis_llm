december 18th. click here for more details. back to top 01. can i preorder items for delivery? yes. however these will be on items that are specifically indicated as a preorder; this generally applies on new releases, we will only accept orders on items that we have in stock at the time. back to top 02. can i change my order? once the order has been placed, it cannot be changed online. if you want to change your order, please telephone on 800 422 8027, and if it has not yet been processed we will change or re-issue a new amended order. back to top 03. do you offer gift wrapping options? unfortunately, at this time we do not offer gift wrapping options. back to top 04. can i return items to a store? yes, with the exception of notified non-returnable0 goods. you must provide proof of purchase such as a delivery invoice or your email order confirmation. your goods will be taken and your exchange or refund will be processed on the card you used to place your order. back to top 05. when do i get my refund once i have returned an item? once we have received the item into our warehouse we aim to process a refund onto the original payment card within two weeks. back to top 06. if i return all the items in my order will i still be charged for delivery? if any of the items we deliver are damaged or incorrect, we will refund the delivery charge. if you change your mind about wanting to keep them, you may return them free of charge but we will not refund the original delivery charge. back to top 07. what should i do if my credit/debit card has been refused when placing an order? check that all the details you have entered on the checkout page are correct. if it is refused after that, try another credit card. back to top 08. do i get charged for delivery of each item i order? postage and packaging for each gift basket is charged for separately but all other items will attract one delivery charge, no matter how many you buy. back to top 09. do you deliver overseas? with the exception of gift vouchers back to top 10. i have a complaint, who should i speak to? if it is about a product you have bought from our stores, please get in touch with the store you purchased from – the telephone numbers of all our stores can be found on this website. if the complaint is about an online shopping experience please call one of our customer service representatives on (800) 422-8027 or complete the form on the contact us page. back to top 11. what do i do if my product breaks down during the guarantee period? if products are under guarantee, your order confirmation email/delivery note is your proof of purchase/receipt and you should follow the manufacturer’s instructions for notifying the manufacturer of any problems within the guarantee period. back to top 12. do you offer discounts on corporate or bulk orders? we offer discounts for deliveries to the same address over certain order values. please telephone on (800) 422-8027 to discuss your requirements. back to top secondary navigation contact us faqs privacy & security privacy policy ordering changing/canceling orders valid payment methods checkout shipping information about shipping
