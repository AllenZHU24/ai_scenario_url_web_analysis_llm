01. can i preorder items for delivery? yes. however these will be on items that are specifically indicated as a preorder; this generally applies on new releases. back to top 02. can i change my order? once the order has been placed, it cannot be changed online. if you want to change your order, please telephone on 800 422 8027, and if it has not yet been processed we will change or re-issue a new amended order. back to top 03. do you offer gift wrapping options? yes we do offer gift wrap on some products. there is a $3.99 flat charge for gift wrapping per item. gift wrap charges not refundable. back to top 04. can i return items to a store? no, items purchased on www.harmankardon.com must follow return policy instructions. back to top 05. when do i get my refund once i have returned an item? once we have received the item into our warehouse we aim to process a refund onto the original payment card within two weeks. back to top 06. if i return all the items in my order will i still be charged for delivery? ground shipping is free on all orders.  expedited shipping cost will be refunded in full as long as return request is within 30 days of shipping. back to top 07. what should i do if my credit/debit card has been refused when placing an order? check that all the details you have entered on the checkout page are correct. if it is refused after that, try another credit card. back to top 08. do i get charged for delivery of each item i order? ground shipping is free on all orders.  expedited shipping charges are calculated by total order weight and service availability. back to top 09. do you deliver overseas? no. back to top 10. i have a complaint, who should i speak to? if it is about a product you have bought from our stores, please get in touch with the store you purchased from – the telephone numbers of all our stores can be found on this website. if the complaint is about an online shopping experience please call one of our customer service representatives on (800) 422-8027 or complete the form on the contact us page. back to top 11. what do i do if my product breaks down during the guarantee period? if products are under warranty, your order confirmation email/delivery note is your proof of purchase/receipt. please call customer service at (800) 422-8027 to set up a warranty replacement. back to top 12. do you offer discounts on corporate or bulk orders? we offer discounts for deliveries to the same address over certain order values. please click here for more details. back to top secondary navigation contact us faqs privacy
