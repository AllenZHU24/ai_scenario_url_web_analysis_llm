you will receive an order confirmation email from cole haan. this will be sent to the email address you provided when you placed your order. the email will contain your order number and a summary of your order. please print and retain for your records. if for any reason you did not receive a confirmation email, please contact customer service at: 800-695-8945. how do i cancel or modify my online order? due to the processing times of our orders, we are unable to cancel or change orders once they have been placed. why was my order cancelled? if you’ve received notification from cole haan that your entire order or an item in your order was cancelled, we apologize for the inconvenience. this can occur for certain reasons. please contact customer service with questions or to place a new order. payments how can i pay for my online purchases? colehaan.com accepts visa, mastercard, american express and paypal. we also accept cole haan gift cards. do i have to pay sales tax when ordering online? cole haan is required by law to charge sales tax on all purchases that are shipped to states where cole haan has retail stores. some states tax shipping charges, as well as the purchase total. sales tax on shipping is not refundable on returned merchandise. please note that order totals reflect estimated tax. the actual charge to your credit card will reflect all applicable state and local taxes calculated at the time your order ships. can a credit card issued by a foreign bank be used for website orders? at this time colehaan.com does not accept international credit cards. in order to place an order on colehaan.com, you must have a billing address in the united states. if you wish to purchase on www.colehaan.com and do not have a united states billing address, you may also sign up for a myus.com account, or use paypal to purchase on our website. my order was not processed and there is a charge on my account. when will the charge be removed? if you used a credit card or debit card the charge you see is actually a pre-authorization hold. this is a temporary electronic authorization. the card will not be charged unless the order ships. if you did not complete the order, the hold will drop off your account according to your credit card or bank policy. this generally happens within 2 - 10 business days. for more information, contact your credit card company or bank to find out when the hold will be released. if you used paypal, the charge you see is actually a pre-transaction notification. this notification will drop off your paypal account within 30 days. contact paypal directly to find out exactly when the pending notification will drop off. products do you allow price adjustments on recently marked-down items? a price adjustment will be given if a price is marked down within 14 days of purchase and if the style is currently available. this accommodation excludes any warehouse sale items, final sale items and previously discounted items. please contact customer service to obtain an adjustment within 14 days of your purchase date. how do your online prices compare with what i will find in stores? prices displayed on colehaan.com are the same as cole haan full-price retail stores. where can i buy cole haan optical frames? cole haan optical frames are available only at fine eye care professionals nationwide. they are not available online at colehaan.com or at retail stores. where does cole haan manufacture its products? cole haan luxury products are manufactured in the world's finest factories using the highest level of artisan craftsmanship and superior materials. many products are handcrafted using time-honored techniques. presently, products are crafted in the united states, italy, brazil, india, mexico, vietnam, china and india. what if the product i want is no longer available online? please use the search box on the website to look for a product. if the product or a specific size is not listed, we are able to search a cole haan retail store for you. please email customer service with 'retail search' in the subject line, along with the following information: your name, telephone number, size, style number or style name/color of the product. we will contact you after researching the availability with any product and store information. how do i choose the right size gloves? to send to multiple addresses, you must place one order per address. what are my shipping options? we offer a variety of shipping methods to get you your merchandise in time. please visit our shipping options page for more information. does cole haan ship internationally? at this time, cole haan ships to the united states and canada. additionally, we are able to ship to other countries using our partnership with myus.com. please visit our international shipping options page for more information. how do i track my package once it has shipped? cole haan ships your order via ups. once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from cole haan, containing a tracking number. your delivery can be tracked at www.ups.com.if you are a registered member on colehaan.com, your order history is available in the my account section of the website. if you have not registered with cole haan prior to placing your order, and do not have your shipping confirmation email, you may contact customer service to assist you with tracking your package. how will my product be packaged during delivery? cole haan packages your order by hand with great care. each cole haan product is packaged to protect during shipment in order to ensure it arrives in excellent condition. how much is shipping? we offer a variety of shipping methods to ensure your merchandise will arrive in time.  please visit our shipping options page for more information. returns/exchanges what is your exchange process? we want to ensure your cole haan product is just what you envisioned and fits you perfectly. to exchange an item, please refer to our returns policy and process. what can i do if i received the wrong merchandise or a defective product? if your online order contains a product that has a material or manufacturing flaw, please email us or call 800-695-8945 so that we may make arrangements to pick up the product for inspection and better assist you. gift cards may i use my cole haan gift card online? yes. gift cards purchased at any cole haan retail or cole haan outlet store may be redeemed online by entering the card number during checkout on the shopping bag page. likewise, cole haan gift cards purchased through our website may be used in any of our retail or factory outlet stores. what if i have more than one gift card? you may use up to 5 cole haan gift cards per online purchase. please enter one gift card number at a time, and choose the "apply card" button between each. restoration services does cole haan restore handbag and shoes? cole haan offers a handbag refurbishment program that can assist with hardware replacement and refinishing. cole haan also offers a restoration program for our men's and women's footwear.  our talented team can replace soles or perform a full restoration. if you are interested in this service, please click here for more information about restoration and repairs, or call 800-890-9440 to learn more about the program. what is the best way to care for my shoes? cole haan offers a variety of shoe care products both online and in our retail stores. products include: brushes, leather conditioners, suede protectors, cleaners, heel and sole edge dressing, polish and creams. before using any care product, always test a small, inconspicuous area of the shoe or accessory to ensure the color is not affected. click here for our collection of leather and shoe care. what is the best way to care for cole haan products? visit our leather and shoe care collections for our recommended leather care products, which will help to extend the life of your cole haan footwear or handbag. we recommend professional dry cleaning for cashmere and wool garments and accessories. check the garment label for instructions regarding special care. leather and suede outerwear should always be professionally cleaned, stored in an unzipped bag and kept away from direct sunlight. locations where is cole haan located?
