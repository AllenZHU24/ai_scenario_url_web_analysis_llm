exchanges return policy every cole haan product is backed by our long standing tradition of quality and craftsmanship. cole haan will accept any unworn or unaltered merchandise within 30 days of the original purchase date. cole haan reserves the right to deny refunds for a returned item that does not comply with this policy. for products purchased on www.colehaan.com, original shipping charges and any shipping taxes are non-refundable. please allow 2-3 business days to process your return once received at our warehouse. you will receive a refund based on the original form of payment. please allow up to one billing cycle for the credit to post to your account. to return or exchange an item, please pack it in its original condition. in-store returns of online product merchandise purchased at www.colehaan.com may be returned to any cole haan retail store for your convenience, free of charge. click on process a return/exchange below to get started please note: canadian, paypal, amazon, affirm, international checkout and final sale, orders cannot be returned to any cole haan retail store. online orders shipped to us addresses may only be returned to cole haan retail stores in the united states. easy returns & exchanges we get it – it has to be perfect. if you need a different color, size or style, we offer free returns and exchanges by mail or in store. to get started, click on process a return/exchange below. we’ll guide you through a few easy steps to complete! process a return/exchange find a store near you please note, prepaid return labels will be generated only for those orders and items that comply with cole haan’s return policy. if you need further assistance please call 800-695-8945. exchanges for online orders are processed in two steps. just let us know what you’d like when you return your original item(s). you will receive a confirmation email when we process your return and a shipping confirmation email for the exchange items when they’re on their way. unfortunately, item availability is not guaranteed – if the item you want is not in stock, we’ll contact you to let you know. exchanges are not offered on paypal, affirm or amazon orders. cole haan retail or outlet store returns cole haan stores can only process returns from purchases made at our retail and outlet stores, or www.colehaan.com as stated within our policy. all other requests should be directed to the original place of purchase. all full-price in-store purchases must be returned to cole haan full-price retail stores only. all outlet purchases must be returned to cole haan outlet stores only. all cole haan items purchased outside the united states must be returned in the country where the original purchase took place. exceptions to return policy for canadian orders, please contact customer service for return authorization forms at 800-695-8945. for orders placed on amazon.com, please visit amazon.com/returns to request return authorization. for shoprunner returns please click here and sign in to access your pre-paid label. canadian, paypal, amazon, affirm, international checkout and final sale orders cannot be returned to any cole haan retail store. online orders shipped to us addresses may only be returned to cole haan retail stores in the united states. international checkout orders may not be returned or exchanged. any items purchase as final sale may not be returned or exchanged. exchanges are not offered on paypal, affirm or amazon payment orders. returned merchandise to our warehouse will be processed as a refund to your paypal, affirm or amazon account. gift returns if you wish to return a product that you received as a gift from colehaan.com or from a cole haan retail store, the refund will go to the original form of payment, i.e., the credit card used to purchase it. if you wish to exchange a gift purchased online at colehaan.com, please contact customer service at 800-695-8945 for assistance. when returning a gift, please provide the order number or purchaser name, address, and telephone number to assist in processing the return. if you return a gift that was purchased through the use of a gift card, your gift card will be restored to its original value or a new one will be provided for you, to be used at any time on colehaan.com. if you return a gift to a cole haan retail store, please bring your packing slip so you can be reimbursed for the appropriate amount. if you need any assistance or have questions about our return & exchange policy, please call us at 800-695-8945, 7 days a week, 24 hours per day.
