returns & free exchanges easy returns & exchanges we get it—it has to be perfect. if you need a different color, size or style, we offer free returns and exchanges by mail or in store. select returns may also be returned to a local ups store. to get started, click on process a return/exchange below. we’ll guide you through a few easy steps to complete! process a return/exchange find a store near you please note, prepaid return labels will be generated only for those orders and items that comply with cole haan’s return policy. if you need further assistance, please call 800-695-8945. exchanges for online orders are processed in two steps. just let us know what you’d like when you return your original item(s). you will receive a confirmation email when we process your return and a shipping confirmation email for the exchange items when they’re on their way. unfortunately, item availability is not guaranteed – if the item you want is not in stock, we’ll contact you to let you know. exchanges are not offered on applepay, klarna, or paypal orders, or orders shipped outside of the united states. return policy every cole haan product is backed by our long-standing tradition of quality and craftsmanship. cole haan will accept any unworn or unaltered merchandise within 30 days of the original purchase date. cole haan reserves the right to deny refunds for a returned item that does not comply with this policy. for products purchased on www.colehaan.com, original shipping charges and any shipping taxes are non-refundable. please allow 2-3 business days to process your return once received at our warehouse. you will receive a refund based on the original form of payment. please allow up to one billing cycle for the credit to post to your account. to return or exchange an item, please pack it in its original condition. in-store returns of online product merchandise purchased at www.colehaan.com may be returned to any cole haan retail store for your convenience, free of
