cooker – model bpr600xl domestic 6qt. programmable stainless steel electric pressure cooker hazard: the sealing gasket can be incorrectly inserted upside down on the lid which can allow the unexpected release of built-up pressure. this poses a risk of burns to the user or consumers nearby. consumers should immediately stop using the recalled pressure cookers until they receive a new gray lid gasket and updated instructions. remedy: repair if you would like to order your free new gray lid gasket with updated instructions, please click on the following link: http://www.brevilleusa.com/lidrecall please see the video below for proper installation and operation of the lid and gasket. consumer contact: for consumers that prefer to contact us via phone, please call the breville usa dedicated recall helpline at 1-800-983-3165 from 6a.m. to 5p.m. pt (monday through friday). recall details units: about 35,600 description: this recall involves breville 6-quart capacity domestic programmable electric pressure cookers with a brushed stainless steel construction and a 3-way safety system. the safety system consists of a locking lid, safety valve and a pressure release button. pressure cookers with model number bpr600xl and batch numbers between 1235 and 1529 are included in the recall. the model number and batch code are located on the back of the unit. sold by: bed, bath & beyond,
