everyone has their own hair journey and the process of discovering what works with your specific curl type can take years, even decades to master. we’ve gathered all the pro tips, products, styles, and tricks so you can skip the long journey and go straight to gorgeous curls. hair type chart 4c 4b 4a 3c 3b 3a 2c 2b 2a no two curl patterns and no two curl stories are exactly the same. your curl pattern is beautiful and unique to you. take the first step in your carol’s daughter curl journey by selecting the strand below that most resembles your curl pattern. 4c 4c hair type your tight, densely packed coils are naturally the most prone to shrinkage, giving you a sponge-like texture. 4c curls can be extremely dry, brittle, and prone to breakage without the proper moisturizing routine. 4c learn more shop for 4c hair type 4b 4b hair type your curls resemble a zig-zag pattern, which can make your 4b hair prone to dryness, tangles, and even occasional breakage. keeping them hydrated and free of tangles can allow your 4b curls to flourish. 4b learn more shop for 4b hair type 4a 4a hair type 4a curls are dense with a very visible coily pattern. your hair without oils and hydrating creams can lack curl definition making it prone to dryness. 4a learn more shop for 4a hair type 3c 3c hair type your tight, corkscrew-like ringlets are densely packed and have the most volume within the type 3s. your curls are prone to dryness and tangles, which means they thrive when moisturized. 3c learn more shop for 3c hair type 3b 3b hair type your curls are springy with lots of volume. 3b curls can range from ringlets to corkscrews and need hydration to keep their definition. 3b learn more shop for 3b hair type 3a 3a hair type your large, loose curls have a very defined “s” shape. 3as biggest issue is battling frizz and maintaining shine. 3a learn more shop for 3a hair type 2c 2c hair type your s-shaped wave pattern starts at your roots. your wave pattern is thick and the most defined of the 2 types. unwanted frizz can be a common issue among this hair type. 2c learn more shop for 2c hair type 2b 2b hair type still pretty flat at the crown like a 2a, your s-like patterned waves are easy to style but are prone to frizz and tend to lose definition easily. 2b learn more shop for 2b hair type 2a 2a hair type your waves tend to be straight at your crown and form a slight
