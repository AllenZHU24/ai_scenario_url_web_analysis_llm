join the nordy club apply for a nordstrom credit card how you earn points you earn points every time you shop at nordstrom, nordstrom rack, hautelook or trunk club. and those points add up to nordstrom notes that you can spend on anything (yes, anything!) in stores or online at nordstrom, nordstrom rack, hautelook or trunk club. $1 = 3 points nordy credit cardmembers, for every dollar spent with us. $1 = 2 points nordy debit cardmembers. $1 = 1 point base earn rate when you pay with cash, check or a non-nordstrom card. redeeming your notes is easy! to redeem your nordstrom note at trunk club, simply enter your notes reward number and access code online or in the trunk club app during checkout. notes can also be redeemed in-person at one of our clubhouse locations. to use your notes at nordstrom, just use the nordstrom app to redeem a $5, $10 or $15 note. or you can wait until you earn 2,000 points, and a $20 note will be automatically added to your account. we'll let you know when your $20 note is ready if you're signed up for nordstrom emails. unlock more benefits with each status want more info?
