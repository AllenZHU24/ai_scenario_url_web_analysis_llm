for html and css. the site displays correctly in current browsers and using standards compliant html/css code means any future browsers will also display it correctly. while gear patrol strives to adhere to the accepted guidelines and standards for accessibility and usability, it is possible that you may occasionally find an area of the gear patrol not as accessible. while this may be a
