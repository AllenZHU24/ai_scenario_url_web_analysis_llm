having trouble logging into my account? coldwater creek who is coldwater creek? coldwater creek is a catalog and online specialty retailer of women’s apparel, accessories and home goods. it is the next evolution of the coldwater creek brand that many loyal customers have come to know and love for more than three decades. coldwater creek remains focused on its core customer by offering many of the same beloved styles along with unique and new looks. where do i sign up for coldwater creek email promotions? you can sign up to receive emails by going to the bottom of our home page and clicking "sign-up." don't miss out on special offers and news on the latest styles! does coldwater creek have a digital catalog? yes! enjoy browsing our online catalog by going to the bottom of our home page and selecting "view catalog online." how do i request a coldwater creek catalog? we are happy to send you a catalog – request one here. a catalog will be mailed to you within 7-10 business days. how do i follow coldwater creek on social media? coldwater creek has a large and loyal following on facebook, instagram, pinterest, and twitter. join them by following, liking and sharing what makes you a coldwater creek fan! are you going to open stores? for the foreseeable future, coldwater creek will strictly be a catalog and online business. this is how the brand started over 30 years ago, and how we believe we can best serve our customers. can you honor a previously issued coldwater creek gift card, store credit, groupon or other coupon? unfortunately, we cannot. before relaunching, coldwater creek inc. went through a bankruptcy process in 2014, which makes it impossible to honor any financial agreements made by the previous company. will you have a loyalty program similar to the original onecreek® program offered by coldwater creek? it is certainly something at the top of our list for 2016. stay tuned! are there career opportunities with coldwater creek? coldwater creek has many exciting and rewarding career opportunities at all levels in its hingham, ma headquarters. the company offers a competitive compensation and benefits package. we encourage you to stay in touch with us on our careers page for news and opportunities. for inquiries or to apply, please email a resume to jobs@coldwaterdirect.com. ordering do you offer livechat? no, not at this time. however, it is definitely a service that we would like to be able to offer to you in the future! for now, should you need any assistance with your order please contact one of our customer service specialists at 888-678-5576 or at customerservice@coldwaterdirect.com and they will be happy to take care of you. can i place my order by phone? yes. please contact one of our customer service specialists at 888-678-5576 and they will be happy to assist you with placing your order. how do i place an order by mail? first, please download our order form and complete all information.then mail your order form to:coldwater creek • 1176 e. dayton yellow springs rd • fairborn, oh 45324orfax your completed order form to: 513-346-2250sorry, checks are not accepted; mail and fax orders must be placed on a major credit card. for mail-in orders, we advise making a copy of your completed order form for your reference. how do i apply for a coldwater creek credit card? we are happy to welcome you to coldwater creek's credit card program where earning valuable points towards rewards certificates is easy! plus, cardholders have exclusive access to special events throughout the year. learn more and apply. how can i browse for products? the search feature at the top right of the home page makes it very easy to find what you are looking for! search for an item by entering its name or five-digit style number, or search by category (sweaters, for example) to return the broadest range of results. you may fine-tune your search by size, color, price and more, using the options shown on the left side of the page. how do i know whether an item is on back-order? if the item is on backorder, you'll immediately see a backorder message that will tell you the date that the item is expected in-stock. you may add it to your cart and complete the checkout process to reserve the item, and your credit card will not be charged until the item ships. where do i find more information about an item? detailed product information, care instructions and size availability can be found on each item page. i tried to checkout and the item in my shopping cart now says unavailable, what happened? as a fashion retailer, we offer limited quantities of our apparel and accessories each season, and items may sell out quickly. when this happens to something you've already put into your cart, it will show as no longer available and should be removed from your cart before proceeding to checkout. how will i know my order processed? after you have placed your order online and clicked the "submit" button, you will see a screen with your order number confirming that your order has been processed. you will also immediately receive an order confirmation and receipt via email. how will i receive confirmation of my order? you will receive confirmation of your order via email if you have provided us with a valid email address. how will i know my order shipped? we will send you a shipping confirmation email that will contain tracking number(s). you may also check your order status by logging into my account. click on purchases & returns to find whether or not your order has shipped, and open it to view the details. once open, you will see an option called track package(s) and this will show the tracking number. select the tracking number to open the fedex website and view the status of the package. if the order has only partially shipped, you will see the other items you ordered still processing due to a backorder, which was noted on the order confirmation email. can i change or cancel my order? you may make changes to your order only prior to clicking the "submit" button. once you have submitted your order, we are unable to change or cancel it, as we process orders immediately in order to get your items on their way as quickly as possible. i received my order, but one item was missing. what should i do? if you received your order and an item was missing (not on backorder), please call customer service at 888-678-5576 for assistance. do you honor price adjustments? yes, we do issue price adjustments as appropriate. however, your order must have been placed no more than 14 days before the price change was made. please have your invoice ready and call our customer service specialists at 888-678-5576 to see if you are eligible for a price adjustment. if a promotion or sale says it concludes at the ‘end of the day' what does that mean? end of day for us is 12:00 am pst, which ensures that our west coast customers can take full advantage of our savings opportunities. contact us how can i get assistance if i need it? you can call 888-678-5576, 24 hours a day/7 days a week or email a customer service specialist at customerservice@coldwatercreek.com. we will respond to email messages within 48 hours. payment payment options we accept the following forms of payment on our site: visa mastercard american express discover coldwater creek credit card coldwater creek mastercard® we do not accept the following forms of payment on our site: cash personal checks traveler's cheques c.o.d. paypal unfortunately, we cannot accept gift cards, gift certificates, e-gift cards, or merchandise credits issued by coldwater creek, inc. prior to october 31, 2014 due to the bankruptcy process incurred at that time, which makes it impossible to honor any financial agreements with the previous company. why are there multiple charges on my credit card for one order? your credit card company may place a preauthorization on your account to ensure the card is not fraudulent when placing your order. the preauthorization will drop off once the product ships and your card is actually charged. you may see multiple charges if your order is sent in multiple packages or if an item is backordered. sales tax to the extent that we are required by law to charge and collect taxes on products that we sell, such taxes are charged based on the tax laws applicable to the locations to which the order is being shipped or delivered. at checkout, all appropriate taxes will be added to the order total. promotions and coupons occasionally, coldwater creek will offer code-based promotions; the details of promotions and discounts will be communicated on the site, in selected catalogs and mailers, and through emails. to redeem a code based promotion, perform the following steps: in your shopping cart, enter the promotion code in the space marked "promo code." click the "apply" button on the right side of the page. prices for the items that are valid for the discount will be reduced by the amount of the promotion and reflected in your order summary. to qualify for a specific promotion, your order must be placed during the specified promotional period. promotional offers may not be combined with other promotional offers within the same order. your order may need to be of a certain dollar amount to qualify; please note that shipping fees and taxes are not applicable towards the qualifying amount. prices and shipping charges, if applicable, will be adjusted according to the applied promotion, with the discount reflected in your grand total. only one promotional offer may be used per order. offers may be used only once unless otherwise specified. free shipping offers are valid for standard shipping on orders of a specified minimum purchase. shipping shipping options coldwater creek ships to most u.s. zip codes, including alaska, hawaii, puerto rico, us virgin islands, guam and military apo/fpo addresses. standard and expedited (second-day and next-day) shipping options are available, although second-day and next-day shipping may be restricted in some locations. at this time, we are not able to ship to canada or other non-u.s. addresses. to ensure prompt delivery of your order, we recommend that you place your order by 3pm est. where orders ship to and expected delivery time:orders shipping to the 48 contiguous united states: standard, normally 4-8 business days; second-day, normally 2 business days; next-day, normally 1 business day. orders shipping to alaska or hawaii: standard, normally 11-16 business days. orders shipping to military apo/fpo addresses: standard delivery is via fedex, normally 3-8 business days (please note these orders may take up to 21 days for delivery). orders shipping to u.s. territories, including puerto rico, us virgin islands and guam: standard delivery is via usps priority delivery, normally 11-16 business days. orders shipping to p.o. boxes: standard shipping only, review may take up to a few days to appear. helpful tips for writing a review: keep your review focused on the product. avoid writing about customer service – please contact us instead if you have sizing do you offer special sizes? yes we do. we offer our products in three distinctive size ranges: misses 4-16/xs-xl, petites p4-p16/pxs-pxl and women's 18w-24w/1x-3x. talls designed for misses sizes are offered in a selection of pants to better fit customers 5' 8"-6' 0". available sizes are clearly listed for every item. where can i find size charts? our size charts are located just to the right of size range on every individual item's page. remember, numbers are simple guidelines to help you find the best fit; learning what looks best on you, regardless of size, is the secret to looking good. what is the difference between misses, women's and petite sizing? our misses sizing is proportioned for women 5' 4"-5' 8" who wear sizes 4-16. our women's sizing is designed to fit and flatter a larger woman's figure and is perfectly proportioned for women who wear sizes 18w-24w. our petite sizing is proportioned for women under 5'4" who wear size p4-p16. if i am still not sure about what size would be best, how can i get more information? please call one of our customer service specialists at 888-678-5576 and they will be happy to help determine the right size for you. remember, numbers are simple guidelines to help you find the best fit; learning what looks best on you, regardless of size, is the secret to looking good. whether you fall into one size range or a combination, we are ready to help you find the right fit! do the color swatches on your website clearly represent the merchandise color selection? yes they do. our color swatches are photographs of actual items; however, based on the merchandise fabrication as well as the display and color capabilities of your computer/tablet/mobile device, there may be instances when the color chip varies slightly from the actual merchandise. returns and exchanges coldwatercreek.com return policy your satisfaction matters! if for any reason you are not happy with your purchase, coldwater creek will
