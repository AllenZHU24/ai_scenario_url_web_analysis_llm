(shipping fee of $9.95 will be deducted from the refund on your original method of payment.) start your return return by mail when you don't mind waiting for your refund start your return online here to receive a usps mailing label via email to ship your items. box up your items in the original packaging, print out and apply the label provided, and ship them back to us. you'll get an email when your items have been received and your refund has been processed. please allow 7-10 business days for your refund to be processed.
