of unworn, unwashed, unused or defective merchandise within 90 days of purchase with a valid receipt. shipping and handling fees are non-refundable. making returns all merchandise purchases must be returned by mail: • fill out the return form enclosed with your package (clearly noting reason for return); • repackage the item(s) and attach either our pre-paid smartlabel (shipping fee of $7.95 will be deducted from the refund on your original method of payment) and give to your postal carrier/post office or use the label and carrier of your choice. we recommend using a form of traceable and insurable delivery. please allow up to 14 days to process a return. you’ll then see your refund on your credit card account within 1-2 weeks from the time your return has been processed. please ship returns to: coldwater creek returns and exchanges 1251 south perry road, suite 163 plainfield, in 46168 price adjustment policy we will gladly adjust the price of an item if it reduces within 14 days
