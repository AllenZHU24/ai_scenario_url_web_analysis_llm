authorization for your return. print the authorized return and include it in your return packaging. please send your return items and return authorization form to the address provided within fifteen (30) days of your original purchase date. i sent my return! how soon will it be processed? when we receive your return it will take 3 to 5 days to process. when processing is complete you will receive
