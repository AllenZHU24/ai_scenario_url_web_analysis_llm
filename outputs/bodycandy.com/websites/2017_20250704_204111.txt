packaging returned items must be unused / unworn returned must be received within 15 days of delivery date* *please note: a 10% fee is charged for cancellation of any and all custom or personalized orders and 25% restocking fee is charged for items returned outside of the fifteen (15) day return window. how do i return an item? email us at sales@bodycandy.com (monday - friday 9am -5pm est) with the following information: order number item to return item condition why item is being returned we will get back to you within 24 - 48 business hours. we are closed on saturday & sunday, as well as all major holidays. how will body candy resolve my return? we process store credits for the cost of the item with no exceptions. because of the nature or our product, refunds are not an option. once customer service receives your complete information and confirms that all return requirements for your order have been met you will receive authorization for your return. print the authorized return and include it in your return packaging. please send your return items and return authorization form to the address provided within fifteen (15) days of your original purchase date. i sent my return! how soon will it be processed? when we receive your return it will take 3 to 5 days to process. when processing is complete you will receive an email that confirms your return authorization and we will process your store credit for the return. body candy return policy regarding defective or incorrect items if you received a broken or defective item, please email customer service at sales@bodycandy.com (monday - friday 9am -5pm est). return policy on custom and personalized items because custom and personalized jewelry is made to order with specific customer requirements in
