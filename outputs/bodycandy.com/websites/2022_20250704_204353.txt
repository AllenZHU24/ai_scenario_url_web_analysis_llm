afterpay﻿ as their order payment method. no returns will be accepted on items made from 14k gold or sterling silver, custom or personalized items, or on items whose product number begins with  au-,g-, cu- (all sales are final on these items) a shipping fee of $4.99 will be deducted from your store credit. how do i return an item? email us at sales@bodycandy.com (monday - friday 9am -5pm est) with the following information: order number item to return item condition why item is being returned we will get back to you within 24 - 48 business hours. we are closed on saturday & sunday, as well as all major holidays. once your item is received we will inspect and notify you if your item has been approved or denied. how will body candy resolve my return? we process store credits for the cost of the item with no exceptions. because of the nature or our product, refunds are not an option. once customer service receives your complete information and confirms that all return requirements for your order have been met you will receive authorization for your return. print the authorized return and include it in your return packaging. please send your return items and return authorization form to the address provided within fifteen (30) days of your original purchase date. i sent my return! how soon will it be processed? when we receive your return it will take 3 to 5 days to process. when processing is complete you will receive an email that confirms your return authorization and we will process your store credit for the return. you will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item(s) unless the item received was damaged. shipping costs are non-refundable, you will receive store credit for the exact price you pay for the product(s) minus any fees and/or coupons used if applicable. body candy return policy regarding defective or incorrect items if you received a broken or defective item, please email customer service at sales@bodycandy.com (monday - friday 9am -5pm est). return policy on custom and personalized items because custom and personalized jewelry is made to order with specific customer requirements in
