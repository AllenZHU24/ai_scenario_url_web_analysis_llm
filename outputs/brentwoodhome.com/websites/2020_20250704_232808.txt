retardants, phthalates, ozone depleters, or heavy metals. our story for over 30 years, we’ve been making mattresses and home essentials with a team of local artisans and fabricators in los angeles. from the beginning, we’ve obsessed over creating products using healthy and ethically-sourced materials, all inspired by our love for the natural world and our home in california. "we strongly believe our environment, especially our homes, are at the center of our well-being, allowing us to live comfortable, happy lives." however, before we started the brentwood home brand,
