planet. we now use only organic certified latex and wool, from our own farms, and organic cotton — all naturally renewable resources. all of our memory foams are now formulated with plant-based biofoam®, up to up to the maximum of 20%, to reduce our environmental impact. and many of our products feature innovative materials like repreve® textiles made from recycled plastics water bottles, up-cycled cotton denim from new cutting scraps, organic coconut husk fiber, natural buckwheat hulls, drought-tolerant hemp, incredibly soft tencel® made from wood pulp, and recycled steel. it's time to rethink what's possible, to challenge the norms, and to live more sustainably. our purposegreener, healthier homesbrentwood home makes mattresses, pillows, sheets, and yoga cushions greener by using natural, recycled, and innovative materials for greater comfort, performance, and sustainability. we are raising the bar on sustainability, social responsibility and transparency. our materials healthy lifestylescalifornia ethos since 1987we’ve been thoughtfully crafting mattresses and pillows in los angeles for nearly 35 years — since 1987, using the very best domestic and international materials. we proudly uphold the california ethos for a safe, equitable, and responsible workplace in our own factory and wood shop, certified to gots organic and fsc® sustainability standards. that includes doing our part to mitigate climate change by reducing what we use and offsetting what we cannot. brentwood home is 100% carbon negative for every scope of our business. instead of planting land trees, we’re planting seatrees to help restore kelp forests in california and beyond. meet our partner, sustainable surf, a nonprofit making ocean conservation accessible to all through their community-based projects dedicated to planting and protecting blue-carbon coastal ecosystems. we’ll donate 1% of proceeds from our crystal cove line to support their mission to take action on climate change by reviving the biodiversity of our oceans. our certifications our mission: do better. use more natural, recycled, and innovative materials from the best domestic and
