usa. fair trade certified responsiblefsc® certifiedsince 1993, the forest stewardship council has been the leading authority on sustainable forest management. being fsc® certified (c156319) validates that brentwood home complies with the highest social and environmental standards for environmentally responsible, socially beneficial, and economically viable management of the world’s forests. our factory, woods, and latex forests are all proudly certified. view certificate fsc® certified coming soonrecycling claim standardswe use the rcs (recycled claim standard) to track recycled raw materials through our supply chain. the rcs verifies the presence and amount of recycled material in our final products, with third-party chain-of-custody verification of recycled material content claims on our products. the global recycle standard (grs) is similar, but applies to full products and vets social and environmental practices and chemical restrictions. brentwood home is currently finalizing both standards. recycling claim standards waste not!nearing zero wasteas part of avocado green brands, we are the first home and bedding brand to achieve the ul environment landfill waste diversion validation, on our journey toward zero waste. the ul standard requires at least 90% diversion through methods other than waste to energy. we are working hard now to reach 95% or greater diversion and achieve a zero waste validation — an incredible achievement! lab testedgreenguard goldproducts achieving the greedguard gold certification are scientifically proven (with testing in
