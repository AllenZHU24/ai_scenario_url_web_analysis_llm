home, taking care of the spaces we inhabit is the foundation of our well-being. at brentwood home, much of the product development process is spent researching and sourcing materials ourselves, making sure they meet golden state standards for environmental responsibility. learn more about the combination of natural, organic, and non-toxic materials we use to make a healthy, happy home below. southeast asia kapok with the smoothness of silk and fluffiness of cotton, our kapok is sustainable, nontoxic, and pesticide-free. new zealand wool new zealand wool liner wicks away moisture and is naturally antimicrobial for a cooler, fresher mattress. north dakota buckwheat all-natural buckwheat makes for a firm, durable fill that’s healthy for you, your home, and the planet. texas organic cotton grown without chemical pesticides or fertilizers, we use organic cotton for mattress covers and bedding. china silk silk helps to regulate temperature by keeping your mattress breathable, while repelling moisture to prevent mold and mildew. mongolia cashmere the fine fibers of cashmere are soft to the touch yet breathable, making it an excellent temperature regulator. south gate, ca coils our coils are heat-treated and stress-relieved for long-lasting support that responds to every movement. vietnam natural latex on top of being sustainable and renewable, natural latex provides incredible support in bedding. brentwood home is committed to creating the most comfortable and most natural sleep products as possible to give you your best night's sleep. shop mattresses bedding
