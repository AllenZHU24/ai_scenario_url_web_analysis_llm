gols-certified organic factorythe brentwood home factory in los angeles is a gots-certified organic facility (cu861640), ensuring the highest standards for organic materials, including ecological and social criteria, backed up by independent certification of our entire supply chain. we are publicly listed in the gots database for transparency and trust. we clearly label our materials and finished products that are certified organic. view products net-zero journeyclimate neutral certifiedas a climate neutral certified company since 2020, we've measured brentwood home's annual cradle-to-consumer emissions, offset them by investing in climate change solutions, and started taking real action to reduce our emissions. learn more ethicalfair trade certifiedfair trade makes the world a better place. when you treat farmers and workers fairly, everyone benefits. fair trade helps businesses source products that are ethically and sustainably produced while giving consumers confidence that the people behind the products they buy get a fair deal for their hard work. responsiblefsc® certifiedsince 1993, the forest stewardship council has been the leading authority on sustainable forest management. being fsc® certified (c156319) validates that brentwood home complies with the highest social and environmental standards for environmentally responsible, socially beneficial, and economically viable management of the world’s forests. view certificate coming soonrecycling claim standardswe use the rcs (recycled claim standard) to track recycled raw materials through our supply chain. the rcs verifies the presence and amount of recycled material in our final products, with third-party chain-of-custody verification of recycled material content claims on our products. the global recycle standard (grs) is similar, but applies to full products and vets social and environmental practices and chemical restrictions. brentwood home is currently finalizing both standards. waste not!nearing zero wasteas part of avocado green brands, we are the first home and bedding brand to achieve the ul environment landfill waste diversion validation, on our journey toward zero waste. the ul standard requires at least 90% diversion through methods other than waste to energy. we are working hard now to reach 95% or greater diversion and achieve a zero waste validation — an incredible achievement! lab testedgreenguard goldproducts achieving the greedguard gold certification are scientifically proven (with testing in environmental chambers) to meet some of the world's most rigorous, third-party chemical emissions standards, helping to reduce indoor air pollution and the risk of chemical exposure, while aiding in the creation of healthier indoor environments. nearly all brentwood home products have achieved this gold standard. view products shared valuesvegan certifiedthe crystal cove yoga collection is 100% certified vegan by vegan action®. this means the products are aligned with your values. our mission: do better. use more natural, recycled, and innovative materials from the best domestic and international sources. consume less energy, create less
