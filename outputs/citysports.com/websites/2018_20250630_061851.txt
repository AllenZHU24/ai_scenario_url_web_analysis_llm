standard shipping shipping rates: for all orders we offer $9.95 (ups surepost) and $9.99 (ups ground) flat-rate shipping. flat shipping rate applies only to the 48 contiguous states (alaska, hawaii and puerto rico are excluded from the offer). order processing and delivery time city sports processes orders monday through friday, between 10:00 am and 5:00 pm (est). all orders placed on saturday and sunday are processed on monday (if monday is a holiday, your order will be processed on tuesday). we might experience longer processing times during peak season or during special sales (up to 3 business days). ups / surepost shipping usually takes from 1 to 6 business days (see map below), depending upon the distance from our delivery center in ny. note that if ups / surepost shipping option is selected, usps will be responsible for final delivery. note: shipments can be sent to both po boxes and military apo/fpo addresses via usps only. ups / surepost shipping map business days in transit from new york (estimate) this map should only be used as a reference. transit days are based on estimated times. ground shipping delivery is not guaranteed in the exact amount of days shown above. express shipping do you need express shipping? no problem. you can select: next day air, 2nd day shipping or 3 day select shipping during checkout. please note that we must receive your order before 3:00pm (est) to ensure proper processing of next day air, 2nd day and 3-day shipping. none of the express shipping options include saturday or sunday delivery. to estimate the cost of these shipping options, first please add items to cart. on the cart page, enter your zip code in the "estimate shipping" section. secure payments by: back to top home my cart © 2020 city sports need help?
