cables. finally, we offer heavy duty high speed specialized cables including outdoor, direct burial, and aerial cords. ethernet cable our networking cables are perfect for your internet network! we sell cat 7, cat 6a, cat 6, cat 5/cat 5 e ethernet cable. our ethernet cables also come in a variety of formats to fit your needs such as snagless, crimped, bootless, shielded, slim, and plenum. our
