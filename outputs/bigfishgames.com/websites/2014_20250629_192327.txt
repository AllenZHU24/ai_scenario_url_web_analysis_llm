the contact us page. enabling javascript in your browser print q: how do i enable javascript in my web browser? a: javascript helps to make web pages more interactive. we use it throughout our site, and in the shopping cart when go to purchase a game. if you're browsing our website or trying to make a purchase, but get a javascript error message, it usually means that javascript is turned off in your web browser. this can happen if your security settings have changed. here's how to make sure that javascript is enabled. just choose your web browser from the list below and follow the steps. internet explorer firefox google chrome safari back to top internet explorer click the gear icon in the top right corner of your internet explorer window. you can also press the alt key on your keyboard to show more menu options at the top of the window, then click tools. choose internet options, then click the security tab. click the custom level button. the security settings window will appear. scroll down to the scripting section. under active scripting, make sure that enable is checked. click the ok button twice to confirm the change and close the pop-up windows. refresh your internet explorer window, then go back to your cart and continue with your purchase. back to top firefox click the orange firefox button in the top left of your firefox window, then click options. go to the content tab at the top of the options window. make sure that the enable javascript box is checked. click the ok button to confirm the change and close the options window. refresh your firefox window, then go back to your cart and continue with your purchase. back to top google chrome click the icon that looks like 3 stacked lines in the top right corner of your chrome window, then choose settings. you can also type chrome://settings/content into your address bar, then press enter, to get to step 4 in these instructions. at the very bottom of the settings window, click show advanced settings... in the privacy section, click content settings... make sure that allow all sites to run javascript (recommended) is checked. click done to confirm the change, then close the settings tab. refresh your chrome window, then go back to your cart and continue with your purchase. back to top safari (mac) click safari in the menu bat at the top of your screen, then select preferences. click the security tab at the top of the options window. make sure that the enable javascript box is checked. close the preferences window to save your changes. refresh your safari window, then go back to your cart and continue with your purchase. still need help? if you still have questions, please visit our contact us page. print back to top was this answer helpful? yes no please tell us how we can
