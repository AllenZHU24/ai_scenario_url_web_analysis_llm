furniture style in a small chateaux, complimented by a bottle of burgundy and aged cheese! with its slight curves and confident grace, french country furniture is almost seductive.colonial furniture colonial style furniture often features turned legs and posts, reminiscent of early american furniture. simple and sturdy, colonial furniture offers a lot of variance to suit many tastes – from a beat
