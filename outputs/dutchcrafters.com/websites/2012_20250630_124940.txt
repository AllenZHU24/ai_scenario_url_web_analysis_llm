2012 by admin reply in the summer of 2012, dutchcrafters introduced $199 flat rate shipping per order for all amish furniture to simplify shipping costs and help customers to save money. the concept is simple – buy a dining room table, pay just $199 in shipping. buy a dining room table and a set of six solid wood dining room chairs, pay just $199 in shipping. buy a dining room table, six dining chairs, a corner hutch with matching buffet, two kitchen stools and a microwave stand – yes, $199 in shipping to any of the 48 continental states. prior to the flat rate shipping, freight charges could vary widely based on quantity and size of products and the shipping destination. “it was actually a hassle for our sales team and confusing to our customers,” said dutchcrafters ceo jim miller. “we would have to get multiple shipping quotes for each customer and often we ended up with outrageous shipping costs that nobody wanted to pay – sometimes more than $1500 for a larger order to the west coast. we especially penalized customers who ordered a lot, which is the opposite of what we wanted to do. so the whole thing now is very simple, and everyone seems to love it. many of our competitors are still charging twice of what we do for shipping.” customers can upgrade to inside delivery for an additional $200, which is still a significant savings over dutchcrafters’ competitors.
