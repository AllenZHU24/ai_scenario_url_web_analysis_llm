reliability and consistency are the hallmarks of beckett grading services. a simple grading scale beckett grading services offers the first and only on time or it's free guarantee. if your order is not completed and on its way back to you by the deadline the whole order is free! this is at any service level! beckett grading services uses an easy-to-understand 1 to 10-point grading scale, with descending increments of one-half point (for example 10, 9.5, 9, 8.5, etc.). since you are provided with a detailed report on your card's key attributes, there are no confusing qualifiers to decipher. top-graded cards are identifiable at a glance thanks to beckett grading services' premium-colored labels. a gold label on the front of the unique cardholder signifies the highest graded cards (9.5-10). a silver label graces cards graded 8.5 to 9. cards in all other grades are identified with a distinctive and attractive white label. a detailed report card among the features unique to beckett grading services is a report card providing specific grade details and leaving no confusion as to why your card received its grade. cards will be graded on four key categories: centering, corners, edges and surface. beckett grading services is the first to use this unique system which assures accuracy in grading. grades from each of these key categories will be considered in determining your card's final overall grade. these key category grades will appear on the back of the beckett grading services cardholder. beckett grading services slab diagram front color coded label: gold for top two grades, silver for next two, white for remaining levels. easy identification of top grades and premium. numerical and letter grade appears on the front of the label, along with the card description. case allows edges of card to be viewed. sealed, archival inner sleeve to protect card from internal movement, yet will not detract from visibility. crystal clear outer shell, sonically sealed case is sturdy - prevents damage and creates a positive impression. back exclusive "report card" grade label details 4 areas of the card's total grade, identifying its strengths and weaknesses. 1/2 point grade increments provide a fine definition of the card's grade. serial number helps identify/record your card in the beckett archives. stackable design that keeps surfaces from being damaged. our guarantee beckett grading services will provide collectors with the finest, most thorough, consistent and accurate grading efforts available in the industry. disputed grades on
