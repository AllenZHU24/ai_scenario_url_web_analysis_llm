american standard at american standard, part of lixil americas, we believe that prioritizing sustainability is good business practice. sustainability already plays a key role in our product portfolio, our operations and our community engagement. in order to maximize our ability to enhance living spaces and truly create a positive impact on society, we are working to make sustainability central in everything we do – in our way to innovation, our way of working and our way to impact. • our way to innovation: we aim to create innovative water technology products and work to reduce our environmental impact as we bring these products to life. • our way of working: we operate in a way that upholds our commitment to sustainable, ethical and safe business practices. • our way to impact: we protect and promote the longevity, sanitation and health of our communities, environment and society. as the needs of our people, industry, customers, communities and environment change, we will continue to evolve our approach to create a more sustainable future for all. american standard water-conserving plumbing products that never sacrifice performance. view our water-saving toilets,
