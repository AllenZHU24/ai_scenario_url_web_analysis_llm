best navigation and site structure in the 13th annual webby awards piscataway, nj — the american standard brands (asb) flagship brand website was recently honored by the coveted webby awards in the category of best navigation and site structure. hailed as the "internet's highest honor" by the new york times, the webby is the leading international award honoring excellence on the internet. this year's competition received over 9,000 entries from all 50 states and over 60 countries worldwide. less than 15% of the submitted entries were recognized as official honorees. the webby award winners are chosen by the international academy of digital arts & sciences a global organization whose members include: david bowie, harvey weinstein, arianna huffington, matt groening, internet inventor vinton cerf, twitter co-founder biz stone, virgin group chairman richard branson, and r/ga chairman and ceo bob greenberg asb, a leading north american plumbing goods manufacturer, launched the latest version of americanstandard.com in december 2008. "our goal was to build a site that provides visitors with an easy-to-navigate experience. the new design provides clear paths to content that our consumer and trade customers need to make informed selections," said jeannette long, director of marketing communications for american standard. "the best navigation and site structure honor for the american standard site could not be more rewarding as a manifestation of all our core disciplines of strategic information architecture, creative designs and technical execution," said paul nealy, managing partner for american standard's digital agency of record, antidote x, inc. of minneapolis, mn. fellow honorees in the best navigation and site structure category this year included the bill and melinda gates foundation, lexus, volkswagen and others. download/view news release word format text format acrobat format image gallery american
