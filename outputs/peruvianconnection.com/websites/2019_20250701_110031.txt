sizing & fit you can use the size charts below to find the perfect fit. you can also rely on our customer service representatives to assist you. email us anytime or contact customer service at 1-800-221-8520 (mon.–fri., 8am–5pm cst). fit definitions (as used in the catalog descriptions): fitted:shaped to the body. semi-fitted:follows the shape of the body but does not fit tightly. classic:follows the shape of the body, with ease added for movement. relaxed:fit with extra ease for movement. falls free from the body. oversized:fit very full with a generous amount of ease added.
