follows the shape of the body but does not fit tightly. classic: follows the shape of the body, with ease added for movement. relaxed: fit with extra ease for movement. falls free from the body. oversized: fit very full with a generous amount of ease added. how to measuremeasure over undergarments. keep tape measure level and close, not tight. if possible, have someone else measure you—it’s more accurate.chest: measure at the fullest part of the chest, keeping the measuring tape up under the armpits and over the shoulder blades. waist: measure around the narrowest part of the natural waistline. hips: standing with heels together, measure around the fullest part of the body (approximately 8" below natural waistline.)neck: measure around the base of the neck.arm / sleeve length: bend elbow slightly. start at center back of neck and measure across the shoulder to the elbow - down to the wrist.outseam / skirt length: measure 1/2" below the waist (at the waist for items without
