is for spanx garments only. measurements are in inches. all measurements are designed to be used as a guideline. how to measure measure over undergarments. keep tape measure level and close, not tight. if possible, have someone else measure you—it's more accurate. chest: measure at the fullest part of the chest, keeping the measuring tape up under the armpits and over the shoulder blades. waist: measure around the narrowest part of the natural waistline. hips: standing with heels together, measure around the fullest part of the body (approximately 8" below natural waistline.) neck: measure around the base of the neck. arm / sleeve length: bend elbow slightly. start at center back of neck and measure across the shoulder to the elbow - down to the wrist. outseam / skirt length: measure 1/2" below the waist (at the waist for items without a waistband), and compare tape measure length to catalog item length. shop our catalog receive our catalog store locations gift cards like
