how to measure measure over undergarments. keep tape measure level and close, not tight. if possible, have someone else measure you—it's more accurate. chest: measure at the fullest part of the chest, keeping the measuring tape up under the armpits and over the shoulder blades. waist: measure around the narrowest part of the natural waistline. hips: standing with heels together, measure around the fullest part of the body (approximately 8" below natural waistline.) outseam / skirt length: measure 1/2" below the waist (at the waist for items without a waistband), and compare tape measure length
