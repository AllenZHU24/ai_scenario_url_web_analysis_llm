descriptions)fitted: shaped to the body.semi-fitted: follows the shape of the body but does not fit tightly.classic: follows the shape of the body, with ease for added movement.relaxed: fit with extra ease for movement. falls free from the body.oversized: fit very full with a generous amount of ease added.how to measure be sure to measure over undergarments. keep tape measure level and close, not tight. if possible, have someone else measure you—it’s more accurate. chest: measure at the fullest part of the chest, keeping the measuring tape up under the armpits and over the shoulder blades. waist: measure around the narrowest part of the natural waistline. hips: standing with heels together, measure around the fullest part of the body (approximately 8" below natural waistline.) outseam/skirt length: measure 1/2" below the waist (at the waist for items without a waistband), and compare tape measure length to catalog item length. womens size chart women’s garment
