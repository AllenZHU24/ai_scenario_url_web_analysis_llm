both xbox series x and s consoles deliver next-generation capabilities powered by xbox velocity architecture, such as faster loading, the ability to seamlessly switch between multiple games with quick resume, richer and more dynamic worlds and frame rates up to 120 fps. the primary difference between the two consoles is that the xbox series x displays games at native 4k and comes equipped with a
